How much does it cost to live like longtime British pop singer Engelbert Humperdinck? Far from a song, it\u2019ll run you $6.2 million to purchase his just-listed Los Angeles home.<\/p>\n

Born Arnold Dorsey, the 86-year-old \u201cRelease Me\u201d star took this 5,602-square-foot Mediterranean-style spread in Bel-Air to the market this past week, one of its listing representatives confirmed. <\/p>\n

It\u2019s being advertised as a remodel or a development opportunity, the latter of which would allow a new owner to build a new home on the existing lot, which offers wide views of the canyons and downtown LA\u2019s skyline.<\/p>\n

Dirt first reported news of the listing.<\/p>\n

As it stands, the residence \u2014 Humperdinck\u2019s stateside retreat \u2014 has four levels, five bedrooms and six bathrooms. It was built in the late 1990s. An LLC linked to the name Arnold Dorsey purchased the manse in 2005 for $2.99 million, according to Mansion Global.<\/p>\n

The reason for the move: Humperdinck and his family, who also spend their time at a home in Leicester, England, want to move to Beverly Hills.<\/p>\n

\u201cI sold him the property \u2026 [he\u2019s] been there since then,\u201d David Kessler, of Coldwell Banker Realty \u2014 who shares the listing with Allisun Sturges, of FrontGate Real Estate \u2014 told The Post. \u201cBut his children now have grown \u2026 after a good 17 years of living at this estate, he wants to find an alternative estate in Beverly Hills, which we\u2019re in the process of doing as we speak.\u201d<\/p>\n

What Humperdinck leaves behind, should a new owner choose to keep the property as is, is still quite nice. <\/p>\n

The gated home opens to a double-height foyer. Hardwood floors adorn the living room and bar\/den, which also have their own fireplaces. The kitchen, set under a stained glass ceiling, is big enough to fit a center island and a breakfast area. Upstairs are four bedrooms \u2014 one of which is the master, which includes a sitting room and a fireplace.<\/p>\n

The property additionally includes an apartment with its own entrance, a kitchen, a living area, a bedroom and a bathroom. Meanwhile, the lower levels include a movie theater, a wine cellar, a sauna and a gym. Outside, there\u2019s a heated pool and spa.<\/p>\n

The listing also notes the property is perched on top of the road where it stands.<\/p>\n
