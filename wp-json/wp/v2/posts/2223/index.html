\u201cThere was some heat,\u201d the 62-year-old actress told People of Depp, whom she dated for about a year after her engagement to Broderick.<\/p>\n

\u201cIt was literally like, \u2018Are you f \u2013 \u2013 king kidding me? Are you f \u2013 \u2013 king kidding me? I\u2019ve never seen a guy like this.\u2019 \u201d<\/p>\n

Grey opened up about past loves including Depp and Broderick \u2014 who she started dating in 1985 while filming \u201cFerris Bueller\u2019s Day Off\u201d \u2014 while promoting her upcoming memoir, \u201cOut of the Corner.\u201d<\/p>\n

The actress also got candid about the tragedy she faced with her \u201cFerris Bueller\u201d co-star when the two got into a car accident in Ireland in 1987 that left two dead.<\/p>\n

\u201cThere was some very, very heavy stuff that went down that changed my life forever and there was no one to blame,\u201d Grey told People. \u201cIt was a pure and simple accident that was tragic. And it had very serious traumatic lasting effects on, I\u2019m sure, Matthew and the family of the other women and me.\u201d<\/p>\n

Grey and Broderick\u2019s relationship didn\u2019t survive the aftermath of the tragedy as news of the accident came on the heels of the \u201cDirty Dancing\u201d premiere. Questions and commentary about it followed her from Howard Stern to a \u201cToday\u201d show segment that was supposed to be about the movie, according to the New York Times.<\/p>\n

But while weathering the storm, Grey moved on to Depp, who she was set up with on a blind date by her agent.<\/p>\n

She admitted that her relationship with Depp, now 58, made up for the hardship she faced.<\/p>\n

\u201cIf a mirage could have been a person in the summer of 1988, that mirage would have been Johnny Depp,\u201d she wrote in her memoir.<\/p>\n

\u201cEnergetically, what it was like being with him, it was like, \u2018Oh, I\u2019m being totally, totally compensated for the s \u2013 \u2013 t I just went through,\u2019 \u201d Grey told People.<\/p>\n

The actress wrote in her memoir that she accepted Depp\u2019s proposal when he asked her to marry him just two weeks into dating.<\/p>\n

Their engagement lasted nine months, but their relationship didn\u2019t survive Depp\u2019s fast-rising career.<\/p>\n
