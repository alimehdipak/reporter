STATELINE, Nev. — Tony Romo won the American Century Championship for the third time, beating Mark Mulder and Joe Pavelski with a 5-foot birdie putt on the second hole of a playoff Sunday.<\/p>\n

Romo also birdied the par-5 18th in regulation to earn three points and match Mulder and Pavelski with 62 points at Edgewood Tahoe in the modified Stableford scoring system event.<\/p>\n

Romo, the former Dallas Cowboys quarterback and current NFL television analyst, also won at Edgewood Tahoe in 2018 and 2019.<\/p>\n

“I had to keep patient,” Romo said. “Coming from behind is a great feeling. All the wins here are special, but this one was different because my boys are 10 and 8 years old and they were into it.”<\/p>\n

Mulder won three straight times from 2015 to 2017.<\/p>\n
