Justin Bieber is back, \u201cBaby.\u201d <\/p>\n

The 28-year-old heartthrob will resume his \u201cJustice\u201d world tour after he postponed a month\u2019s worth of shows to recover from facial paralysis, both AEG and the verified Twitter account Bieber Fever announced Tuesday. <\/p>\n

Starting with the Lucca Festival in Italy on July 31, the Biebs will complete a run of European festivals, then play shows \u201cAll Around The World\u201d in South America, South Africa, the Middle East, Asia, Australia and New Zealand. He\u2019ll then return to Europe in 2023. <\/p>\n

His postponed US concerts have yet to be rescheduled. <\/p>\n

Life was anything but \u201cPeaches\u201d for Bieber when he was forced to cancel several North America stops \u2014 including in New York City \u2014 amid his battle with Ramsay Hunt syndrome, a rare neurological disorder that is reflected in facial palsy or paralysis, often accompanied by a rash affecting the ear or mouth.<\/p>\n
