Air conditioners are used in almost every home and office globally, but there are some interesting facts about air conditioning that you probably didn\u2019t know.<\/span><\/p>\n

Movie theaters used to be the coolest\u2014literally<\/b>.<\/span><\/h2>\n

Movie theater owners invested in AC systems to fill seats during the year’s hottest days. They did this because most people living at that time didn\u2019t own homes with AC which made movie theaters a perfect escape from heat and sweat.<\/span><\/p>\n

The first air conditioner wasn\u2019t designed for comfort<\/b>.<\/span><\/h2>\n

In 1902, Willis Carrier designed the first air conditioner on a large scale. His manufacturing company was in the publishing business and often had quality issues during the summer. Carrier wanted to make his life easier so he created what would be a widely known air conditioning system!<\/span><\/p>\n

Ice was the original air conditioner<\/b><\/h2>\n

People would use blocks of ice to keep their homes cool before ACs were invented. The first air conditioners were able to measure their output in terms of how many ice cubes would have been required to achieve the same function.<\/span><\/p>\n

Without AC, warmer states would be pretty empty<\/b>.<\/span><\/h2>\n

What if you live in a hot state like Texas or Florida? Without an AC, it would be unbearable. This is why when people found out about ACs, the populations of these states began to grow.<\/span><\/p>\n

You can thank your air conditioner for modern medicine<\/b>.<\/span><\/h2>\n

A lot of our medicines, that fight the common cold to cancer, were developed in laboratories with temperature-sensitive tools and equipment.<\/span><\/p>\n

School\u2019s out for the summer\u2014thanks to the heat<\/b>.<\/span><\/h2>\n

When AC units were not invented, it would be very hot during the summer months and people would go on “summer vacation.” Fortunately, the idea of summer vacations stuck and now kids can take advantage of ACs in the Summertime.<\/span><\/p>\n

Allergies and air conditioners go together like peanut butter and jelly<\/b><\/h2>\n
