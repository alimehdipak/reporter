<\/p>\n

Amazon pays summer interns more than three times as much as some warehouse and delivery workers, according to data published Tuesday.\u00a0<\/p>\n

The median corporate intern at Amazon \u2014 which offers internships throughout the year \u2014 rakes in $8,000 a month, according to a new report from corporate review site Glassdoor. That\u2019s the equivalent of $96,000 per year or $46.15 per hour based on a 40-hour work week.\u00a0<\/p>\n

By comparison, Amazon\u2019s warehouse and delivery workers make a minimum of $15 an hour and the company says its median starting wage for warehouse workers stands at $18 an hour. <\/p>\n

The data on intern pay comes on the heels of Amazon warehouse workers in New York becoming the company\u2019s first-ever US employees to unionize.\u00a0<\/p>\n

The Amazon Labor Union, which is representing thousands of workers at the JFK8 warehouse in Staten Island, is pushing for Amazon to increase its company-wide minimum wage to $30.\u00a0<\/p>\n

While that\u2019s double Amazon\u2019s current minimum pay, it\u2019s still just 65% of the company\u2019s reported median intern pay. <\/p>\n

Amazon\u2019s internship program is the fifth-highest-paying in the US, according to Glassdoor\u2019s report.\u00a0Bloomberg via Getty Images A union representing Amazon warehouse workers in New York wants the company to boost minimum pay to $30 per hour. AFP via Getty Images <\/p>\n

Amazon did not immediately respond to a request for comment. <\/p>\n

Amazon\u2019s internship program is the fifth-highest-paying in the US, according to Glassdoor\u2019s report.\u00a0<\/p>\n

Online gaming company Roblox takes the number-one spot with median monthly intern pay of $9,667 \u2014 the equivalent of $116,000 per year \u2014 followed by Uber and Capital One at $8,333 each and Salesforce at $8,167.\u00a0<\/p>\n

Interns at gaming company Roblox are the highest-paid in the US, making the equivalent of $116,000 per year. San Francisco Chronicle via Gett <\/p>\n

Amazon, Meta and Nvidia all have median monthly intern salaries of $8,000, followed by LinkedIn, Expedia and HubSpot at $7,500 each.\u00a0<\/p>\n

Among other big tech firms, Microsoft pays $7,366 per month, followed by Apple at $7,000 and Google at a paltry $6,454, according to the report.<\/p>\n

Investment bank Goldman Sachs, meanwhile, doesn\u2019t even rank in the top 25 highest-paying internships. The company is beaten by JPMorgan, Citi, Deutsche Bank and several other competitors, according to Glassdoor. <\/p>\n

Glassdoor said it based the rankings on more than 88,000 self-submitted pay reports from current and former interns from February 2021 to February 2022.\u00a0<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
