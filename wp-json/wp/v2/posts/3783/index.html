Edgar Berlanga defeated Alexis Angulo on Saturday by unanimous decision to stay unbeaten in the main event of a Top Rank card at the Hulu Theater inside Madison Square Garden in New York, but the fight will be remembered for a different reason.<\/p>\n

In Round 7, Berlanga tried to bite Angulo on the left shoulder and then his left ear. The referee warned Angulo for punching behind the head, but didn’t reprimand Berlanga.<\/p>\n

“He was throwing elbows. I was about to do a Mike Tyson on him,” Berlanga said when asked about the incident in the postfight interview. “He kept throwing his elbows, and I didn’t want to get cut.”<\/p>\n

The incident was similar to Mike Tyson biting Evander Holyfield in their rematch in 1997, and the boxing world reacted to it.<\/p>\n
