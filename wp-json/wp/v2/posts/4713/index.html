BEREA, Ohio — Cleveland Browns wide receiver and kick returner Jakeem Grant Sr. suffered what the team fears is a torn Achilles, a league source told ESPN.<\/p>\n

Grant had to be carted off the field during Tuesday’s practice after suffering the injury in the drill. A source said the team is awaiting imaging to confirm the Achilles tear.<\/p>\n

The Browns signed Grant to a three-year deal this offseason to be their kick and punt returner; Grant, however, was also having a standout training camp as a receiver out of the slot, as well.<\/p>\n
