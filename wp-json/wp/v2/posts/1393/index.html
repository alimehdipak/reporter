Venus Williams had a nip slip in the midst of Sunday\u2019s chaotic 2022 Oscars show. <\/p>\n

ABC cameras caught the fashion mishap, and fans on Twitter shared screen grabs that showed Williams holding her right arm over her chest. <\/p>\n
