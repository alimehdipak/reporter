The infamous Australian house where Johnny Depp and Amber Heard\u2019s two Yorkshire terriers, Pistol and Boo, were held hostage after they were illegally smuggled into the country is now making news for other reasons.<\/p>\n

On Monday, Depp\u2019s house manager, Ben King, testified about finding the actor\u2019s severed finger inside the home after Heard allegedly threw a vodka bottle at him.<\/p>\n

The testimony comes as Depp, 58, is suing Heard, 36, for $50 million, claiming she defamed him in a 2018 Washington Post opinion piece in which she claimed she was a domestic and sexual violence survivor.<\/p>\n

The five-week trial began on April 11, with Heard expected to testify in the coming days.<\/p>\n

Located in the oceanside city of Gold Coast in the Australian state of Queensland, Depp and Heard rented the home from MotoGP world champion Mick Doohan in 2015 while the actor filmed the fifth installment of the \u201cPirates of the Caribbean\u201d franchise.<\/p>\n

King claimed he arrived at the residence on the afternoon of March 8, 2015, where he could hear \u201chysterical sobbing\u201d from Heard. That was when he witnessed Depp\u2019s personal doctor, David Kipper, \u201crummaging through a bin\u201d in the kitchen to look for Depp\u2019s fingertip.<\/p>\n

King said he eventually discovered the fingertip \u201cdirectly below the bar,\u201d where he noticed a paper towel with \u201clots of blood around it\u201d on the floor.<\/p>\n

\u201c[They] were keen to get it to the hospital quickly to see if it could be reattached,\u201d King testified.<\/p>\n

The house manager then detailed that the pair had left the house in complete disarray with up to $50,000 in significant damage following their explosive fight.<\/p>\n

A \u201cbig chunk had been taken out of the marble staircase,\u201d there was a \u201ccollapsed\u201d ping-pong table, \u201cremnants of a coffee cup was embedded\u201d in a wall-mounted TV and the walls around the room had also been damaged, King added.<\/p>\n

\u201cNothing was really intact,\u201d King added, claiming he also saw a broken vodka bottle.<\/p>\n
