Don\u2019t be silly, get smart. <\/p>\n

Smartwatches are a brilliant and simple way to start achieving your fitness goals and motivate you to get moving. <\/p>\n

These nifty watches have become an everyday accessory, be it the wildly popular Apple Watch or the original FitBit. <\/p>\n

Both are enabled with touchscreens to help keep you on track throughout the day. Best smartwatches for adults<\/h2>\n

1. Apple Watch Series 6 (GPS + Cellular), $529<\/h3>\n

Best Buy <\/p>\n

The Apple Watch is one of the most sought-after smartwatches in the game, and here\u2019s why. <\/p>\n

The newest Series 6 model combines all the activity tracking of it\u2019s elder models and the high speed cellular capabilities of the latest iPhone, making it a handsfree experience to take calls, send messages and also track your heart rate, sleep, blood oxygen levels and more. 2. Apple Watch SE (GPS) 44mm, $213<\/h3>\n

Best Buy <\/p>\n

For a less expensive Apple Watch with some of the highest quality specs, the SE model is for you. <\/p>\n

This watch is about $200 less than the Series 6, but still has the GPS and fitness tracking you need from a smartwatch. The watch is water and sweat-proof, so never fear when jumping into the pool to get in some cardio. The screen is also the same size as the Series 6, with an easy-to-read display showing you your cycling directions, sleep tracking, music and podcasts and more. <\/p>\n

Amazon <\/p>\n

This isn\u2019t your mother\u2019s Fitbit. <\/p>\n

Taking a huge step forward from the original model, this Fitbit is as high-tech as it gets, complete with Bluetooth to easily sync with Alexa and other voice assistants. This new model comes with the most advances 24\/7 heart tracking with PurePulse 2.0, as well as sleep tracking, blood oxygen measuring and more. Sold on Amazon, the watch comes in the above black color as well as in navy, light pink and light purple. <\/p>\n

4. Fitbit Luxe, $100, original price: $130<\/h3>\n

Best Buy <\/p>\n

For a slimmer fit and a stylish design, check out the Fitbit Luxe. <\/p>\n

Sold at Best Buy, the smaller model looks like the original skinny smartwatch, but still has an impressive AMOLED color display screen and easy-to-use display. Even though the watch itself is small, the little battery can go up to five whole days without a charge, making this a great choice for those who want to track sleep, activity and exercise all day and all night long. The slim smartwatch comes in three colors, including the pictured white, an orchid pink and a solid black model. <\/p>\n

5. Kenneth Cole Wellness Watch, $95<\/h3>\n

Kenneth Cole <\/p>\n

Who said Kenneth Cole was only about fashion. <\/p>\n

Not that this smartwatch isn\u2019t stunning, as you can pair this with a work outfit or when working out for 24\/7 health monitoring. The watch comes in under the $100 mark, making it an affordable must-have for health tracking, recording sleep patterns, steps, heart rate and more features that compete with the most high-tech of watches. The watch and strap come in a versatile black, with customizable watch faces depending on your activity. <\/p>\n

6. Wyze Activity Fitness Tracker Watch, $33<\/h3>\n

Amazon <\/p>\n

Possibly one of the most affordable smartwatches, the Wyze is one to watch. <\/p>\n

The smartwatch is sold on Amazon and can easily track your fitness levels when paired with any smartphone, iOS or Android. The watch is waterproof as well as Bluetooth enabled, syncing up with Alexa to help you out hands-free. The best part is that the watch can track your heart rate for up to 10 whole days on one charge, giving you a great overview of your health without needing to take it off for days at a time. <\/p>\n

7. Withings Steel HR Smartwatch, $180<\/h3>\n

Withings <\/p>\n

Embrace the classic look of a streamlined watch while also tracking your health with Withings. <\/p>\n

The Steel HR model is one of the best in their lineup, giving you an impressive 25-day battery life and continuous tracking of your heart rate, sleep and activity level. The watch pairs with your smartphone to give you in-depth reporting of all of the above, while looking like a sleek fashion watch on the outside. The band comes in an array of colors to choose from as well as the option to choose the face color, case and screen size. <\/p>\n

8. Samsung Electronics Galaxy Watch 4 40mm, $210, original price: $250<\/h3>\n

Amazon <\/p>\n

Android lovers, rejoice. The Samsung watch is a highly requested one, now ranked as an \u201cAmazon Choice\u201d product online. <\/p>\n
