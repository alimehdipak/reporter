Goop\u2019s former second-in-command is throwing mud at clean queen Gwyneth Paltrow\u2019s allegedly \u201ctoxic\u201d workplace. <\/p>\n

Fans know Elise Loehnen as cohost of the Goop podcast and as someone who also made regular appearances on their Netflix series \u201cThe Goop Lab.\u201d But in 2020, Loehnen \u2014 then the brand\u2019s chief content officer \u2014 stepped down after working closely with Paltrow for seven years. <\/p>\n

At the time, Loehnen cited a forthcoming book for Dial Press as a reason for her departure. But her recent comments on Instagram suggest otherwise.<\/p>\n

\u201cSo when I left my last job at Goop almost two years ago, I decided to foreswear all cleansing,\u201d she began in a Reel on Tuesday.<\/p>\n

\u201cTo me, it had become synonymous with dieting and restriction and I felt like I was not in a healthy relationship with my body, where I was always trying to punish it, bring it under control. And I\u2019ve been eating like a teenager for two years and enjoying it, to be honest,\u201d Loehnen explained in the video with more than 9,000 views.<\/p>\n

Elise Loehnen, Goop\u2019s former chief content officer, is speaking out about the \u201cdistorted\u201d relationship she said she developed with her body during her time at the clean lifestyle company.Courtesy of @eliseloehnen <\/p>\n

Loehnen also blamed problematic wellness trends for her \u201cdistorted\u201d body image.<\/p>\n

In the caption, the former ghostwriter of 11 book titles rejoiced in the \u201cfull rebellion\u201d she launched after quitting Goop, calling the breakup \u201chealthy in terms of letting go of ideas of what my body should look like as a 42-year-old who has had two kids.\u201d (Paltrow, for comparison, is 49 with two teenagers.)<\/p>\n

\u201cWellness culture can be toxic,\u201d Loehnen added before a segue into a plug for Kroma Wellness and their liquid \u201cfive-day cleanse\u201d of broths, smoothies and lattes.<\/p>\n

Elise Loehnen and Gwyneth Paltrow attend a \u201cThe Goop Lab\u201d screening in Hollywood in 2020.Getty Images The former Goop podcast cohost worked side-by-side with the celebrity wellness guru for seven years.WireImage <\/p>\n

But this time will be different, she insisted, by skipping weigh-ins and giving herself room to stretch the parameters of the diet.<\/p>\n

\u201cI\u2019m just trying to get to a place where I can again be in conversation with my body,\u201d she continued, implying that \u201cthose conversations had become distorted\u201d during her time at Goop.<\/p>\n

\u201cI refuse to punish myself with food, or hold myself under the weight my body seems to want to be anymore,\u201d she continued in the video caption. \u201cI don\u2019t have the energy or the interest, thankfully.\u201d<\/p>\n

Of course, Goop\u2019s reputation has come into question repeatedly over the years by critics who question the legitimacy of their health guidance, as well as by those inside with dirt on the clean lifestyle company.<\/p>\n

Media reports \u2014 plus many unfavorable employee reviews online \u2014 have revealed a mass exodus of employees occurring since September 2019, with many echoing the \u201ctoxic\u201d workplace sentiment, plagued by low wages, \u201cfear-based management\u201d and a \u201c\u200c\u2019mean girl\u2019 vibe,\u201d according to the Daily Mail. The celebrity wellness empire that began as a newsletter in 2008 lost reportedly around half the staff \u2014 about 140, reported Business Insider \u2014 due to layoffs and resignations, including that of corner suite officers.<\/p>\n

The former Goop exec previously stated that she left the brand to focus on a new book deal.Getty Images for goop <\/p>\n

This is the first time Loehnen \u2014 who coined one of the celebrity era\u2019s biggest buzzwords, \u201cconscious uncoupling\u201d \u2014 has opened up about her personal hurdles at Goop. Paltrow had considered her a \u201ccounterpart\u201d in the business, according to her statement on the COO\u2019s leave. <\/p>\n

\u201cElise is like a sister to me, and I plan to watch proudly as she enters this monumental stage in her professional life,\u201d the Oscar-winning actress concluded.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
