Mother\u2019s Day has arrived \u2013 and whether you\u2019re spending quality time with your own family or just relaxing at home, there are a slew of specials, TV episodes, and streaming movies that are a perfect way to celebrate. <\/p>\n

Here\u2019s your essential guide to what to watch on the Big Day.\u00a0<\/p>\n

\u201cMrs. Doubtfire\u201d\u00a0<\/h2>\n

In this 1993 modern classic, Daniel (Robin Williams) a loving dad and scatterbrained husband, gets divorced from his wife, Miranda (Sally Field). In order to spend more time with his children, he dons an elaborate disguise as older British nanny \u201cMrs. Doutfire\u201d and takes his colorful new persona into their household, where chaos ensues.\u00a0<\/p>\n

Where to watch it: 10 a.m. on Freeform\u00a0<\/em><\/p>\n

Robin Williams in \u201cMrs. Doubtfire.\u201d \u00a920thCentFox\/Courtesy Everett C <\/p>\n

\u00a0\u201cTo Mom (And Dad), With Love\u201d<\/h2>\n

There\u2019s a new animated Peanuts special for Mother\u2019s day, following Charlie Brown, Snoopy, and the gang. While the gang celebrates, Peppermint Patty feels left out, since she doesn\u2019t have a mom, before her friends help her realize that family comes in different forms. Snoopy and his trusty feathered friend Woodstock also go on an adventure in search of Woodstock\u2019s mom.\u00a0<\/p>\n

Where to watch it: AppleTV+\u00a0<\/em><\/p>\n

Snoopy and Woodstock go on an adventure in a new Peanuts special for Mother\u2019s Day. Apple TV+. <\/p>\n

\u201cWelcome Home, Nikki Glaser?\u201d\u00a0<\/h2>\n

In this new reality series, comedian Nikki Glaser leaves her LA life behind to return to her hometown of St. Louis.\u00a0In Sunday\u2019s episode \u201cBaby Fever,\u201d Nikki plans a baby shower for her pregnant sister and also starts thinking about what her own idea of motherhood might look like.\u00a0<\/p>\n

Where to watch it: 10 p.m. on E!\u00a0<\/em><\/p>\n

Nikki Glaser Mary Ellen Matthews\/E! Entertain <\/p>\n

\u201cFreaky Friday\u201d<\/h2>\n

This 2003 remake of the 1976 movie stars Jamie Lee Curtis and Lindsay Lohan as a mother and daughter who swap bodies after sharing a mysterious fortune cookie. Curtis is a standout, elevating the movie with her comedic performance of a surly rock\u2019n\u2019roll teenager who\u2019s disgruntled to find herself in Curtis\u2019s body.\u00a0<\/p>\n

Where to watch it: Disney+\u00a0<\/em><\/p>\n

Jamie Lee Curtis and Lindsay Lohan in \u201cFreaky Friday.\u201d \u00a9Walt Disney Co.\/Courtesy Evere <\/p>\n

\u201cThe Joy Luck Club\u201d<\/h2>\n

This groundbreaking 1993 movie based on the novel by Amy Tan follows four Chinese women who are immigrants in San Francisco as they meet and play mahjong and grapple with their relationships, generational divides, and culture clashes with their daughters.\u00a0<\/p>\n

Where to watch it: Peacock\u00a0<\/em><\/p>\n

Tsai Chin in \u201cThe Joy Luck Club.\u201d \u00a9Buena Vista Pictures\/Courtesy <\/p>\n

\u201cBecause I Said So\u201d\u00a0<\/h2>\n

This 2007 film follows overbearing mom Daphne (Diane Keaton) as she tries to meddle in her daughter Milly\u2019s (Mandy Moore) love life and wreaks havoc.\u00a0<\/p>\n

Where to watch it: Starz (on demand)<\/em><\/p>\n

Diane Keaton and Mandy Moore in \u201cBecause I said So.\u201d \u00a9Universal\/Courtesy Everett Col <\/p>\n

\u201cKnocked Up\u201d<\/h2>\n

Technically this movie is about preparing <\/em>to become a mom \u2013 and a dad \u2013 but it still fits the bill. After ambitious reporter Alison (Katherine Heigl) has a one-night stand with slacker Ben (Seth Rogen) that results in an unplanned pregnancy, the two must figure out their differences so that they\u2019ll be able to co-parent.\u00a0<\/p>\n

Where to watch it: 12 p.m. on TBS\u00a0<\/em><\/p>\n

\u201cAlways Mother\u2019s Day Marathon\u201d<\/h2>\n

This day-long marathon features curated episodes with themes involving becoming a mother, and moms and grandmothers. Spotlighted shows include \u201cBurns and Allen\u201d, \u201cThe Partridge Family\u201d, \u201cOne Day at a Time\u201d, \u201cThe Jeffersons\u201d and \u201cAlice.\u201d<\/p>\n

Where to Watch it: starting at 8 a.m. on Antenna TV\u00a0<\/em><\/p>\n

\u201cDumplin\u2019\u201d<\/h2>\n

Based on the 2015 novel of the same name, this movie follows Willowdean Dickonson (Danielle Macdonald) who has a difficult relationship with her former beauty queen mother, Rosie (Jennifer Aniston), which comes to a head when Willowdean enters a local pageant.<\/p>\n
