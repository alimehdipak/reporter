Star attorney Camille Vasquez will be reunited in court with Johnny Depp next month when she defends the actor in his latest legal drama.<\/p>\n

Vasquez, 37, attained celebrity status during Depp\u2019s headline-grabbing defamation case against ex-wife Amber Heard \u2014 and is now gearing up to represent him again as he faces claims he assaulted a worker on a film set.<\/p>\n

The attorney will be part of Depp\u2019s legal team when he heads to Los Angeles Superior Court next month to answer the lawsuit brought by Gregg \u201cRocky\u201d Brooks, the location manager on Depp\u2019s 2018 movie \u201cCity of Lies.\u201d<\/p>\n

Brooks claimed in legal papers that Depp, now 59, twice socked him in the ribs and then allegedly yelled, \u201cI\u2019ll give you $100,000 to punch me right now!\u201d<\/p>\n

Depp may testify in the LA trial \u2014 which is slated to begin July 25 \u2014 weeks after taking the stand in Fairfax, Virginia, in his defamation case. <\/p>\n

Vasquez ascended from associate to partner at law firm Brown Rudnick during the six-week Depp-Heard trial, going viral during her cross-examination of Heard. <\/p>\n

After the verdict, Vasquez was reportedly the focus of a bidding war between firms for her services.<\/p>\n

\u201cI\u2019m proud of the uniquely talented team I\u2019ve had the privilege to lead, which exemplified teamwork and collaboration, and I look forward to continuing to represent Brown Rudnick\u2019s culture of excellence,\u201d she said in a statement after her promotion.<\/p>\n
