Cloud-based systems like RingCentral and Vonage add a long list of advanced calling features, video conferencing capabilities, messaging options and integrations with popular business applications \u2013 everything a modern business needs to communicate effectively.<\/p>\n

    RingCentral: Who do we recommend it for?<\/h2>\n

    RingCentral is a cloud-based business phone system with an excellent list of features and software integrations. You can call, text, fax or chat from the same application across multiple devices. Its flagship product, RingCentral MVP, ranges in price from $19.99 to $49.99 per user per month with an annual plan. The platform offers 24-hour meeting durations, automatic call recording, single sign-on, business phone numbers in 100 countries, and 24\/7 support.<\/p>\n

      Vonage: Who do we recommend it for?<\/h2>\n

      Vonage Business Communications is a cloud-based phone system with a flexible list of features, integrations and add-ons. Prices for the smallest businesses range from $14.99 to $39.99 per line per month, depending on the plan and number of licenses you purchase.\u00a0<\/p>\n

      Vonage offers more than 50 voice and unified communications features with desktop and mobile apps to keep teams connected across devices. Its customer and employee experience features shine with tools like Call Announce, which tells your agents the reason for each call before they answer it. Advanced features include the AI Virtual Assist, which takes a conversational approach to voice-enabled customer self-service.<\/p>\n

                            What are some alternatives to Vonage and RingCentral?<\/h3>\n

                            Other popular cloud-based business phone systems include Nextiva, Ooma, Dialpad and 8×8. Each one varies slightly in features and pricing, but they all allow employees to communicate effectively with team members and customers from almost any internet-connected device or location.\u00a0<\/p>\n

Because it’s a cloud-based phone system, its call quality can be affected by a poor internet connection. If your internet connection is strong, however, expect high call quality and clarity with Vonage Business Communications.\u00a0<\/p>\n
