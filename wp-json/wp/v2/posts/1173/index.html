\u201cApparently my Facebook account has been disabled because it didn\u2019t follow community standards,\u201d one peeved Facebook user wrote on Twitter on Friday morning. \u201cNo appeal, no further explanation. Account is just\u2026 gone.\u201d\u00a0<\/p>\n

\u201cDid something occur last night because a bunch of us that I\u2019m friends with all of a sudden had our Facebook accounts disabled for no apparent reason and with no warning,\u201d a third user said.\u00a0<\/p>\n

Several hours after The Post flagged the issue, Meta said the problem had been fixed as of Friday afternoon.\u00a0<\/p>\n

\u201cEarlier today, a technical issue caused a small number of people to have trouble accessing Facebook,\u201d a company spokesperson told The Post. \u201cWe resolved the issue as quickly as possible for everyone who was impacted, and we apologize for any inconvenience.\u201d<\/p>\n
