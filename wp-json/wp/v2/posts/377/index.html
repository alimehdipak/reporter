Netflix\u2019s US subscribers can now stream \u201cServant of the People,\u201d the satirical comedy series that helped to launch Ukrainian President\u00a0Volodymyr Zelensky\u2019s political career \u2013 and eventual role in leading the resistance against an escalating Russian invasion of Ukraine.<\/p>\n

In the series, Zelensky plays a high school history teacher who gains national fame after a video of his rant against government corruption goes viral \u2014 eventually resulting in the teacher being elected president of Ukraine.<\/p>\n

\u201cYou asked and it\u2019s back!\u201d Netflix tweeted in a post confirming the show\u2019s return.<\/p>\n

The satirical comedy series debuted in 2015 and ran for three seasons. <\/p>\n

Production ended after Zelensky, once a popular comedian and actor, decided to actually run for political office \u2013 easily winning the presidency in 2019 as a member of the \u201cServant of the People\u201d political party.<\/p>\n

Netflix previously included \u201cServant of the People\u201d in its US content library from 2017 until 2021.<\/p>\n

Eccho Rights, the Swedish firm licensing the series, described \u201cServant of the People\u201d in a recent statement as \u201can important document of where Zelensky comes from.\u201d<\/p>\n
