    Are you guilty of brushing your teeth for less than two minutes? If so, you’re not alone. Most people only brush their teeth for about 45-60 seconds. In order to reduce your risk of cavities and gum disease, it’s important to brush your teeth for at least two minutes each time.       We all know that flossing is important, but many of us don’t make it a daily habit. So if you’ve been putting off flossing, start making it a part of your daily routine today. You’ll be glad you did!<\/span><\/p>\n
