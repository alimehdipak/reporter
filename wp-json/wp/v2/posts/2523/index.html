What are top 10 Thanksgiving foods?<\/h3>\n

Thanksgiving is a time for family, friends and great food! There are many different types of foods that are popular during this time of year. Each has its own history and unique flavor. Here are the top 10 Thanksgiving foods you should definitely try to add to your menu this year:<\/p>\n

1. Turkey \u2013<\/h3>\n

This is probably the most well known dish associated with Thanksgiving meals. The turkey is typically roasted or deep-fried and then served with stuffing, gravy and cranberry sauce.<\/p>\n

2. Mashed potatoes \u2013<\/h3>\n

A staple on any Thanksgiving table, mashed potatoes can be made in countless ways to suit everyone\u2019s tastes. They are usually topped with butter, cheese or sour cream depending on how rich you like them.<\/p>\n

3. Sweet potatoes \u2013<\/h3>\n

Another Thanksgiving favorite, sweet potatoes are often baked or mashed and served with marshmallows or brown sugar on top.<\/p>\n

4. Stuffing \u2013<\/h3>\n

This dish is usually made from a mix of bread crumbs, herbs, vegetables and spices. It can be cooked inside the turkey or separately in a casserole dish.<\/p>\n

5. Cranberry sauce \u2013 This tart and tangy sauce is typically made from fresh cranberries, sugar and water. It can be served as is or used as a topping for other dishes like turkey or stuffing.<\/p>\n

6. Green beans \u2013<\/h3>\n

A classic side dish, green beans are often boiled or sauteed and then served with various seasonings like salt, pepper and butter.<\/p>\n

7. Corn \u2013<\/h3>\n

Another popular side dish, corn can be served boiled, grilled or roasted. It is often seasoned with salt, pepper and butter as well.<\/p>\n

8. Pumpkin pie \u2013<\/h3>\n

This dessert is typically made with a pumpkin puree, eggs, sweetened condensed milk and spices like cinnamon, nutmeg and ginger. The pie filling is then poured into a pre-baked pie crust and baked until set.<\/p>\n

9. Apple pie \u2013<\/h3>\n

This classic American dessert is usually made with a mix of apples, sugar, flour, butter and spices like cinnamon and nutmeg. The filling is then placed into a pre-baked pie crust and baked until the apples are tender and the crust is golden brown.<\/p>\n

10. Pecan pie \u2013<\/h3>\n

This sweet dessert is made with pecan nuts, eggs and sugar mixed with a pre-baked pie crust. The filling is then baked until set and served warm or cold.<\/p>\n

So there you have it \u2013 the top 10 Thanksgiving foods that are sure to delight your family and friends this year! Which ones will you be adding to your menu? Experiment with different recipes, ingredients and cooking methods to create a meal that everyone will love! Have fun in the kitchen this season!\u200b<\/p>\n

Whether you\u2019re hosting Thanksgiving dinner this year or simply attending as a guest, be sure to try some of these delicious foods! They are sure to become new family favorites that you\u2019ll enjoy for many years to come. Have fun cooking and enjoy your meal!\u200b<\/p>\n
