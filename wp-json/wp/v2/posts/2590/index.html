Jennifer Grey has dropped yet another bombshell about her fiery relationship with ex-fianc\u00e9 Johnny Depp in her new memoir \u201cOut of the Corner,\u201d out Tuesday.<\/p>\n

The enduring \u201cDirty Dancing\u201d legend is revealing how things took a dark turn after her agent set her up on a blind date with the hot \u201cCry-Baby\u201d actor in 1989, when he was 26 and she was 29.<\/p>\n

The pair became engaged just two weeks after meeting that year, she claimed.<\/p>\n

Of their first date, Grey, 62, penned: \u201cWe ate, talked, drank Jack Daniel\u2019s, laughed our asses off, took cigarette breaks midcourse. He was so ridiculously beautiful. And surprisingly open, funny, quirky and sweet.\u201d<\/p>\n

The lovebirds even got a Pekingese-poodle puppy named Lulu. \u201cShe was our practice baby and someone to keep me company when Johnny was out of town,\u201d Grey wrote, according to an excerpt obtained by the Independent.<\/p>\n

Depp, 58, is embroiled in a high-profile defamation suit against 36-year-old ex-wife Amber Heard \u2014 but Grey claims that isn\u2019t his first brush with relationship drama.<\/p>\n

The \u201cFerris Bueller\u2019s Day Off\u201d scene-stealer described how Depp\u2019s behavior became erratic during their time together: \u201cJohnny was commuting every week back and forth from Vancouver, but had begun more and more regularly to be getting into trouble: fights in bars, skirmishes with cops.\u201d<\/p>\n

\u201cHe\u2019d started missing his flights home to LA having overslept or, when he did come home, he\u2019d be crazy jealous and paranoid about what I\u2019d been up to while he was gone,\u201d the mother of one continued. \u201cI attributed his ill temper and unhappiness to him feeling miserable and powerless to get off [the TV series]\u00a0\u201921 Jump Street.\u2019 \u201d<\/p>\n

Reps for Depp have not responded to requests for comment.<\/p>\n

Meanwhile, Grey previously revealed ahead of her tell-all book\u2019s release that \u201cthere was some heat\u201d in her relationship with Depp.<\/p>\n

\u201cIt was a f\u2013king bonfire,\u201d Grey said. \u201cIt was literally like, \u2018Are you f\u2013king kidding me? Are you f\u2013king kidding me? I\u2019ve never seen a guy like this.\u2019 \u201d <\/p>\n
