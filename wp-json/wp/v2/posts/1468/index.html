Kid Rock kicked off his 2022 national tour with a video introduction from pal and former president Donald Trump.<\/p>\n

Before the \u201cAll Summer Long\u201d singer took to the stage at the Ford Center in Evansville, Indiana Wednesday night, Rock played a video message from the 45th President.<\/p>\n

\u201cHello, everyone. I love you all. I know you\u2019re having a great time at the Kid Rock concert tonight,\u201d Trump says to the cheering crowd in the short clip above [warning: it features some strong language]. \u201cQuite frankly, he\u2019s amazing. All of you in attendance are truly the backbone of our great country. Hard-working, God-fearing rock-and-roll patriots.\u201d<\/p>\n

\u201cBob is truly one of the greatest entertainers of our time,\u201d he said, referring to the rocker by his real name, Bob Ritchie.\u00a0\u201cNot the best golfer by any means, his golf game could use a little work, but a great, great entertainer, and that\u2019s why you\u2019re there.\u201d<\/p>\n

\u201cLet\u2019s all continue to love one another, fight for our God-given freedoms, and most of all, let\u2019s make America rock again,\u201d the ex-president concluded, putting on a red MAGA-style hat that reads \u201cMake America Rock Again.\u201d<\/p>\n

Former President Donald Trump praised Kid Rock\u2019s fans for being \u201cGod-fearing rock-and-roll patriots.\u201d@tammyraber\/TikTok <\/p>\n

As soon as Trump finished his address, the stage lights went on and Rock broke into his inflammatory new song \u201cWe The People\u201d \u2014 which\u00a0bashes President Joe Biden and Dr. Anthony Fauci over vaccine mandates and other policies \u2014 off his new album \u201cBad Reputation.\u201d<\/p>\n

The song features a chorus repeating \u201cLet\u2019s go Brandon,\u201d the not-so-secret code for \u201cF\u2013k Joe Biden.\u201d<\/p>\n

Rock has been an outspoken supporter of Trump since 2016 and has headlined rallies for him.\u00a0<\/p>\n
