<\/p>\n

Joe Rogan offered to train Elon Musk for a hand-to-hand fight with Russian President Vladimir Putin \u2014 and noted that Tesla\u2019s billionaire CEO appeared to hold his own during a past bout with a sumo wrestler. <\/p>\n

Musk, 50, initially challenged 69-year-old Putin to \u201csingle combat\u201d earlier this month, tweeting, \u201cstakes are Ukraine.\u201d He also shared several memes poking fun at the Russian leader on Twitter.<\/p>\n

In a podcast last week, Rogan noted Musk\u2019s recent claims that he previously tussled with a professional sumo wrestler. The Tesla founder claimed last week on Twitter that he \u201cmanaged to throw\u201d the sumo wrestler but suffered a back injury in the process. <\/p>\n

Musk shared an image of the match, although the neither the date nor the authenticity of the photo could be determined.<\/p>\n

\u2018Managed to throw him, but it cost me smashing my c5-c6 disc & 8 years of mega back pain!\u201d Musk tweeted. <\/p>\n

\u201cAccording to him, he had some match with a world champion sumo wrestler back in the day for fun, and he f\u2014ed his neck up, like, throwing this guy outside of the ring,\u201d Rogan said. \u201cBut he actually defeated some world champion sumo wrestler.\u201d<\/p>\n

Rogan, who has cultivated a friendship with Musk and twice invited him to appear on his podcast, suggested that he likes the eccentric billionaire\u2019s chances in a fight with Putin.<\/p>\n

\u201cElon Musk is a strange character, because first of all, he\u2019s a very big man. He\u2019s not small. Putin is smaller than me. Elon is quite a bit bigger than me. Elon is probably 6\u20192\u201d and he\u2019s big, he\u2019s a big guy,\u201d Rogan said.<\/p>\n

Putin, who was trained as a KGB officer, meanwhile has his own history of posting tough-guy photos on the internet, including a famous shot of him riding shirtless on a horse.<\/p>\n

Vladimir Putin caused a stir in 2009 when photos surfaced of him riding a horse bare-chested during his vacation in southern Siberia.AFP via Getty Images <\/p>\n

Musk\u2019s bizarre challenge to Putin had previously drew a response from Chechen warlord Ramzan Kadyrov, a longtime Putin crony. Kadyrov slammed Musk over his words toward Putin, referring to him as \u201cgentle (effeminate) Elona\u201d and claiming he wouldn\u2019t stand a chance in a fight.<\/p>\n

Kadyrov also suggested the billionaire should train with Chechen special forces to toughen up. Musk rejected Kadyrov\u2019s offer \u2013 though he briefly changed his Twitter name to \u201cElona\u201d following the exchange.<\/p>\n

\u201cThank you for the offer, but such excellent training would give me too much of an advantage. If he is afraid to fight, I will agree to use only my left hand and I am not even left-handed,\u201d Musk tweeted.<\/p>\n

Joe Rogan has twice invited Elon Musk to appear on his podcast.Getty Images Vladimir Putin has yet to respond to Elon Musk\u2019s challenge.Anadolu Agency via Getty Images <\/p>\n

Rogan, the popular podcaster who moonlights as a UFC color commentator, revealed he contacted Musk shortly after the Tesla chief challenged Putin to a brawl in response to the war in Ukraine.<\/p>\n

\u201cI offered my services. I texted him. I said \u2018dude, I will arrange all your training.\u2019 I go, \u2018if you really do fight Putin, I will arrange all your training.\u2019\u201d he said during a recent episode of \u201cThe Joe Rogan Experience.\u201d<\/p>\n

Musk, whose personal fortune is estimated to exceed $270 billion, has played an active role in the Russia-Ukraine war. His firm SpaceX has provided \u201cStarlink\u201d internet access to assist the people of Ukraine since fighting began.<\/p>\n

The billionaire has also feuded with the head of Russia\u2019s space agency, who issued an ominous warning that the International Space Station could fall from the sky without the country\u2019s support.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
