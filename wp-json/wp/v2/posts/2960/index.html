Marvel fans are losing their minds on Twitter after learning of an obscure Spider-Man villain who shares a name with the current US president.<\/p>\n

\u201cI\u2019m crying why the f \u2013 \u2013 k is Joe Biden a Spider-Man villain,\u201d wrote one flabbergasted Spider-fan in a tweet with more than 120,000 likes. The fan also included a Wikipedia page for the minor league baddie, whose name is indeed Joe Biden. <\/p>\n

The presidential doppelganger, whose villainous alter ego is called Spectrum, first appeared in \u201cPeter Parker\u00a0<\/em>No. 1\u2033 in 2010, when the real Biden, 79, was the US vice president under Barack Obama. The page even includes an out-of-date disclaimer to warn readers that the web slinger\u2019s nemesis is \u201cnot to be confused with Vice President Joe Biden.\u201d<\/p>\n
