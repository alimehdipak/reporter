\u201cResident Evil\u201d and \u201cFifth Element\u201d actress Milla Jovovich, 46, and her husband, director Paul W.S. Anderson, 57, have put their Los Angeles mansion on the market for $14 million.<\/p>\n

While the pair say they adored the seven-bedroom, 11-bathroom property where they were married in 2009, they\u2019re ready for their next real estate adventure: Turning the over $10 million Hollywood Hills fixer-upper they bought last year into a modern \u201csci-fi dream house\u201d inspired by their work, according to the Wall Street Journal.\u00a0<\/p>\n

\u201cWe were itching to build a house from scratch,\u201d Jovovich told the publication.\u00a0<\/p>\n

Their currently listed home in the Beverly Hills Post Office area is distinctly not futuristic and emanates a distinctly of-this-world decadence.\u00a0<\/p>\n

\u201cWe fell in love with the idea of owning our own Tuscan village \u2014 a compound on sprawling, beautiful land,\u201d Jovovich said of her and Anderson\u2019s inspiration to buy the approximately 11,000-square-foot home, which was built in 2006, for $8.5 million in 2007. \u201cIt is the next best thing to being in Tuscany.\u201d<\/p>\n
