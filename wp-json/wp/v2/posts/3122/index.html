How do you iron Navy summer whites?<\/p>\n

Tip
Wash the uniform inside out in cold water with bleach. Make a vertical crease on each trouser leg. Apply starch. Hang the summer white trousers via a plastic clip hanger. Iron the summer white shirt by placing it regular side out. Place three creases centered on the rear of the shirt.<\/p>\n

Why does the Navy wear white?<\/p>\n

Ships are not free from electrical power failures, blackouts and to give distress calls. White uniforms help easy identification to provide Search and Rescue.<\/p>\n

Is Navy a rank?<\/p>\n

How many uniforms does the Marines have?<\/p>\n

Can Navy put hands in pockets?<\/p>\n
