Some parents give their kids the world \u2014 others give them designer bags.<\/p>\n

Christi Fritz, the mother of a 3-year-old daughter, is feeling the heat on TikTok after revealing the toddler\u2019s designer bag collection.<\/p>\n

In a room tour video online \u2014 during which she showed her kid\u2019s glammed-out, all-pink room, complete with a vanity and chandelier \u2014 eagle-eyed viewers spotted a stash of luxury handbags on a shelf in the little girl\u2019s walk-in closet.<\/p>\n

In response to someone asking why Fritz would ever gift the bags to her young daughter, she explained it\u2019s better to \u201cinvest\u201d in items that will last.<\/p>\n

\u201cShe has three bags and, honestly, she has them because we think it\u2019s better to invest in high-quality bags that will last a long time,\u201d Fritz says in a voice-over for the clip, which has amassed more than 3 million views. \u201cWe bought her the first two little ones, and the big one is actually my old one.
