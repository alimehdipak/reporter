REVEALED<\/h2>\n

Player 1 = Cristiano Ronaldo | Back to top<\/p>\n

Getty<\/p>\n

Somehow we don’t think the snood is enough to disguise one of the most famous footballers on the face of the planet. If you guessed anyone other than Ronaldo then we strongly suggest you find another sport to watch.<\/p>\n

Player 2 = Zlatan Ibrahimovic | Back to top<\/p>\n

Photo by Giuseppe Bellini\/Getty Images<\/p>\n

He may be turning 41 this year, but Ibrahimovic could still roundhouse kick a grapefruit off the top of your head without breaking a sweat.<\/p>\n

Player 3 = Marek Hamsik | Back to top<\/p>\n

Getty<\/p>\n

This should be an easy one, as it’s a player known for his trademark mohawk. Who else but Hamsik, formerly of Napoli and now plying his trade in Turkey?<\/p>\n

Player 4 = Adama Traore | Back to top<\/p>\n

David Ramos\/Getty Images<\/p>\n

Traore is one of the fastest wingers in the world and can usually be found flying down the flanks at Barcelona, having moved on loan from Wolves.<\/p>\n

Player 5 = Olivier Giroud | Back to top<\/p>\n

Emilio Andreoli\/Getty Images<\/p>\n

The veteran World Cup winner is still going strong at AC Milan after a fine career with Arsenal and Chelsea.<\/p>\n

Player 6 = Karim Benzema | Back to top<\/p>\n

David S. Bustamante\/Soccrates\/Getty Images<\/p>\n

You may not be familiar with the Real Madrid striker’s secondary career as a hip-hop artist, but take it from us, he’s much, MUCH better at the day job.<\/p>\n

Player 7 = Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang | Back to top<\/p>\n

Quality Sport Images\/Getty Images<\/p>\n

Given his excellent form since moving to Barcelona on a free transfer from Arsenal over the summer, the noted “Dragonball Z” aficionado and anime fan is likely to go Super Saiyan at any moment.<\/p>\n

Player 8 = Leroy Sane | Back to top<\/p>\n

Carlos Rodrigues\/Getty Images<\/p>\n

You’d look windswept too if you hit the kind of top speeds that Bayern Munich winger Sane reaches on a regular basis.<\/p>\n

Player 9 = Dani Alves | Back to top<\/p>\n

Pedro Salado\/Quality Sport Images\/Getty Images<\/p>\n

One of the most decorated players in the history of football is back at Barcelona. Alves has 42 career titles and is looking to add more, even at 38 years old.<\/p>\n

Player 10 = Vinicius Junior | Back to top<\/p>\n

Juan Lazkano\/DeFodi Images via Getty Images<\/p>\n

The 21-year-old Real Madrid starlet has really hit his stride for his club and country this season.<\/p>\n

Player 11 = Joao Felix | Back to top<\/p>\n

Adam Davy\/PA Images\/Getty Images<\/p>\n

Player 12 = Guillermo Ochoa | Back to top<\/p>\n

Hector Vivas\/Getty Images<\/p>\n
