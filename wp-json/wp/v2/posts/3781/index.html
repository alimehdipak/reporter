Learn and grow from your mistakes<\/h3>\n

At the time, Monique was disappointed in herself for not living up to her standards, but she says that experience didn\u2019t go to waste \u2014 in fact, it helped her grow. \u201cTruthfully that experience was so necessary because it made me understand how important it is to be prepared, especially if I want to represent my business in a meaningful way.\u201d<\/p>\n

Instead of feeling defeated and discouraged from working with brands, Monique challenged herself to try again, this time learning from her mistakes and letting her values guide her to do better. \u201cTo me every failure is an opportunity to learn, grow and get better. I like to call it failing forward. Mistakes and failures are inevitable, but I refuse to let mine go to waste.\u201d<\/p>\n

Don\u2019t be afraid to ask for help<\/h3>\n

Monique turned to her friends, her boyfriend and fellow creators to help her get up again after that first campaign went sideways. \u201cHaving a support system really helps when you\u2019re suffering from self-doubt,\u201d Monique says. \u201cDon\u2019t be afraid to ask for help. You\u2019d be surprised at how many people want to help you and to see you succeed.\u201d<\/p>\n

Share your highs and lows with your followers<\/h3>\n

Monique also says creators shouldn\u2019t be afraid to share their challenges and mistakes with their followers. \u201dPeople love a comeback story and things they can relate to. I\u2019m all about being authentic and bringing my followers along with me for the ride. That way they feel more connected to my work.\u201d<\/p>\n
