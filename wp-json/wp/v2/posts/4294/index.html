The 32-year-old \u201cHeroes\u201d star revealed she had a secret addiction to opioids and suffered from alcoholism as well as postpartum depression.<\/p>\n

\u201cI was on top of the world and I ruined it,\u201d she told People recently. \u201cI\u2019d think I hit rock bottom, but then there\u2019s that trap door that opens.\u201d<\/p>\n

Panettiere shares a daughter,\u00a0Kaya, 7, with her ex Wladimir Klitschko and sought help for her health battles for the sake of her family after years of public drama.<\/p>\n

\u201cI put a lot of work into myself and I had to be willing to be incredibly honest,\u201d she divulged.<\/p>\n

\u201cThey were to make me peppy during interviews,\u201d Panettiere looked back. \u201cI had no idea that this was not an appropriate thing, or what door that would open for me when it came to my addiction.\u201d<\/p>\n

And as I got older, the drugs and alcohol became something I almost couldn\u2019t live without.\u201d<\/p>\n
