Meghan Markle is loving life in NYC. <\/p>\n

Last night, the Duchess of Sussex stepped out in a black-and-white jumpsuit by Gabriela Hearst \u2014\u00a0and fans on social media were quick to point out the similarity to a gown recently worn by her sister-in-law, Kate Middleton.<\/p>\n

Markle, 40, looked sleek in her strapless outfit as she joined Prince Harry, 37, for dinner at Locanda Verde after the two visited the United Nations to mark Nelson Mandela Day. <\/p>\n

Wearing a strapless black jumpsuit with flared legs, black and white ties at the back and a wide white band at the top, the duchess\u2019 look was similar in style to the Roland Mouret dress the Duchess of Cambridge wore to the \u201cTop Gun: Maverick\u201d premiere.<\/p>\n

Unsurprisingly, Team Meghan and Team Kate had different opinions on the look.<\/p>\n

Meanwhile, more fans argued on Twitter. \u201cIts so so different,\u201d a Meghan fan wrote. \u201cLook at the back. Meghan markle is a style icon. Kate looks to her for styles.\u201d<\/p>\n
