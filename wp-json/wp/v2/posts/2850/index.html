Comics Dave Chappelle and Chris Rock are standing up for themselves in light of their infamous onstage attacks.<\/p>\n

Chappelle \u2014 who was tackled by a knife-wielding assailant during a set at the Hollywood Bowl on Tuesday \u2014 and Rock, who made headlines for being slapped by Will Smith at this year\u2019s Oscars, both performed a \u201csecret\u201d comedy show Thursday night in Los Angeles, according to the Hollywood Reporter.<\/p>\n

The tight-lipped show took place inside a small venue in the Comedy Store club and featured a star-studded audience consisting of Kim Kardashian, rappers Sean \u201cDiddy\u201d Combs and Yasiin Bey (Mos Def), in addition to comedian Jeff Ross.<\/p>\n

Fans paid $160 to see a cigarette-smoking Chappelle, who was billed as a last-minute, unnamed comedian. After raucous ovation, he was joined onstage by Rock about 10 minutes into his set as the duo made light of their vicious circumstances. <\/p>\n

In response, Rock \u2014 who previously poked fun at Chappelle\u2019s open-field tackle \u2014 took the opportunity to clap back at Smith for the now months-long drama he caused.<\/p>\n

\u201cAt least you got smacked by someone of repute!\u201d Chappelle said to Rock in front of the 70-seat crowd. \u201cI got smacked by a homeless guy with leaves in his hair.\u201d<\/p>\n

Rock chuckled and quipped back: \u201cI got smacked by the softest n\u2014a that ever rapped,\u201d reported the Hollywood Reporter.<\/p>\n

Eventually the two moved on to make punchlines about other currents, such as Amber Heard and Johnny Depp\u2019s defamation court case and the looming uncertainty of Roe v. Wade.<\/p>\n

Then Chappelle decided to take some shots at Combs.<\/p>\n

\u201cI am in the [place] where you sat in the car with Biggie Smalls as he died,\u201d Chappelle said near his set\u2019s end, eyeing Combs. \u201cI hate this city.\u201d<\/p>\n

After Chappelle was leveled by since-arrested 23-year-old Isaiah Lee \u2014 who had his arm broken by the comic\u2019s security team \u2014 celebrities and comedians across the nation rushed to the headliner\u2019s defense.<\/p>\n

He continued, praising the fact that \u201cDave went back after that and finished doing the show.\u201d<\/p>\n
