5 Types of Residential Window Tinting Films<\/h2>\n

1. Heat Blocking Film<\/h3>\n

Homes can become hot during summers, depending on the amount of sun exposure and the size of windows. To make the environment cooler in hot temperatures, people often overuse AC units, resulting in increased energy bills and costly repairs. But you can prevent all this hustle by installing window tinting films. These films can block 85% of infrared rays from entering your house. This means your rooms will retain heat. So your home will be cooler in summer and warmer in winter.<\/p>\n

2. Decorative Window Tint<\/h3>\n

As the name suggests, decorative window tints add curb appeal to your home windows. You can choose from a variety of colors, designs, and patterns. These films can be used to enhance your room\u2019s appearance while adding a layer of privacy to it. Depending on your needs, you can choose opaque, frosted, colored, or translucent window tints. So, if your home\u2019s appearance and privacy are your concern, decorative window tint can be the solution!<\/p>\n

3. UV Protection Window Film<\/h3>\n

Sun\u2019s ultraviolet radiation can damage skin and cause fading of furniture, carpets, and other items. However, installing window films with UV protection can help block 99% of the sun\u2019s UV rays while still allowing some natural light into your home. If fading and skin damage is your concern, consider UV protection window film to enjoy natural light while keeping everyone and everything in your home safe.<\/p>\n

4. Glare Blocking Window Film<\/h3>\n

Glare can cause irritation when it makes it difficult for us to see the screens of computers, cellphones, and television sets. Installing glare-blocking window film is important for offices to help workers focus well. This can improve the team\u2019s productivity and comfort. So instead of using curtains and obstructing the beautiful outside view, you can use glare-blocking window films in your homes and offices.<\/p>\n

5. Security Window Tint<\/h3>\n

Home windows are often the weakest area of your home regarding security. High-speed flying objects can easily shatter glass. But with security window films, you can have peace of mind knowing your windows won\u2019t break during an intrusion or natural hazard. Security window films are the thickest films that hold the shattered pieces of glass together. By installing security window films, you can add an extra layer of security to your home.<\/p>\n
