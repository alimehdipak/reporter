David Crosby went viral over the weekend for his reaction to a piece of fan art.<\/p>\n

An admirer of the singer-songwriter shared a drawing of him on Twitter and wrote, \u201cHi @thedavidcrosby hope you are keeping well. Did a picture of you\u2026. Thanks for the music.\u201d<\/p>\n

The 80-year-old founding member of Crosby, Stills & Nash was blunt with his reaction, saying: \u201cThat is the weirdest painting of me. I have ever seen \u2026..don\u2019t quit your day job.\u201d<\/p>\n

People were quick to blast Crosby for being harsh to a fan.<\/p>\n

\u201cThis is the weirdest reaction to a fan trying to do something nice I have ever seen,\u201d a user wrote.<\/p>\n
