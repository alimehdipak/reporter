Billy Joel\u2019s life and prolific career may be getting the biopic treatment, but the musician won\u2019t be involved in any capacity.<\/p>\n

The upcoming \u201cPiano Man\u201d is in development at Michael Jai White\u2019s\u00a0Jaigantic Studios with Adam Ripp attached to write and direct the feature film, Variety reported.<\/p>\n

The movie will dive into the \u201cUptown Girl\u201d singer\u2019s early life, his discovery by The Hassles\u2019 band manager Irwin Mazur and his working relationship with Clive Davis.<\/p>\n

\u201cBilly Joel is not involved with this film project. No rights in music, name\/likeness or life story will be granted,\u201d a rep for the 72-year-old confirmed to The Post.<\/p>\n

According to Variety, producers noted that music necessities for the flick are \u201cyet to be determined.\u201d<\/p>\n

The \u201cPiano Man\u201d singer has won five Grammy Awards over the course of his long career.Ethan Miller <\/p>\n

Jaigantic Studios was able to work around Joel by acquiring the life rights to Mazur. The music rep worked with the Long Island native from 1970 until 1972.<\/p>\n

Mazur\u2019s father was the proprietor of a club where Joel first linked up with The Hassles in 1966.<\/p>\n

White, Mazur and Ripp will be producing the film alongside Mayne Berke and Donovan de Boer. <\/p>\n

The \u201cDevil\u2019s Whisper\u201d director\u2019s father Artie Ripp had signed the five-time Grammy winner to his first recording contract and even produced his 1971 debut solo album, \u201cCold Spring Harbor.\u201d <\/p>\n

Ripp said in a statement, \u201cBilly Joel has been a part of my life since my father signed him to his record label when I was 4 years old; his music is ingrained in my DNA and it\u2019s been a dream of mine as a filmmaker to explore and celebrate the untold story of how Billy Joel became the Piano Man.\u201d<\/p>\n

Billy Joel plays the accordion during a soundcheck in New York, December 1977.Michael Putland <\/p>\n

\u201cWhat a dream come true! These are the real-life events I witnessed first-hand that shaped Billy from a keyboard player in the local Long Island band, The Hassles, into the iconic musician, singer-songwriter, and entertainer who thrills audiences around the world, today,\u201d Mazur added.<\/p>\n

\u201cAs a lifelong fan of Billy Joel, I am elated to be part of the team to produce his biopic. Billy Joel is the key figure on the Mount Rushmore of American Music,\u201d White chimed in.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
