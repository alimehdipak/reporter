Britney Spears\u2019 rocky road to love is paved with men who have used her for her fame and fortune.<\/p>\n

Those who know the pop princess well hope that her marriage to long-time boyfriend, Sam Asghari, 28, will be her happily-ever-after following her 2021 release from conservatorship.<\/p>\n

But one Hollywood source who worked closely with Spears, 40, told The Post: \u201cI honestly just really hope that they\u2019ve signed a prenup. This guy\u2019s a wannabe actor, and I hope this is real.<\/p>\n

\u201cYou just want her not to be taken advantage of.\u201d<\/p>\n

Although Spears\u2019 brother Bryan was due to be at the wedding, her mom, Lynne, dad Jamie, younger sister Jamie Lynn were not invited. Her two boys, Sean, 16, and Jayden, 15, were not attending either, although a lawyer for their dad, Kevin Federline, said: \u201cAlthough the boys will not be in attendance, Kevin and the boys are happy for Britney and wish her and Sam all the best going forward.\u201d<\/p>\n

\u201cI think it\u2019s a good thing, if everything goes well,\u201d a former boyfriend of Spears told The Post Thursday of the marriage. \u201cI do worry about the age difference, and I was around Hollywood enough to know that it\u2019s hard to tell when someone loves you for you and when they\u2019re after your money. But I don\u2019t want to rain on Britney\u2019s parade. I hope it\u2019s a good thing.\u201d<\/p>\n

\u201cWe had a great time together, she\u2019s a great girl, even if the people around her were a little sketchy. But I hope that\u2019s behind her now.\u201d<\/p>\n

Here are the lovers Spears has left in her wake:<\/p>\n

Justin Timberlake (1998-2002)<\/h2>\n

Justin Timberlake met Britney Spears when they were 12, filming \u201cThe Mickey Mouse Club.\u201dGetty Images <\/p>\n

The former couple first met in the early \u201990s while filming \u201cThe Mickey Mouse Club.\u201d<\/p>\n

\u201cLike, we\u2019ve gone through so much together and we\u2019ve known each other since we were 12 years old,\u201d Spears gushed to The Observer in 2001. We know each other inside and out.\u201d<\/p>\n

Timberlake, the former NSYNC heartthrob, told GQ magazine in 2006: \u201cI was infatuated with her from the moment I saw her.\u201d<\/p>\n

The Hollywood source who worked with Spears told The Post: \u201cThe real love of her life was Justin, because it was true love in the beginning and they were on the same level. Britney was in fact a little bigger, because she was solo and Justin was in a boy band.<\/p>\n

\u201cBritney really loved Justin. They were best friends and they grew up in the same industry \u2026 until they grew out of each other.\u201d<\/p>\n

Timberlake has apologized to Spears for not speaking up for her in the past.WireImage <\/p>\n

In 2002, the two broke up amid allegations that Spears had cheated on Timberlake with her choreographer Wade Robson.<\/p>\n

Timberlake, now 41, released his hit heartbreak track \u201cCry Me a River\u201d that same year, hiring a Britney lookalike as the cheating star of the video.<\/p>\n

Now married to Jessica Biel, Timberlake has apologized to Spears for not speaking up for her in the past. When she spilled the agonizing details of her conservatorship in court in June 2021,\u00a0Timberlake tweeted: \u201cAfter what we saw today, we should all be supporting Britney at this time.\u201d<\/p>\n

Earlier this year, Spears blasted Timberlake for \u201cusing\u201d her name to sell records of his debut album, \u201cJustified,\u201d back in 2002 \u2014\u00a0writing in a since-deleted Instagram post that Jesus had spoken to her about those who betrayed her:\u00a0\u201cYour ex did the same thing \u2026 with his first album using your name claiming you did him dirty!!!!\u201d<\/p>\n

Wade Robson\u00a0(2001-2002)<\/h2>\n

Wade Robson was a choreographer for Spears and NSYNC.Corbis via Getty Images <\/p>\n

Sources who know Spears confirmed to The Post that Robson and Spears hooked up behind Timberlake\u2019s back, prompting the sweethearts\u2019 split \u2014 and \u201cCry Me a River.\u201d<\/p>\n

Robson, 39 \u2014 who featured in HBO\u2019s \u201cLeaving Neverland\u201d doc, in which he accused Michael Jackson of molesting him when he was 7 \u2014 was Spears\u2019 choreographer and worked with NSYNC as well.<\/p>\n

Legend has it that Timberlake uncovered the alleged affair when he found a note from Robson in Spears\u2019 dressing\u00a0room in February 2002, on the night that the couple were scheduled to appear on \u201cSaturday Night Live.\u201d<\/p>\n

Robson, who now runs a health and wellness website, was unavailable for comment.<\/p>\n

Jason Alexander (2004)<\/h2>\n

Jason Alexander\u2019s marriage to Spears lasted only 55 hours.FilmMagic <\/p>\n

The Louisiana native, 40, is best known for his short-lived marriage to Spears. It lasted only 55 hours \u2014\u00a0but he continues to haunt her.<\/p>\n

On Thursday, Alexander live-streamed himself on Instagram somehow getting inside Spears\u2019 home \u2014\u00a0in an attempt to crash her wedding to Asghari \u2014 and being apprehended by police<\/p>\n

They were childhood friends who decided to tie the knot in the early hours of\u00a0Jan. 3, 2004, at The Little White Wedding Chapel in Las Vegas. The marriage was then annulled due to pressure from the pop star\u2019s family. <\/p>\n

Divorce attorney Mark Goldberg later said the pair had wanted to stay wed: \u201cIt was the mother interfering in and inserting herself into her daughter\u2019s life.<\/p>\n

\u201cShe came to Las Vegas, threw Jason out and got him a plane ticket home.<\/p>\n

Despite this, a source who knows Spears told The Post: \u201cIt was not real with Jason.\u201d<\/p>\n

Alexander showed he still supports his ex-wife from a distance by participating in a #FreeBritney protest in August 2020 amid her 13-year conservatorship battle.<\/p>\n

\u201cShe\u2019s really been a prisoner,\u201d he said on a podcast at the time, according to Newsweek.<\/p>\n

Kevin Federline (2004-2007)<\/h2>\n

Spears has two sons with Kevin Fedeline.FilmMagic <\/p>\n

Spears wed Federline, a backup dancer, in September of the same year her first marriage was annulled. The pair \u2014 who share\u00a0matching dice tattoos on their wrists \u2014 got engaged just two months after meeting and surprised loved ones with a top-secret wedding three months after that.<\/p>\n

\u201cI think my mom was dumbfounded,\u201d Spears said on the couple\u2019s fever dream of a reality show \u201cBritney & Kevin: Chaotic.\u201d<\/p>\n

The two were a constant source of public\u00a0ridicule and practically fueled the tabloid industry and paparazzi machine during their relationship, with Federline\u2019s look \u2014 Von Dutch hat, gravity-defying\u00a0low-slung pants and a sleeveless undershirt \u2014 inspiring countless Halloween costumes.\u00a0<\/p>\n

Insiders say Spears really did love Federline in the moment.J.Sciulli\/WireImage for Fingerprint Communications <\/p>\n

They went on\u00a0to welcome two sons, Sean Preston and Jayden James, but separated in November 2006, just before Spears\u2019 tragic series of public meltdowns.<\/p>\n

The\u00a0source who knows Spears told The Post: \u201cI think Britney did love Kevin for the moment. [He] was weirdly stable for her. It was a time of her life when she was grabbing on to something.<\/p>\n

\u201cI think the day they fell out of love was when she started losing it a little bit.\u201d<\/p>\n

Federline\u2019s attorney added: \u201cHe wants her to be happy and believes this will be a positive step for her in a\u00a0post-conservatorship era, building a healthy, happy future going forward.\u201d<\/p>\n

Adnan Ghalib (2007-2008)<\/h2>\n

Adnan Ghalib was a Hollywood paparazzi who followed Spears\u2019 every move before dating her.mavrixphoto.com <\/p>\n

Ghalib was one of the Hollywood paparazzi who hounded Spears\u2019 every move \u2014 until they ended up dating before her conservatorship was put into effect.<\/p>\n

The relationship began in December 2007 just after her split with Federline, much to the dismay of those closest to Spears. Her cousin and former assistant Alli Sims told US Weekly she disapproved of British-born Ghalib.<\/p>\n

\u201cI only know who he is through him following us for the last eight months,\u201d she said at the time. \u201cI do not think Adnan is a good person; I think he only has bad intentions. He has always given me a weird feeling and creeped me out. I wish he would just go away.\u201d<\/p>\n

In January 2009, the star\u2019s dad, Jamie Spears, filed for a restraining order against Ghalib and other associates; the photographer was eventually ordered to stay away from Spears and her family for three years. <\/p>\n

Jason Trawick (2009-2013)<\/h2>\n

According to the rumor mill, Spears\u2019 relationship with agent Jason Trawick ended because she wanted more children while he did not.WireImage <\/p>\n

Spears started dating the Hollywood agent, who worked at powerhouse agency WME and had once represented her, in April 2009. After they became engaged in 2011, a request was approved for Trawick to be in charge of the singer\u2019s court-imposed conservatorship.\u00a0<\/p>\n

\u201cJamie\u2019s thrilled,\u201d Spears\u2019 attorney, Geraldine Wyle, said at the time.\u00a0\u201cIt\u2019s all in the family.\u201d<\/p>\n

But less than a year later, Trawick resigned from the position on the same day that Spears changed talent agencies. The next day, the singer\u2019s rep announced that the couple had ended their engagement, and TMZ reported that it was because Spears wanted more children and Trawick did not.\u00a0\u00a0<\/p>\n

Last year Trawick shot down persistent rumors that they were secretly married back in 2012.<\/p>\n

David Lucado (2013-2014)<\/h2>\n

Non-celebrity David Lucado believed Spears did not need a conservatorship.FilmMagic <\/p>\n

The pair began dating in 2013 and called it quits a year and a half later after rumors of his alleged infidelity, which he has adamantly denied.<\/p>\n

Despite their rocky history, normal dude Lucado, who worked at a law firm, was\u00a0a firm believer that Spears no longer needed people controlling her assets.<\/p>\n

\u201cIf anyone knew the real Britney, they would know that she would rather be remembered for being the great mother she is rather than the artist she is,\u201d he has said. \u201cAnd if anyone could see her interactions with her kids, they would know that there is no need for a conservatorship over Britney\u2019s personal life.\u201d<\/p>\n

Charlie Ebersol (2014-2015)<\/h2>\n

On their first date, Charlie Ebersol cooked for Spears.FilmMagic <\/p>\n

Spears and Ebersol \u2014\u00a0the son of former NBC Sports chairman Dick Ebersol and actress Susan Saint James \u2014\u00a0reportedly shared a cozy domestic life together, with the radio producer cooking for her on their first date.<\/p>\n

\u201cFor my birthday, we made waffles on the floor, with candles, and he bought me a real beautiful cake and a beautiful gift that I have on the wall,\u201d she told People while they were together. \u201cOne of my favorite songs is \u2018Thank U,\u2019 by\u00a0Alanis Morissette, and he put all the words to the song in a long frame. It was really thoughtful.\u201d<\/p>\n

The source who knows Spears told The Post: \u201cI think Britney loved Charlie. I was shocked when they split.<\/p>\n

\u201cOut of all of her\u00a0boyfriends, he was the most reliable.\u201d <\/p>\n

Sam Asghari (2016-current)<\/h2>\n

When Sam Asghari met Spears, he said he had butterflies in his stomach.Getty Images <\/p>\n

Asghari said it was love at first sight when he met Spears in October 2016 on the set of her \u201cSlumber Party\u201d music video. \u201cI was excited that I [would] get to meet one of the biggest artists of all time,\u201d the personal trainer told Men\u2019s Health in July 2018. \u201cI had butterflies.\u201d<\/p>\n

But when Asghari, who was born in Iran, tried to\u00a0break the ice, he almost ruined his chances with the star.<\/p>\n

\u201cShe said, \u2018Hi, I\u2019m Britney,\u2019 and I said, \u2018I\u2019m sorry, what\u2019s your name again?\u2019\u201d he told the magazine: \u201cI tried to be funny. I don\u2019t think anybody got it.\u201d<\/p>\n
