          The Survival Rate of Oral Cancer<\/h2>\n

          Overall,\u00a060%\u00a0of people with oral cancer will survive for 5 years or more. The possibility of surviving after treatment will increase for earlier stages. In fact, if you have stage 1 or 2 oral cancer, your five-year overall survival rate is typically between 70% and 90%. This makes early diagnosis and timely treatment most important.<\/p>\n
