Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck are wasting no time solidifying their future plans together. After months of searching for their perfect home for their blended family, it seems they finally found it. <\/p>\n

The two lovebirds are in contract to shell out more than $50 million for a massive Bel-Air, Los Angeles, abode spanning nearly 20,000 square feet, according to TMZ.<\/p>\n

And it looks like, despite the hefty price to be paid, they\u2019re still snagging a deal for the gigantic property, which will offer plenty of room for their children to play.<\/p>\n

The property first went up for sale in February for a whopping $65 million.<\/p>\n

In 2016, Todd Lemkin, a Texas hedge fund manager, purchased the traditional home for $27.6 million. So it looks like he is walking away with one heck of a profit \u2014 a win-win situation for all.<\/p>\n

Comprised of 10 bedrooms and 17 bathrooms, the estate sits in a secluded patch surrounded by trees for optimal privacy.<\/p>\n
