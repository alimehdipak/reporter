About Reese Witherspoon\u2019s Book Club<\/h2>\n

Each month, Witherspoon selects a female-protagonist-centered read for her audience on the first Tuesday of the month. Pretty much, it feels like we\u2019re all BFFs with Witherspoon with a shared interest in beautiful scenery, dynamic plots (and plot twists!), and heartstring-pulling characters.<\/p>\n

As an avid reader, I\u2019ve perused her picks and found some of my favorites through the much-loved club, including \u201cThe Jetsetters,\u201d \u201cSuch A Fun Age\u201d and \u201cWhere The Crawdads Sing.\u201d<\/p>\n

What\u2019s more, most of Witherspoon\u2019s picks are available on Audible and Kindle platforms and in paperback and hardcover form.<\/p>\n

Ahead, find all of Reese Witherspoon\u2019s book club picks \u2014 along with ratings, book descriptions from Amazon and Witherspoon herself, and reviews \u2014 since the first-ever featured book in June 2017. <\/p>\n
