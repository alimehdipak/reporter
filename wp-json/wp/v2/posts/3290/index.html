Christian Bale is unrecognizable in \u201cThor: Love and Thunder,\u201d but while fans are not surprised by his dramatic shift in appearance, they\u2019re likening it to a heavy metal band photo.<\/p>\n

Previously known for DC Comic\u2019s \u201cBatman,\u201d Bale is crossing over to the Marvel Cinematic Universe in the latest installment, playing the villain this time.<\/p>\n

On-screen, he portrays Gorr the God Butcher, who seeks to destroy all gods, going head-to-head with hero Thor, played by Chris Hemsworth.<\/p>\n

Hemsworth, 38, promised fans the movie \u201cis gonna blow your mind\u201d in a clip posted to Instagram \u2014 and true to his word, it didn\u2019t disappoint.<\/p>\n

The film trailer showed a chilling first look at Bale, whose character appears bloodied and scarred. The sneak peek bewildered fans who described his look as unrecognizable, comparing him to Marilyn Manson and Voldemort from the \u201cHarry Potter\u201d series.<\/p>\n

In the trailer, Bale as Gorr emerges from what looks like a shadowy forest before cutting to various scenes of fighting between Gorr and other heroes, as well as Thor reuniting with ex-girlfriend Jane Foster, played by Natalie Portman.<\/p>\n

Bale was compared to Marilyn Manson and Voldemort by surprised fans online.Marvel Entertainment <\/p>\n

\u201cYou are not like the other gods I\u2019ve killed,\u201d Gorr says to heroes Thor and Valkyrie, played by Tessa Thompson, as they duke it out on screen.<\/p>\n

The latest teaser debuted Monday via the official Marvel Entertainment YouTube channel, and within two hours it garnered more than two million views. As of Tuesday, it had amassed more than 13 million.<\/p>\n

Gorr fights Thor, played by Hemsworth, in the film.Marvel Entertainment <\/p>\n

\u201cYou flicked too hard, dammit!\u201d a stripped-down Thor yells as he\u2019s restrained before Zeus, his full package making the women around him faint.<\/p>\n
