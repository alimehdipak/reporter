Are terminals B and C connected at Logan?<\/h3>\n

Walkways connect terminals B, C and E, and there are regular shuttle buses that go to every terminal, as well as the airport MBTA station, the Logan boat dock and economy parking. Logan shuttle buses run 24 hours a day, stop at each terminal every 15 to 20 minutes and are wheelchair-lift equipped.<\/p>\n

What is a Delta one flight?<\/p>\n

Delta One is Delta\u2019s most premium fare type, usually only available on international flights. It includes a lie-flat bed, premium food and beverage, and other luxury perks. It is not the same as Delta\u2019s official first class fares, which offer fewer perks and are available on shorter and domestic flights.<\/p>\n

How do I find out what terminal I fly from?<\/p>\n

You can find the terminal number on your e-mail confirmation when you first book a flight. The gate number is typically offered when you check in for your flight online or in person at the airport.
