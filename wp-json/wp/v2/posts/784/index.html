What are the benefits of keeping your teeth clean and healthy?<\/b><\/h2>\n

It is beneficial for your health to keep your teeth clean and healthy. By doing so, you are able to maintain your oral hygiene. Your teeth need to be clean in order for them to function properly and for them to be healthy. Additionally, keeping your teeth clean and healthy can help you avoid toothache and other dental problems.
