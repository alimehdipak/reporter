A California coroner is probing the untimely death of 34-year-old Dance Gavin Dance bassist Tim Feerick, an official told The Post on Friday.<\/p>\n

The rocker was found dead Wednesday of unknown causes at a modest apartment complex known as \u201cThe Retreat\u201d on the city\u2019s east side, according to\u00a0records from the coroner\u2019s office in Sacramento, Calif.<\/p>\n

Members of the post-hardcore screamo band announced Feerick\u2019s passing on social media Thursday, prompting an outpouring of grief from dedicated fans.<\/p>\n

A small crowd of friends and band devotees gathered at the city\u2019s Afterburner mural Thursday night to light candles and blast Dance Gavin Dance tunes on a portable speaker in Feerick\u2019s honor, footage shows.<\/p>\n

Others mourned from afar.<\/p>\n

\u201cLighting [a candle] all the way from Scotland tonight. Been a fan of DGD since early 2014, Tim is and forever will be my inspiration as a bassist,\u201d one fan wrote on Twitter. \u201cRest in peace, Tim.\u201d<\/p>\n

On Thursday, the band announced Feerick\u2019s death on Twitter, writing, \u201cWe are absolutely devastated to share the news that our friend and bass player, Tim Feerick, passed away last night.\u201d<\/p>\n

Feerick first joined the band as a touring member in 2009 before joining officially in 2012.<\/p>\n

The band was scheduled to embark on a 19-date national tour later this month, according to its website.<\/p>\n
