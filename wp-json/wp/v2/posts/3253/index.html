When sisters Nicky and Simone Zimmermann co-founded their Sydney-based design house in 1991, they had no idea it would turn into one of the most stylish brands in the world \u2014 beloved by A-listers like Beyonc\u00e9, Kate Middleton, Kendall Jenner and Margot Robbie. <\/p>\n

What started as a simple swimwear and casual resort line has bloomed into a worldwide empire, complete with runway shows and stores throughout the United States, Australia and Europe. <\/p>\n

The label\u2019s easy-breezy, feminine sophistication, mixed with bright hues and playful prints, oozes summer fun and has proved to be catnip for the elevated beach set. <\/p>\n

Zimmermann will open its seventh New York shop next month in Southampton, a sister store to its East Hampton outpost, which is already a local favorite. Here, Nicky Zimmermann shares her sleek \u2014 and oh-so chic \u2014 picks for summer.<\/p>\n

\u201cI always like to have fresh-cut orchids at home. They have such an intense color and each flower has its own unique beauty.\u201d <\/h2>\n

\u201cVanda\u201d orchids (two stems), $65 at Flowerbx <\/p>\n

\u201cThis dress is a different take on a kaftan and has a real sense of ease to it. I love the deep color \u2014 it\u2019s really beautiful.\u201d<\/h2>\n

Beverley Rogers \u201c22-62\u201d painting, $163 at Martumili <\/p>\n

\u201cThe shape of this chair is so elegant. We have one in our Paris store and around our dining table at home. They swivel, which everyone seems to enjoy!\u201d<\/h2>\n

\u201cTulip\u201d chair, $2,280 at Knoll <\/p>\n
