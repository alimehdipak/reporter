Fans of \u201cJeopardy!\u201d are buzzing on Twitter about a potential new host.\u00a0<\/p>\n

Former Tournament of Champions winner Buzzy Cohen caused a stir when he shared a cryptic tweet that many believe to be a signal that he\u2019ll be hosting the long-running game show.<\/p>\n

Last week, fan account BuzzerBlog tweeted, \u201c100% prepared to be wrong but still not totally believing that the Mayim-hosts-primetime-Jeopardy thing is still here. If that was still a thing I feel like the many press releases talking about\u00a0\u2018Celebrity Jeopardy!\u2019\u00a0would have mentioned her by now.\u201d<\/p>\n

Cohen, 37, replied to the tweet with a gif of Yoda saying \u201cNo, there is another.\u201d<\/p>\n

Feeding into the theories, the \u201cJeopardy!\u201d alum shared yet another secretive tweet.\u00a0<\/p>\n

Buzzy Cohen was known as \u201cMr. Personality\u201d to Alex Trebek.Jeopardy! <\/p>\n

\u201cShout out to the camera guys on [as yet unannounced project] for convincing me to get a picopresso from\u00a0@wacaco_ltd\u00a0\u2013 cannot wait to get this thing going!!!\u201d he wrote.<\/p>\n

However, host Mayim Bialik this week revealed on social media that she tested positive for COVID, despite being vaccinated and boosted and staying \u201cvery vigilant\u201d while traveling. <\/p>\n

\u201cI feel guilty that I went out. I feel sad and I\u2019m worried,\u201d said an \u201cexhausted\u201d Bialik. \u201cI\u2019m just worried\u00a0about the world again and our health in the world.\u201d<\/p>\n

The Post has reached out to Sony, which produces the game show, for comment.<\/p>\n

Meanwhile, in light of Cohen\u2019s post, \u201cJeopardy!\u201d fans immediately jumped on the speculation.<\/p>\n

\u201cDon\u2019t tease us you menace!!!!\u201d one user responded.<\/p>\n

\u201cBUZZY IS THIS AN ANNOUNCEMENT,\u201d another excitedly wrote.<\/p>\n

\u201cA lot to unpack here,\u201d another said.<\/p>\n

Buzzy Cohen earned the Grand Champion title in the Tournament of Champions in 2017.Peggy Sirota\/ABC via Getty Images <\/p>\n

Cohen, who was called \u201cMr. Personality\u201d by Alex Trebek, earned the \u201cGrand Champion\u201d title in the Tournament of Champions in 2017. He guest-hosted the show for the 2021 Tournament of Champions after Trebek\u2019s passing.<\/p>\n

Mayim Bialik, 46, and Ken Jennings, 48, have taken turns as \u201cJeopardy!\u201d hosts, though Jennings announced in May he would be taking a break from hosting for a \u201cfew months.\u201d <\/p>\n
