They haven\u2019t Heard enough.<\/p>\n

Fans desperate for access into the Fairfax, Virginia, courtroom where Johnny Depp and Amber Heard are locked in a heated defamation trial have been forking over thousands for a coveted seat. But now, they\u2019re even buying from scalpers on the \u201cblack market,\u201d according to TMZ, turning the orderly waiting game into a free for all.<\/p>\n

Court officials have limited numbers of wristbands to hand out \u2013\u00a0100 for the main courtroom and an additional 100 to 150 for the overflow room \u2013 yet many fans don\u2019t make the cut. TMZ reported that people have engaged in screaming matches if others cut them in line and there\u2019s even a \u201cblack market\u201d for wristbands, where people are offering cash to buy a front-row seat.<\/p>\n

Many of the fans, according to TMZ, are rabid supporters of 58-year-old Depp, who also has hordes of fans on social media siding with him and railing against Heard, 36.<\/p>\n

Since the beginning of the trial, fans have camped out and lined up outside the courthouse in the hopes of witnessing the proceedings in person. <\/p>\n

Fans of Johnny Depp line up outside the courthouse to watch the trial against Amber Heard.EPA Some fans began fighting over wristbands, according to TMZ.Cliff Owen\/CNP for NY Post Depp\u2019s fans line up as early as the night before to secure a seat inside the courtroom.Ron Sachs\/CNP for NY Post <\/p>\n

People began lining up at 10 p.m. Sunday ahead of Monday\u2019s 9 a.m. start time, the day Heard took the witness stand for a second time.<\/p>\n

But fans may have pushed their luck a little too far, prompting the sheriff to post a sign that read: \u201cEffective immediately the line for the Johnny Depp and Amber Heard case spectators are not permitted to loiter or camp out on the judicial complex before 1 a.m.\u201d<\/p>\n

When the trial first began, fans would begin a single file line as early as 1 a.m. to secure a prime spot. Some even spent thousands of dollars in airfare and other expenses, traveling from around the country and world just to see Depp in person.<\/p>\n

Ivan De Boer, a 59-year-old superfan of Depp\u2019s, said she used a year\u2019s worth of paid vacation time to come from Los Angeles to the trial to support Depp, even spending upwards of $30,000 on expenses as of last week.<\/p>\n

\u201cI took my whole year\u2019s vacation, so I could be here for Johnny,\u201d she told People. \u201cI\u2019m the same age as Johnny. I\u2019m single, so I do what I want to, basically.\u201d<\/p>\n

Despite spending all that money and time off, she has \u201cno regrets,\u201d saying she\u2019d \u201cregret it more\u201d if she wasn\u2019t there.<\/p>\n
