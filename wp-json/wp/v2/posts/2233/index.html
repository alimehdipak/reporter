Kim Basinger got emotional while discussing her agoraphobia, opening up that her phobia of leaving home has caused her debilitating issues. <\/p>\n

The 68-year-old discussed her struggles with the disease on a forthcoming episode of \u201cRed Table Talk,\u201d revealing she forgot how to drive a car.<\/p>\n

In a clip acquired by People, Basinger and her daughter Ireland Baldwin stopped by Jada Pinkett-Smith\u2019s talk show where the \u201c9 1\/2 Weeks\u201d star spoke about the many challenges she faced while battling agoraphobia.<\/p>\n

She said it was \u201creally horrible\u201d to suffer from the condition \u201cas fiercely as I did during those years and not know what it was.\u201d<\/p>\n

\u201cI wouldn\u2019t leave the house. I would no longer go to dinner,\u201d Basinger said.<\/p>\n

Agoraphobia\u00a0is classified as a type of anxiety disorder that causes an extreme amount of fear of certain situations, with many people even resisting leaving home.<\/p>\n

Kim Basinger said she forgot how to drive while battling agoraphobia.Red Table Talk\/Facebook <\/p>\n

Basinger added that her agoraphobia felt \u201clike something just completely shuts down within you and you have to relearn everything.\u201d<\/p>\n

\u201cI had to relearn to drive, and for many years I would not go through the tunnels at Malibu,\u201d the \u201cL.A Confidential\u201d actress explained. \u201cEverything used to make me nervous, like the glass [sliding] to open the door, or, \u2018Where do I step to open the door?\u2019 Everything became a big job to figure out how to do it.\u201d<\/p>\n

\u201cYou live with a dry mouth all the time, you\u2019re very shaky, you\u2019re just so exhausted all the time,\u201d she went on.<\/p>\n

Ireland Baldwin said that she previously would \u201cself-medicate with Xanax\u201d and \u201cdrank excessively.\u201dRed Table Talk\/Facebook <\/p>\n

As for Baldwin, 26, she got candid about her own health issues, revealing that she previously would \u201cself-medicate with Xanax\u201d and \u201cdrank excessively.\u201d<\/p>\n

The model also admitted she \u201cdidn\u2019t talk to\u201d her mom or dad\u00a0Alec Baldwin\u00a0\u201cfor, like, a year\u201d because of her problems. \u201cI saw them here and there but I was so ashamed of what I had become and how I was living,\u201d she said.<\/p>\n

\u201cI just became this different person,\u201d she said. \u201cI was emaciated in every way; I was lifeless.\u201d<\/p>\n
