But it\u2019s already bringing up \u201cgenerational trauma\u201d for the late King of Rock \u2018n\u2019 Roll\u2019s daughter, Lisa Marie Presley, and her own daughter, Riley Keough. <\/p>\n

\u201cIt was such an emotional [experience],\u201d Lisa Marie said in a teaser for \u201cExclusively Elvis,\u201d a \u201c20\/20\u201d special airing at 10 p.m. Tuesday on ABC. <\/p>\n

\u201cLike Riley said, it just brings up such generational trauma \u2014 in a good way. In a good way,\u201d said Presley, 54, alongside Keough, 33. <\/p>\n

She also shared her wish for Butler, 30, to receive an Oscar for his performance next year. <\/p>\n
