Amazon introduced five of the new characters that will appear in its upcoming \u201cThe Lord of the Rings: The Rings of Power\u201d series. <\/p>\n

Thursday\u2019s announcement was accompanied by posters of each character\u2019s hands holding various objects, including apples, a scroll and a seemingly pregnant stomach. <\/p>\n

In February, the studio shared stunning portraits of the show\u2019s sexy stars with Vanity Fair, including an alluring portrait of Welsh beauty Morfydd Clark, 32.<\/p>\n

Clark will portray Galadriel \u2014 a character previously played by Cate Blanchett in Peter Jackson\u2019s \u201cThe Lord of The Rings\u201d trilogy.<\/p>\n

The prequel series is set to take place 1,000 years before the events of \u201cThe Lord of The Rings.\u201d <\/p>\n

However, there won\u2019t be many other crossover characters, with Amazon creating new, racially diverse roles in order to modernize J.R.R. Tolkien\u2019s Middle-earth.<\/p>\n
