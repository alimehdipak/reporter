An original \u201cWest Side Story\u201d star is ready to rumble for Rachel Zegler.<\/p>\n

The 20-year-old actress who played Maria in the revival film revealed Monday that she was not invited to the 2022 Oscars despite the Steven Spielberg-helmed musical scoring seven nods at the award show.<\/p>\n

But Russ Tamblyn \u2014 who played Riff in the 1961 adaption of the Broadway play \u2014 is not happy about the invite snub.<\/p>\n

\u201c@TheAcademy As a voting member and the original Riff, let me say: it\u2019s your duty to find Rachel a seat at the Oscars,\u201d Tamblyn, 87, tweeted on March 21.<\/p>\n

\u201cShe STARS in #Westsidestory which is nominated across the board. When they say representation matters, this is what that means. Please do right by her,\u201d he continued.<\/p>\n

Tamblyn tweeted his thoughts in response to a Los Angeles Times report regarding Zegler\u2019s snub.<\/p>\n

The singer replied, \u201cI\u2019m not invited so sweatpants and my boyfriend\u2019s flannel.\u201d<\/p>\n
