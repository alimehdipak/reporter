Living like Taylor Swift doesn\u2019t have to be one of your \u201cWildest Dreams.\u201d It\u2019s doable \u2014 and now, someone can call a piece of her history their very own.<\/p>\n

Before Swift, 32, moved to Nashville to pursue her music career, later shooting to mega-stardom, she lived in Berks County, Pennsylvania. Although she often sings about her 11-acre Christmas Tree Farm she grew up on in the town of Reading, she and her family later rented a five-bedroom, five-bathroom estate in the same town. <\/p>\n

In February, that five-bedroom home hit the market for $1.1 million. And after a $100,000 price cut, the property went into contract on Friday, The Post has learned \u2014 meaning it\u2019s about to enter its next chapter under new ownership.<\/p>\n

Swift lived in this Georgian Colonial home for three years.<\/p>\n

Pegged as \u201cone-of-a-kind,\u201d the home stands three stories tall and has been described as a structure of \u201cold-world charm,\u201d with modern updates throughout. <\/p>\n

Features include hardwood flooring, crown molding and a brick fireplace with a wooden mantel. <\/p>\n

The gourmet kitchen comes with a breakfast room and a solid marble-top island with floor-to-ceiling cabinets and access to a second staircase. The adjacent breakfast room has a butler\u2019s pantry with a wet bar, expansive windows, and sliding doors that lead to the front yard, the listing notes. <\/p>\n

On the second level is the primary suite with a luxury bathroom with a double vanity \u2014 and access to a private deck. <\/p>\n

The third floor features three additional bedrooms, all with angled\/vaulted ceilings, hardwood flooring and closets, while the fifth bedroom offers recreational room potential.<\/p>\n

As for outdoor amenities, there\u2019s a heated pool surrounded by a stone paver patio and a hot tub, with a fenced garden.<\/p>\n

Eric J. & Eric P. Miller of The Eric Miller Team at RE\/MAX of Reading held the listing. <\/p>\n

Away from Pennsylvania, Swift, 32, has since amassed a real estate portfolio worth $80 million. She currently owns homes in London, New York City, Rhode Island and Los Angeles.<\/p>\n

Swift purchased an entire block in the Tribeca neighborhood for $47.7 million, beginning in 2014. A year prior, she purchased a $17 million Rhode Island mansion. She also has a modest $1.9 million Nashville penthouse and a second estate in the Music City she bought shortly following her meteoric rise. <\/p>\n
