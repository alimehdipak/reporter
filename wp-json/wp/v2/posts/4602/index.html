<\/p>\n

They\u2019re putting their best suit forward.<\/p>\n

A new poll has indicated that almost half (49%) of men own two or three suits, while 33% own four or more.<\/p>\n

On behalf of\u00a0Generation Tux, OnePoll surveyed about 2,000 men about their ideal wedding wardrobes and most said they attend about five formal events a year.<\/p>\n

The average man owns three different tuxedos or suits, with two of them not even fitting anymore. While 16% noted that their suits and tuxedos fit them, almost 53% of all respondents undergo stress because the outfit they wanted to wear to a party no longer fits them.<\/p>\n

When asked which occasions are best for a man to wear a suit or tux, more than half (52%) said their own\u00a0wedding\u00a0(52%) while 50% said when invited as a guest to a\u00a0wedding.<\/p>\n

Twenty-seven percent want to be overdressed and 19% prefer to be underdressed for bashes.\u00a0<\/p>\n

\u201c[Events] are a great opportunity to show off a bit of your personal style with different suit colors, choosing a bow tie or necktie, fun accessories like socks and pocket squares, and everything in between,\u201d Ramirez said.Getty Images\/Purestock The average man owns three different tuxedos or suits, with two of them not even fitting anymore.Shutterstock <\/p>\n

The survey also reported that 25% of respondents think that bowties are fancier than regular neckties.<\/p>\n

Popular colors for suits include white (64%), black (15%), dark gray (13%) and navy (11%). But men should avoid bright hues like orange (35%) and yellow (34%).<\/p>\n

When it comes to color schemes, 63% of people agree that a wedding party should match their colors to each other.<\/p>\n

\u201cKnowing the difference between a tuxedo and a suit can be helpful when choosing the perfect attire for your event. According to the results, 58% correctly identified that a tuxedo usually includes shirt studs, cufflinks, typically worn with a bow tie, suspenders, and a vest,\u201d Matt Ramirez, SVP of Marketing at Generation Tux noted. <\/p>\n

Sixty-four percent prefer wearing white to formal events with 15% favoring black suits for weddings. <\/p>\n

\u201cAnd 21% were aware that matte shoes can be worn with a suit or tuxedo, but glossy shoes are commonly worn with a tuxedo. Another helpful distinguisher is that tuxedos have satin accents on the jacket lapel and striping down the side of the pants,\u201d he continued.<\/p>\n

The research also discovered that the average person needed 45 minutes to get ready for an event such as a\u00a0wedding\u00a0or charity ball.<\/p>\n
