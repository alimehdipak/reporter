This mini is having a major moment. <\/p>\n

Miu Miu\u2019s ultra-cropped, low-rise, pleated skirt has taken the fashion world by storm \u2014 with celebrities such as Nicole Kidman and Zendaya sporting it on magazine covers and social media devouring it.<\/p>\n

With barely enough fabric to peek out from under a belted waistband, the itty-bitty kilt is reminiscent of Britney Spears\u2019 sexy schoolgirl outfit in her 1998 \u201cBaby One More Time\u201d music video.<\/p>\n

Some have decried the look as tawdry, but demand is skyrocketing. Miu Miu has been sold out of the coveted item for weeks despite an eye-popping price tag: The skimpiest version of the khaki miniskirt costs $950, with a slightly longer version available for $1,150.<\/p>\n

Miu Miu first presented the micro-mini skirt during its spring 2022 fashion show last October. After the skirt was seen on nearly every red carpet, designer Miuccia Prada revamped the daring style for her fall 2022 collection, which just hit the Paris fashion week catwalk.<\/p>\n

Since its release, demand for miniskirts has hit a three-year high, according to Lyst. In the last week of February, there were 900 daily searches\u00a0for the iconic set \u2014 the mini can be paired with an array of ultra-cropped tops, which just about cover the bra line. <\/p>\n

Miuccia Prada first debuted the micro mini skirt set during the her spring 2022 show. Dominique Charriau Fashion Nova has made their own knockoff version of the Miu Miu micro miniskirt set.Fashion Nova <\/p>\n

The Miu Miu skirt set has become so popular it even has its own Instagram account, @miumiuset. Meanwhile, the luxury label\u2019s Soho location has a long wait list of fashionistas itching to get their hands on the coveted skirt. <\/p>\n

Shoppers not ready to drop nearly $1,000 or wait several weeks can head over to Fashion Nova for a knockoff priced at $50. <\/p>\n

While most of the fashion world seems obsessed with the hip-bone-baring thigh-high hem line, not everyone is a fan. <\/p>\n

The Miu Miu skirt set received a wave of backlash after Nicole Kidman wore a navy blue and white collegiate-inspired set on the cover of Vanity Fair for its 28th annual Hollywood Issue. <\/p>\n

<\/blockquote>\n

The 54-year-old actress showed off her long, lean legs and toned torso in the saucy style but some internet users were left \u201cconfused.\u201d <\/p>\n

While many said the blowback smacked of ageism, several comments pointed to a major critique of the skimpy outfit: It\u2019s made for a certain body type. <\/p>\n

The style seems to be riding the wave of Y2K nostalgia championed by Gen Z, though the micro miniskirt set makes Paris Hilton\u2019s dreaded low-slung jeans from the early aughts seem modest by comparison.<\/p>\n
