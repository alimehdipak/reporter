    Life is truly scrumptious.<\/p>\n

    Dick Van Dyke, 96, looked happier than ever as he left lunch with friends in Malibu last Thursday, telling well-wishers that he\u2019s \u201cjust glad to still be here.\u201d<\/p>\n

    The \u201cChitty Chitty Bang Bang\u201d legend was subsequently seen running errands after the lunch date, picking up a bottle of Neutrogena self-tanning spray from a local store. <\/p>\n

    The spritely star dressed casually for the outing, clad in a light gray sweater and navy pants. He added a pair of sensible slip-on shoes and a stylish leather shoulder bag. <\/p>\n

    Proving he\u2019s still sharp as a tack, Van Dyke got behind the wheel of his Lexus and drove himself home afterward.<\/p>\n

    The \u201cMary Poppins\u201d vet is occasionally seen around Malibu, grabbing lunch and hitting the gym with his wife, Arlene Silver, 50. <\/p>\n

    The couple celebrated their 10th wedding anniversary in February by\u00a0going viral with a romantic Valentine\u2019s Day video\u00a0\u2014 and are rarely seen without one another. <\/p>\n

    The age-defying actor is famous for keeping fit and healthy in spite of his advancing age, telling\u00a0Closer Weekly\u00a0that he still hits the gym three times a week.<\/p>\n

    \u201cI\u2019ve always been an exerciser and still am \u2026 I get in the pool and exercise. At my age, they say to keep moving. Put me on solid ground and I\u2019ll start tapping,\u201d he declared in 2019.<\/p>\n

    Van Dyke revealed he does water aerobics, lifts weights and walks on a treadmill. He also credited his fit physique to \u201cgood genes.\u201d<\/p>\n

    \u201cI sometimes forget that we\u2019re doing a great experiment here \u2014 46 years\u2019 difference. And we work at it to some extent,\u201d he explained. \u201cThere\u2019s got to be some understanding. Find out what old habits don\u2019t work anymore.     Van Dyke first met Silver at the 2007 Screen Actors Guild Awards. At the time, he was 81 and she was just 35.<\/p>\n

    Silver spoke with\u00a0Closer in a separate interview\u00a0earlier this year, recalling the moment she first laid eyes on her now-husband.<\/p>\n

    The \u201cChitty Chitty Bang Bang\u201d legend was seen running errands after his meal with friends, picking up a bottle of Neutrogena self-tanning spray from a local store. Coleman-Rayner <\/p>\n

    The devoted wife further cooed: \u201cHe is the most perfect human being. I\u2019ve never met anyone so happy, so genuine, so amazing. He\u2019s just like a happy pill.\u201d<\/p>\n
