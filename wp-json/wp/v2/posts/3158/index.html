Do you need egg to bind burgers?<\/p>\n

You can bind burgers without eggs using breadcrumbs. Breadcrumbs are great because they absorb moisture from the meat and help hold everything together. You can also use crushed crackers or corn flakes. Just mix the ingredients together until the mixture forms into a ball.<\/p>\n
