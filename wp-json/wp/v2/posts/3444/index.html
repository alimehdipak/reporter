How do you fit a wooden broom handle?<\/h3>\n

Before you fit a wooden handle to a broom, hammer or axe, first flatten all round the end of the handle with a hammer. That compresses the wood so the handle can be pushed deeper into the hole in the tool. The wood of the handle will later expand again by itself, making the handle a very tight fit.<\/p>\n
