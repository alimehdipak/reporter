Who is that in the corner of the bodega, lurking around the condiments and napkins by the deli counter? <\/p>\n

If you\u2019re in New York City, chances are it\u2019s a new member of the NYC Deli Association, here with a treat for you \u2014 and maybe a gentle nudge and nag, too. <\/p>\n

Jokes aside, the Old Jewish Men and New York Post are back again with a hot new \u201cdelicore\u201d merchandise collab you need to see. <\/p>\n

By way of tees, tote bags, hats and mugs, the new launch brings the fictional but necessary \u201cNYC Deli Association\u201d to life, in addition to sharing the headlines we wish were true and other gripes with the world, like carrying too much, having to go too far or the sudden switch to crypto (what is that, anyway?)<\/p>\n

However, this is no joke to us, nor those who value accessible and affordable pastrami, among other things. The merch lineup includes two limited-edition shirts with fully produced New York Post covers, in addition to tote bags, mugs, hoodies and a cap. Each feature the NYP and OJM logos, in addition to the brand new NYC Deli Association pickle logo, that we guess is a classic Kosher dill.<\/p>\n

New York Post Composite <\/p>\n

For those unfamiliar, OJM is a lifestyle brand created by Noah Rinsky and Bryan Seversky, which features images of elderly Jewish men in a variety of meme-able moments \u2014 most\u00a0recently demanding affordable lox prices and access to public restrooms \u2014 in addition to real life demonstrations and \u201cprotests\u201d to bring attention to their various causes.<\/p>\n

\u201cThe inspiration for this was an homage to New York Post and Page Six designs, and then celebrate this bygone era of deli culture,\u201d Seversky told The Post. <\/p>\n

\u201cThese guys have sort of become the New York City police in some ways, holding these places accountable for what they see as outrageous prices. Obviously there is a level of absurdity and comedy to it, but at its base truth, it\u2019s true,\u201d Rinsky added. <\/p>\n

The accounts now have over 55,000\u00a0Instagram\u00a0followers and 53,000\u00a0TikTok\u00a0followers along with a\u00a0merch store\u00a0where you can also shop this collection, plus prepare for Christmas with this topical tee from last season. <\/p>\n

Back to this new collection: Each item will be available on both the OJM Merch site as well as the New York Post Store. Some are also outlined below in greater detail with exclusive quotes from the OJM founders, most likely given on their lunch break \u2014 pockets full of condiment packets, we\u2019d imagine. <\/p>\n

\u201cThe NYC Deli Association, while it doesn\u2019t exist, we believe that it does,\u201d said Rinsky. \u201cIt\u2019s the Old Jewish Man in the sky keeping a watchful eye, making sure things are done right and correctly,\u201d added Seversky. <\/p>\n

Help protect the deli of the city with this stylish and silly cap, with an embroidered pickle logo front and center and the OJM and NYP logos on the back. <\/p>\n
