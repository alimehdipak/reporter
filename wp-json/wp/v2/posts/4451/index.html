Actor Pierce Brosnan has shared a poignant series of photos on Instagram showing a frail, wheelchair-bound James Caan before his death. <\/p>\n

\u201cOn the set of the movie \u2018Fast Charlie\u2019 with the great man himself James Cann [sic], farewell Jimmy,\u201d Brosnan captioned the carousel of photos he posted on Friday.<\/p>\n
