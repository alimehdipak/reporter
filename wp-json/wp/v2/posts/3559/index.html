Chaya Milchtein has created a safe online space for women and LGBTQ people in the world of cars and mechanics.<\/h3>\n

Purchasing and maintaining a car often involves moving in traditionally male-dominated spaces. But Chaya Milchtein wants everyone to feel comfortable both under the hood and behind the wheel. Chaya is an automotive educator and journalist who is dedicated to providing women and queer people with automotive education. Through her company Mechanic Shop Femme<\/a>, she teaches people about cars, speaks to people about cars, and creates content about cars for her website, social accounts, and major publications such as Parents<\/i>, Real Simple<\/i>, Via,<\/i> and others. Her goal is to equip her community with all the information they need to feel confident about their automotive decisions.
