Author JK Rowling unknowingly became the victim of a \u201cdistasteful\u201d prank carried out by two Russian men pretending to be Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelensky on a Zoom call.<\/p>\n

In the video, discovered Tuesday by\u00a0The Rowling Library, the \u201cHarry Potter\u201d author was fooled by Russian comedy duo Vovan and Lexus, who are known for prank-calling high-profile celebrities.<\/p>\n

The duo\u2019s past prank victims include Elton John, Boris Johnson, Kamala Harris, Prince Harry, Billie Eilish, Bernie Sanders, and George W. Bush \u2014 and it seems as though they can now add Rowling to their star-studded list.<\/p>\n

The multi-millionaire author \u2014 who seemingly didn\u2019t know she was being recorded \u2014 was under the impression that Zelensky called to thank her for her charity work in the country.<\/p>\n

Earlier this year, Rowling announced she would match donations up to $1.2 million after her Lumos Foundation launched an emergency appeal to help vulnerable children and families in Ukraine.<\/p>\n<\/p>\n

Fully thinking she was speaking to the president of the war-torn country, Rowling appeared invested in the conversation as the pranksters asked increasingly bizarre questions throughout the interview.\u00a0<\/p>\n

During the increasingly-awkward 12-minute call, the impersonators asked Rowling about her thoughts on imposing sanctions on Russian actor Aleksandr Kuznetsov, who starred in the most recent\u00a0\u201cFantastic Beasts\u201d film.<\/p>\n

\u201cFantastic Beasts\u00a0has been a very interesting experience. I\u2019ll certainly talk to people and see what we can do,\u201d Rowling said raising her eyebrows while taking copious notes.<\/p>\n

Zelensky attends a press conference on June 21.EPA <\/p>\n

The prankster duo, whose real names are Vladimir Kuznetsov and Alexey Stolyarov,\u00a0later pressured Rowling into answering whether or not Dumbledore was gay as they asked who the friendly giant had slept with.<\/p>\n

The multi-millionaire author was under the impression that Zelensky called to thank her for her charity work in Ukraine. <\/p>\n

\u201cHopefully not with a transgender,\u201d the duo said as Rowling continued to think it was Zelensky on the other line. <\/p>\n

\u201cI said in 2007 that I always saw Dumbledore as gay,\u201d she said in response.<\/p>\n

The two men later said they were writing a book on missiles titled \u201cAvada Kedavra\u201d \u2014 the infamous killing curse in the\u00a0wizard\u00a0franchise.<\/p>\n

The duo asked Rowling, \u201cBy the way, I ordered that our fighters write \u2018Avada Kedavra\u2019 on the missiles that we will launch in Donetsk and Luhansk, so how do you like this joke?\u201d<\/p>\n

\u201cI love the joke, I love the joke, I really do,\u201d she responded.<\/p>\n

Next, the pair told Rowling that Harry Potter\u2019s lightning bolt scar on his forehead closely resembles the Russian war symbol displayed on their military vehicles. They asked her whether it could be changed to the Ukrainian trident instead as an act of solidarity.<\/p>\n

\u201cI will look at that. It might be good for me to do something with that myself on social media because I think that will get into the newspapers,\u201d she responded.<\/p>\n

After an uncomfortable 12 minutes on the call, the prank was over. The duo turned their camera to introduce three elderly people wearing T-shirts that read, \u201cOnly Putin!\u201d in Russian.<\/p>\n
