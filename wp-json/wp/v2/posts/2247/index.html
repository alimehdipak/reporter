<\/p>\n

Romance was certainly in the air during the filming of the 1980 drama \u201cSomewhere in Time.\u201d<\/p>\n

Jane Seymour revealed that she \u201cfell madly in love,\u201d both on- and off-screen, with her co-star Christopher Reeve. While the 71-year-old had previously touched upon the strong friendship she had with Reeve in the past, she is just now getting candid about their love affair.<\/p>\n

\u201cWell, here comes the story that I\u2019m officially telling you now, because Chris and I, when we made the film, we literally fell madly in love,\u201d Seymour said at the\u00a0TCM Classic Film Festival over the weekend.<\/p>\n

While chatting with TCM host Alicia Malone, the \u201cWedding Crashers\u201d star got emotional talking about her relationship with the late \u201cSuperman\u201d actor while filming the time-travel movie.<\/p>\n

Reeve and Seymour in \u201cSomewhere in Time,\u201d 1980.Courtesy Everett Collection Seymour had a real-life romance with Reeve.Courtesy Everett Collection <\/p>\n

\u201cWhen you see this film, you will see the real thing,\u201d she said. \u201cBut we didn\u2019t let anyone know. So a few of the people who worked on the show kind of sussed it out, but we were as subtle as we could be about it.\u201d<\/p>\n

\u201cWe were madly in love and life was wonderful,\u201d Seymour continued. \u201cWe were both single; it was a fantastic, amazing experience.\u201d<\/p>\n<\/p>\n

She then noted that their relationship came to a halt at the same time she filmed a scene where her character, Elise, and Reeve\u2019s Richard lose each other after the latter wakes up in his own time period.<\/p>\n

She explained what went down that fateful day that led to their split: Seymour received the shocking news that Reeve was expecting a baby with his ex-girlfriend the same day the actors were to shoot the love scene as well as a breakup scene.<\/p>\n

\u201cWhen you see this film, you will see the real thing,\u201d the British actress said.\u00a9Universal\/Courtesy Everett Collection <\/p>\n

\u201cAnd then one day I came into work [to film] one of the biggest scenes in the movie\u2026\u00a0Just before that, Chris had had an earlier call and I came in about half an hour later, and they said, \u2018Chris needs to talk to you about something.\u2019 I thought, \u2018That\u2019s really odd, we\u2019ve had a long time to talk about things, so what could it be?’\u201d she recalled.<\/p>\n

\u201cIt was that he was about to have a baby, and that his ex-girlfriend hadn\u2019t told him, and that she\u2019d just announced it to the world,\u201d the \u201cFifty Shades of Black\u201d actress said.<\/p>\n

Seymour said he had \u201ctears coming halfway up my eyeballs\u201d while filming.Getty Images <\/p>\n

Reeve and his ex, Gae Exton, had a son named Matthew around the time of filming. The disability activist and the modeling executive stayed together until 1987 and also welcomed a daughter, Alexandra.<\/p>\n

Seymour joked that she had to \u201cput my big-girl pants on\u201d to shoot and had \u201ctears coming halfway up my eyeballs\u201d when she shot heartbreaking scenes.<\/p>\n

\u201cAnd I just kept saying, \u2018You can\u2019t cry, you can\u2019t cry, you can\u2019t cry, you\u2019re happy. Elise is really, really happy right now,’\u201d she said.<\/p>\n

Reeve and Gae Exton in 1980.Ron Galella Collection via Getty <\/p>\n

Reeve died in 2004 at the age of 52 after suffering from cardiac arrest. Seymour and the \u201cVillage of the Damned\u201d actor stayed good friends over the decades up until his passing. <\/p>\n

Her son Kristopher is even named after her late friend.<\/p>\n

\u201cThe good part of the story is that Chris went on to have these two beautiful children and we met one another on many occasions,\u201d Seymour said during the festival. \u201cWe remained really, really close friends, literally until the day he died. I have to believe that I will one day see him somewhere in time.\u201d<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
