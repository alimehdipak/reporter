<\/p>\n

She\u2019s one of the world\u2019s most popular virtual assistants, her robotic voice reverberating through millions of homes every day.<\/p>\n

But while the voice-controlled helper Alexa is more often than not a useful presence at home, she can get on your nerves, too.<\/p>\n

One of the biggest complaints made about Amazon\u2019s assistant \u2013 which is loaded into its Echo speaker range \u2013 is her volume.<\/p>\n

It makes using her at night or early in the morning a big issue, particularly if you\u2019ve got young kids.<\/p>\n

Tinkering with volume settings can be a pain, and even when you do that, Alexa\u2019s voice doesn\u2019t become any softer.<\/p>\n

Fortunately, you can make Alexa whisper or shorten her responses to ensure she doesn\u2019t make any unwanted disturbances.<\/p>\n

To do that, you\u2019ll need to activate \u201cWhisper Mode\u201d or \u201cBrief Mode.\u201d<\/p>\n

What is Alexa\u2019s Whisper Mode?<\/h2>\n

Your Echo speaker has a setting that, when activated, causes Alexa to speak more softly.<\/p>\n

It\u2019s called Whisper Mode, and it\u2019s designed to allow night-time Alexa conversations that won\u2019t wake up your entire house.<\/p>\n

You need to enable Whisper Mode manually, and then activate it whenever you wish to use it.<\/p>\n

Alexa can be enabled so that she only \u201cwhispers\u201d when activated.REUTERS\/Mike Blake <\/p>\n

How to enable Whisper Mode<\/h2>\n

First, you\u2019ll need to enable Whisper Mode in the Alexa app:<\/p>\n

    \n
  • Launch the app<\/li>\n
  • Go to Settings<\/li>\n
  • Then go to Alexa Account<\/li>\n
  • Next, click Alexa Voice Responses<\/li>\n
  • Then activate Whispered Responses<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    This simply makes it possible for Alexa to enter Whisper Mode, but you\u2019ll still need to activate it each time you want to use it.<\/p>\n

    To do this, simply say \u201cAlexa, turn on Whisper Mode\u201d \u2013 and she\u2019ll speak in a hushed voice.<\/p>\n

    This will continue until you say \u201cAlexa, turn off Whisper Mode.\u201d<\/p>\n

    When in Whisper Mode, Alexa will speak in a \u201chushed\u201d voice.AP\/Elaine Thompson <\/p>\n

    What is Alexa\u2019s Brief Mode?<\/h2>\n

    Another feature that reduces the amount of Alexa in your life is Brief Mode.<\/p>\n

    With Brief Mode, Alexa speaks less and may play a short sound instead of giving a voice response.<\/p>\n

    For instance, when Brief Mode is activated Alexa won\u2019t always say \u201cOK\u201d, she\u2019ll simply take care of the task at hand.<\/p>\n

    The mode is great for anyone who finds Alexa\u2019s responses frustrating or who wishes she\u2019d be a little quieter.<\/p>\n

    Alexa\u2019s settings can also be changed so her responses are more quick to the point.AP\/Elaine Thompson <\/p>\n

    How to enable Brief Mode<\/h2>\n
      \n
    • Open the Alexa app.<\/li>\n
    • Open More and select Settings.<\/li>\n
    • Select Voice Responses.<\/li>\n
    • Turn Brief Mode on or off.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n
