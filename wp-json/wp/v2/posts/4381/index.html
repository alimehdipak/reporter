These \u201cdemon dolls\u201d washing up on Texas beaches are giving John Oliver the creeps. <\/p>\n

In an online exclusive of his HBO show \u201cLast Week Tonight,\u201d the 45-year-old British American came in on his week off to discuss disturbing baby dolls randomly found along a 40-mile stretch in southern Texas. <\/p>\n

He played a video from a local news station that said 30 dolls have been found on the shore. <\/p>\n

\u201cWatch where you step because something might be already watching you,\u201d a local news reporter said in the clip. <\/p>\n

\u201cBurn them \u2014 burn them now,\u201d Oliver begged. \u201cI hate those dolls. I hate them so much.\u201d<\/p>\n

He went on to show close-ups of several of the dolls, many of which had their eyes seemingly gouged out or otherwise missing, only to be replaced with rocks, barnacles and other debris. <\/p>\n

\u201cLook at this one,\u201d Oliver said about a doll covered in green \u201cwar\u201d paint. \u201cLook at these blank eyes. Look at the skull and crossbones drawn on its chest. What does it want? What does it know?\u201d the highly agitated host asked. <\/p>\n<\/p>\n

Mission-Aransas Reserve <\/p>\n

According to a clip played by Oliver, Texans can buy the doll heads at an annual fundraiser for the Mission-Aransas Reserve, which helps rehabilitate sea turtles. <\/p>\n

But there was one that stood out. One doll head \u2014 with its mouth wide open \u2014 sold for $35 at the auction. Quickly deemed it a sex doll, Oliver demanded, \u201cThat sex doll head and whoever bought it needs to be thrown directly into the middle of the ocean!\u201d <\/p>\n

Oliver hypothesized several ways to get rid of the dolls \u2014 \u201cbreak some kind of a curse \u2026 or a special volcano we can throw them into\u201d \u2014 before pledging to take care of the spookiness himself. <\/p>\n

But, really: Can you blame John Oliver for freaking out over the beach dolls?YouTube \/ LastWeekTonight At the end of his rant, John Oliver offered a solution that could prevent the dolls from haunting Texas beachgoers again. Mission-Aransas Reserve He said he\u2019d give the Mission-Aransas Reserve $10,000 to help sea turtles. Mission-Aransas Reserve <\/p>\n

\u201cThis is my firm offer to you,\u201d he proposed to the turtle reserve. \u201cWe will reluctantly buy all of the remaining dolls that you have \u2014 normal or sex \u2014 with a $10,000 donation to the turtle and marine wildlife rehabilitation program.<\/p>\n

\u201cThat\u2019s right, we will do it, and then crucially \u2014 I promise \u2014 we will figure out the best way to destroy every last one of these pieces of s\u2013t for you, for all of you,\u201d Oliver promised beachgoers. <\/p>\n

A Mission-Aransas Reserve spokesperson told The Post that they sent the picture below to Oliver\u2019s team, letting him know that he\u2019s ready to ship them to the show. <\/p>\n

The Post also reached out to reps for Oliver for comment. <\/p>\n
