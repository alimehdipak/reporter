If the shoe fits \u2026 come celebrate Sandals\u2019 suite 16!<\/p>\n

Mind you, the festivities aren\u2019t for the all-inclusive resort chain\u2019s age (it\u2019s now a fortysomething in that department), but for the size of its ever-growing litter in the Caribbean and the Bahamas.<\/p>\n

Sandals Royal Cura\u00e7ao, which hard-opened on June 1 (a tad fashionably late, thank you very much supply chain), was born inside the island\u2019s Santa Barbara Estate where a Hyatt once dwelled (later, gator).<\/p>\n

This past weekend \u2014 graced by the likes of DJ Menasa, the Blue Man Group, sexy-phonist extraordinaire Nelson Braveheart and violinists\u00a0CJ Opus\u00a0and\u00a0Clari Montero \u2014 visitors and brass alike went Dutch to a tropical party thrown in its honor on the beachy alumnus of the now-dissolved Netherlands Antilles. <\/p>\n

Sitting pretty in the Caribbean\u2019s Deep South, SRC is the brand\u2019s first property to open in five years as part of its \u201c2.0\u201d expansion. <\/p>\n

Not to be all cringingly clickbait-y about things, but let\u2019s just go right ahead and unleash the listicle. Here\u2019s how to try on Sandals No. 16 in as many ways.<\/p>\n

Buy the numbers <\/h2>\n

The host boasts lots of coast. Sandals Royal Cura\u00e7ao <\/p>\n

Sandals\u2019 newest resort doesn\u2019t shy away from number flexing, offering 351 rooms and suites in 24 categories, occupying 44 waterfront acres of \u201cHyatt who?\u201d turf, and all of it sitting within 3,000 acres of the cedilla-flaunting island\u2019s famed Santa Barbara Estate. If you must, ugh, there\u2019s a 38,000-square-foot meeting and convention space, the largest on the island. <\/p>\n

Bon bini to Cura-chow <\/h2>\n

The fresh-faced place also has 11 dining ops (including three food trucks) and, more importantly, a baker\u2019s dozen of bars. Dushi bida<\/em>! <\/p>\n

In plane site<\/h2>\n

Dying for more digits? Here\u2019s a fun one: Royal Cura\u00e7ao is only 15 miles from the airport. There are no ride-sharing services on the island (they do, however, have plenty of taxis) so feel free to half-marathon it from Hato Int\u2019l. By the way, the island enjoys nonstop service from NYC, Charlotte and Miami.<\/p>\n

If you\u2019re a fellow travel geek, you\u2019ll be over the moon to learn that this fresh new Sandals is utilizing the stagecraft of award-winning \u201cfather of fashion week\u201d Stan Herman for all of its staffers\u2019 unis \u2014 from the concierges and restaurant crews, to the butlers and events managers and everyone in between. Think loads of pocket squares, sun-washed colors, ruffled shirts and custom \u201cS\u201d pins. We stan Stan. <\/p>\n

Table manners<\/h2>\n

SRC has a spa. It\u2019s called Red Lane. Never been touched by the Dutch? Book now, thank us later. <\/p>\n

Solar power<\/h2>\n

Sunrises are for chumps. SRC is a West-facing resort so magnificent sunsets up the yin-yang await.<\/p>\n

Mini me<\/h2>\n

Wheels of fortune: Cura\u00e7ao\u2019s roads have fun, very dirty sounding names like Jan Donker and Kaya Semikok. Ride them in style with a complimentary Beemer. Sandals Royal Cura\u00e7ao <\/p>\n

You rang? Guests staying in the Awa Seaside Butler Bungalows\u00a0and\u00a0Kurason Island Poolside Butler Bungalows will not only have said stewards, but also private pools and access to BMW Mini Coopers to mob around the island. German tourists? Not everyone\u2019s cup of tea. German engineering? Everyone\u2019s cuppa! <\/p>\n

Off-site\u2019s on point<\/h2>\n

Many Caribbean resorts in general try to hold you captive on their property for safety (and spending) purposes \u2014 and often for good reason; there can be quite a lot of unpleasantness out in them thar tropics. But SRC instead encourages you to take a feasting field trip, partnering with up to eight off-site restaurants as part of their \u201cIsland Inclusive\u201d program. Book seven nights in a butler suite and they\u2019ll gift you a $250 restaurant voucher to use out in the real world.<\/p>\n

Barkeep, get me a pool \u2014 and make it a double, with a splash of splash<\/h2>\n

We see your infinity and raise you an infinity, says the Dos Awa bi-level pool.Sandals Royal Cura\u00e7ao <\/p>\n

You don\u2019t need a degree in discrete mathematics to know that 2 infinities is > 1. Royal Cura\u00e7ao\u2019s bi-level infinity pool proves it, QED. <\/p>\n

Baller move<\/h2>\n

These fried meat-filled snacks are sphered, paired with beers, get used to it.VI-Images via Getty Images <\/p>\n

One word (although it sounds like a few more): Bitterballen. <\/p>\n

Plastic surgery<\/h2>\n

It\u2019s a net positive! Sandals Resorts International\u2019s philanthropic arm, the Sandals Foundation, is teaming up with the Netherlands\u2019 AFC Ajax team to launch Future Goals \u2014 <\/em>a program that turns fishing nets sourced from the ocean and recycled plastic waste into soccer goals for the kiddos.<\/p>\n

Attention span<\/h2>\n

Willemstad\u2019s floating bridge is very much a mover and a shaker \u2014 take that Golden Gate! AFP via Getty Images <\/p>\n

You\u2019re only seven miles from Willemstad which, sure, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site in its own right, but more awesomely has a funky pontoon bridge that swings open on a hinge when boats need to pass through. <\/p>\n

After three years, carnival is back, baby! <\/h2>\n

However, sadly, this year\u2019s post-quarantine comeback fest is already over, but there\u2019s always next year (one hopes!). If you don\u2019t come home from a Caribbean-based carnival with at least a mild concussion, you did it wrong. Trust me on this, I\u2019m a professional. <\/p>\n

PADI cake<\/h2>\n

Cura\u00e7ao has more than five dozen dive sites \u2014<\/em> take an easy-breezy 3-hour scuba course onsite at its Aqua Center and head under da sea, day of, for $120\/pp. Or, take it up a notch and go the $480\/pp fully certified PADI Open Water Diver (OWD) route, part of which you can \u201ce-learn\u201d ahead of the trip in advance. <\/p>\n

Adult swim<\/h2>\n

Because of a certain ruling by a certain highest court in our land, Sandals staying true to its cherished adults-only policy at Royal Cura\u00e7ao has never been more timely. <\/p>\n
