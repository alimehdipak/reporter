A Brazilian beauty queen has died at 27 after suffering a brain hemorrhage and a heart attack following a routine operation to have her tonsils removed. <\/p>\n

Gleycy Correia \u2014 who was crowned Miss United Continents Brazil in 2018 \u2014 passed away Monday at a hospital in the Brazilian city of Maca\u00e9 more than two months after the simple procedure. Her body has been sent to a local medical examiner for an autopsy. <\/p>\n

\u201cSince then she was in a coma, with no neurological activity, and today she passed away,\u201d the priest heartbreakingly stated. <\/p>\n
