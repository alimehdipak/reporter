With this year’s Sweet 16, we’ve decided to have some fun. For every team that’s still alive in the 2022 NCAA tournament, we picked an all-time starting five — and we ranked them.<\/p>\n

This list is solely based on collegiate production. The goal was to create a traditional lineup that features guards and big men. We couldn’t pull that off with every school. Bill Walton and Kareem Abdul-Jabbar have to be paired together on UCLA’s all-time team, right?<\/p>\n

We also admit that there are a lot of players who could have cracked these lists. But when you limit yourself to five players, things get more difficult and probably more controversial.<\/p>\n

That didn’t stop us from trying.<\/p>\n

Here is our list of the all-time starting fives — and the coaches to lead them — for this season’s Sweet 16 teams, and where we believe they should be ranked.<\/p>\n
