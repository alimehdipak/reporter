While the 20-year-old superstar\u2019s regimen includes plenty of pricey potions \u2014\u00a0like Laura Mercier Body Cr\u00e8me ($72) and Biba de Sousa Cream Barrier ($95) \u2014\u00a0she calls Aquaphor Healing Ointment ($5) the \u201clove of my life.\u201d<\/p>\n

\u201cI have [it] with me at all times, 24\/7,\u201d Eilish told Vogue.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Aquaphor Healing Ointment ($5)<\/p>\n
