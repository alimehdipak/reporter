A popular influencer from the UK has gone viral with a TikTok video showing him giving his father a full drag queen makeover.<\/p>\n

Alex Ullah, 23, transformed his 54-year-old dad, Joe Ullah, with a \u201cEuphoria\u201d-inspired makeup look in the now-viral video. <\/p>\n

\u201cHe was so shocked at his transformation at first, but I knew he secretly liked it,\u201d Alex told Ark Media. <\/p>\n

In the video, Joe sits in a chair stone-faced as his son completely alters his appearance. <\/p>\n

He starts by blocking out his eyebrows and stubble, before applying glamorous makeup with added gems and sparkles. <\/p>\n

The look took Alex, who has been a makeup artist for seven years, an hour and 15 minutes to complete, showcased in the 45-second TikTok video, which has 3.4 million views and 732,000 comments.<\/p>\n

A dad is seen getting transformed into a \u201cEuphoria\u201d-inspired drag queen by his son in a viral TikTok video. @thechaneldior via ARK Media <\/p>\n

\u201cI was proud of how it turned out \u2014 it\u2019s easier to do good makeup on someone who already has pretty features but when it\u2019s an older man\u2019s face, it\u2019s much more hard to soften and make everything feminine!\u201d Alex shared.<\/p>\n

Fans were shocked by the transformation, but this wasn\u2019t the first time Alex has turned Joe into an unrecognizable queen.<\/p>\n

The TikTok video has 3.4 million views and 732,000 comments.@thechaneldior via ARK Media Alex has been a makeup artist for seven years and was inspired by the HBO Max series \u201cEuphoria.\u201d@thechaneldior via ARK Media <\/p>\n

\u201cI put my dad in drag for the first time in 2020 and that video went viral, reaching over 3 million views, so I put him into drag again inspired by \u2018Euphoria,\u2019 as I\u2019m obsessed with that show,\u201d Alex said. <\/p>\n

\u201cHe\u2019s very supportive of my career and doing his makeup was such a good laugh. Everyone was so supportive, and they enjoyed the comedy of it all.\u201d<\/p>\n

Alex began performing drag in 2016 after practicing his impressive makeup skills and now performs as Chanel Dior.<\/p>\n

\u201cIt\u2019s easier to do good makeup on someone who already has pretty features but when it\u2019s an older man\u2019s face, it\u2019s much more hard to soften and make everything feminine,\u201d Alex said of transforming his dad, Joe Ullah (above).@thechaneldior via ARK Media <\/p>\n

\u201cDrag is such a transformative process where it changes everything about your original face and can create a whole new person, so when someone who has spent their life looking a certain way [gets a makeover], it\u2019s a fun shock to see something fun and new,\u201d Alex said. <\/p>\n

Joe isn\u2019t the only family member whom Alex has shocked with his makeup skills. Alex also shared a TikTok video transforming his 74-year-old grandpa \u201cfrom Charlie to Charlotte.\u201d <\/p>\n
