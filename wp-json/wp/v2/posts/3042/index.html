A \u201cJeopardy!\u201d spinoff will be arriving soon on ABC.<\/p>\n

\u201cCelebrity Jeopardy!\u201d is set to air during the fall 2022 to 2023 TV lineup, broadcasting on Sundays at 8 p.m. ET.<\/p>\n

The show will give famous faces the chance to score money for a charity of their choice, similar to star-studded game shows like \u201cCelebrity Wheel of Fortune\u201d and \u201cCelebrity Family Feud.\u201d But who will host this trivia VIP extravaganza?<\/p>\n

Craig Erwich, president of Hulu Originals & ABC Entertainment, told the Wrap on Tuesday that a host has yet to be announced and \u201cwe\u2019ll have details soon.\u201d<\/p>\n

However, Erwich seemingly alluded to who will be the host in an interview with Variety, which asked if frequent \u201cJeopardy!\u201d host Mayim Bialik is the front-runner to emcee the spinoff.<\/p>\n

\u201cIt\u2019s really just about the details of the announcement,\u201d he said. \u201cAnd we just are not ready yet, but we will be soon. I wouldn\u2019t read anything into it.\u201d<\/p>\n

Bialik, 46, and Ken Jennings, 47, have taken on hosting duties for \u201cJeopardy!\u201d following the\u00a0ousting\u00a0of the show\u2019s former executive producer, Mike Richards.<\/p>\n

Bialik could be a possible front-runner to host the new spinoff for ABC.ABC via Getty Images <\/p>\n

Former game-show champ Mattea Roach\u00a0gave her take on who should be the next permanent host for the long-running game show.<\/p>\n

\u201cI would say Ken because of his history with the show,\u201d the 23-year-old contestant from Toronto, Canada,\u00a0told Vulture\u00a0earlier this month of her pick to replace longtime host Alex Trebek,\u00a0who died in 2020.<\/p>\n

Roach continued, \u201cAs a contestant, there\u2019s something really special about being onstage with the greatest player of all time. Someone who understands in a very visceral way what it\u2019s like to be in your position.\u00a0Mayim is fantastic, but she doesn\u2019t have that same experience.\u201d<\/p>\n
