A Texas recruiter has come under fire for using Johnny Depp and Amber Heard\u2019s defamation trial to stimulate interest in his LinkedIn job posting. <\/p>\n

Shawn Barnes, who works as a recruiter at GTN Technical Staffing and Consulting, crafted a fake post-trial \u201ctranscript\u201d between the two actors that he posted to the site Tuesday to advertise a data analyst role. <\/p>\n

\u201cBOMBSHELL,\u201d Barnes wrote. \u201cThe conversation between Johnny Depp and Amber Heard had at the end of the trial has been leaked!\u201d<\/p>\n

The recruiter then imagined Heard repeatedly asking Depp to look at her, before she asked: \u201cDo you still love me, Johnny?\u201d<\/p>\n

In the faux transcript, Depp replied: \u201cAmber, this might be the last time we ever speak, so please listen to me very carefully. If you know of a Data Analyst with experience in Python, SQL, A\/B testing and Excel, you absolutely need to call Shawn Barnes with GTN Technical Staffing. He is the best!\u201d<\/p>\n
