The husband of an ex-\u201cReal Housewife\u201d used her fame to bamboozle a marijuana grower into an investment deal \u2014 and sent the enterprise up in smoke when his private-equity fund didn\u2019t cough up the money, according to court papers.<\/p>\n

The fund \u2014 Gateway Privileged Fund LLC \u2014 is controlled by Reid Drescher, husband of RHONY Aviva and cousin of Fran Drescher, star of \u201cThe Nanny.\u201d<\/p>\n

Reid Drescher claimed to have access to gobs of cash to invest in Los Angeles-based marijuana grower P&S Ventures LLC, according to P&S\u2019s suit, filed Monday in Manhattan Supreme Court.<\/p>\n

\u201cDrescher bragged that he was a cousin of Fran Drescher, who played The Nanny in the television series of that name, and that his wife, Aviva Drescher, was on Bravo\u2019s Real Housewives of New York, claiming to have many celebrity contacts as a result,\u201d the suit said.<\/p>\n

Aviva Drescher hasn\u2019t been on the show since 2014.Getty Images for Hamptons Magazine <\/p>\n

Aviva Drescher \u2014 famous for throwing her prosthetic leg at another housewife onscreen \u2014 was cut from the hit show in 2014. <\/p>\n

Believing his \u201cnumerous materially false representations,\u201d P&S agreed to accept a $5.4 million investment from Drescher\u2019s fund in exchange for stock.<\/p>\n

But Reid Drescher was soon \u201cforced\u201d to admit that his fund didn\u2019t have the promised money, the suit said.<\/p>\n

Reid Drescher was soon \u201cforced\u201d to admit that his fund didn\u2019t have the promised money.Alamy Stock Photo <\/p>\n

The fund had raised just $1 million of a planned $120 million as of 2019, according to its most recent securities filing.<\/p>\n

Gateway ultimately only kicked $1.35 million into P&S. The shortfall was \u201chighly destructive\u201d to P&S, the suit said and drove it into a \u201ccostly and damaging receivership,\u201d the suit said.<\/p>\n

P&S is seeking compensatory and punitive damages, and asking for its contract with Gateway to be canceled. An attorney for P&S, Matthew Press, declined to comment. <\/p>\n

An attorney representing Drescher and Gateway said P&S\u2019s lawsuit was \u201ca frivolous contract claim.\u201d<\/p>\n

\u201cThe entity that is being sued will be filing some very serious counterclaims,\u201d Richard Roth, attorney for Drescher and Gateway, said in an interview. \u201cWe\u2019re going to file a massive counterclaim here.
