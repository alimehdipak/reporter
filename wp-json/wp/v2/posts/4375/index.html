Gina Rodriguez has nothing but love and admiration for her body.<\/p>\n

The 37-year-old actress got candid about her battle with her body image over the course of her career in Hollywood.<\/p>\n

Rodriguez recently chatted with Rachel Bilson and Olivia Allen on their podcast, \u201cBroad Ideas,\u201d namely about her battle with Hashimoto\u2019s disease and why she practices self-love.<\/p>\n

The \u201cJane the Virgin\u201d alum noted how her autoimmune disease \u2014 a disorder that affects one\u2019s thyroid and leads to hormonal changes \u2014 influenced her physique.<\/p>\n

\u201cI got an actor\u2019s curse because it directly affects your metabolism, you know, you\u2019re not producing the hormone that your body needs,\u201d she said. <\/p>\n

Rodriguez continued, \u201cSo you, at times, it can feel like you have a loss of communication with this thing, that spacesuit that you\u2019re in, and you\u2019re just like, \u2018Why don\u2019t I have a grasp on shifting what this physical being is?’\u201d<\/p>\n
