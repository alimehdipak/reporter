What is the feasibility?<\/p>\n

What does feasible mean in business?<\/p>\n

Feasible is the extent of which something can be done, and not only done, but conveniently done. In business, convenience speaks to the cost of getting that thing done, if those costs can be recovered and how soon they can be recovered after completion. A feasibility study is the measure.<\/p>\n
