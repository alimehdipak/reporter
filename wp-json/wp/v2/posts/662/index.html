    Colby Covington scored a lopsided unanimous decision over Jorge Masvidal when they met earlier this month at UFC 272, but apparently they have unfinished business.<\/p>\n

    The UFC stars were allegedly involved in an incident outside the Miami Beach hotspot Papi Steak on Monday around 11:30 p.m., according to TMZ, who obtained video of police at the scene.<\/p>\n

    A Miami Beach Police Information Officer didn’t respond to messages seeking comment.<\/p>\n

      “He’s over here swinging, trying to come at me, and I ran,” Covington is overheard saying in the video. ” … How would he know I’m here?”<\/p>\n

      “You talk that s—, you gotta back it up,” Masvidal said in a video he tweeted and in which he tagged Covington. “That’s how my city rolls, man.”<\/p>\n

      Masvidal, 37, is a native of Miami, while Covington now resides in the city. The former friends-turned-enemies exchanged heated words in the lead-up to their UFC bout, which was named Fight of the Night.<\/p>\n

      The defeat was Masvidal’s third consecutive, including two losses to Kamaru Usman, in vies for the UFC welterweight championship.<\/p>\n
