The cast of \u201cSeinfeld\u201d paid poignant tribute to its two beloved moms, following the back-to-back deaths of Liz Sheridan and Estelle Harris.<\/p>\n

Sheridan, famed for playing Jerry Seinfeld\u2019s sitcom mom, died at the age of 93, just over a week after Estelle Harris\u2019s death earlier this month.<\/p>\n

Leading tributes, Seinfeld took to Twitter to share some heartfelt words about his TV mom.<\/p>\n

\u201cLiz was always the sweetest, nicest TV mom a son could wish for,\u201d he wrote alongside a picture of the pair.<\/p>\n

\u201cEvery time she came on our show it was the coziest feeling for me. So lucky to have known her.\u201d<\/p>\n

Jason Alexander, who played George Costanza in the hit sitcom, remembered his own TV mom and wrote: \u201cOne of my favorite people has passed \u2013 my tv mama, Estelle Harris.<\/p>\n

\u201cThe joy of playing with her and relishing her glorious laughter was a treat. I adore you, Estelle. Love to your family. Serenity now and always.\u201d<\/p>\n

In a later tweet, Alexander thanked fans for sending their love and memories. <\/p>\n

\u201cShe would be so touched,\u201d he wrote. \u201cAs am I. And as she would say, \u2018WHY CANT YOU BE MORE LIKE LLOYD BRAUN!!??\u2019\u201d Lloyd, of course, was George\u2019s archrival on \u201cSeinfeld\u201d.<\/p>\n

Julia Louis-Dreyfus,\u00a0who played Elaine, also took to Twitter to remember the late comic icon. <\/p>\n

\u201cRIP Estelle Harris. I love you. #serenitynow,\u201d\u00a0she wrote in a tweet.<\/p>\n

Sheridan starred as overprotective mom Helen Seinfeld in 21 episodes of \u201cSeinfeld\u201d from 1990 to 1998. The star died from natural causes around 2:30 a.m. on Friday,\u00a0TMZ\u00a0reports.<\/p>\n

She is the only \u201cSeinfeld\u201d star \u2014 apart from the four main leads \u2014 to be featured in all nine seasons of the hit sitcom.<\/p>\n

Just two weeks prior, Harris died on April 2 of natural causes.<\/p>\n
