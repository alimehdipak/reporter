For just $1 per night, you can stay inside the famed Moulin Rouge windmill \u2014\u00a0which will be available to rent for the first time in history. <\/p>\n

Located on the edge of the tourist-favorite Montmartre neighborhood near Pigalle, the cabaret (whose name in English translates to Red Mill) is considered the home of the French can-can \u2014\u00a0a number that became a popular music-hall dance in the 1840s.<\/p>\n

According to the Airbnb listing, \u201cguests will experience a peek behind the velvet curtains of the fabled landmark, including backstage access to the theater, a traditional French three-course meal, the best seats during the Moulin Rouge\u2019s acclaimed show F\u00e9erie and an overnight stay inside the iconic windmill.\u201d <\/p>\n

But if the crazy low price of just a buck to stay in one of the world\u2019s most iconic properties sounds too good to be true that\u2019s because it is. Only three individual one-night stays on June 13, 20 and 27 are available and potential guests can beginning requestng a spot on Tuesday, May 17.<\/p>\n

Those who get one will be in for a treat. Airbnb worked with the 19th-century French historian, Jean-Claude Yon, who took a month to renovate the windmill to exactly as it would have been, according to a press release. <\/p>\n

It even comes with a secret room, which \u201cbrings to life the art and romance of the Belle \u00c9poque era for an experience through time.\u201d <\/p>\n

The windmill is now available on Airbnb for $1 per night. Daniel Alexander Harris\/Newsflash The bedroom. Daniel Alexander Harris\/Newsflash <\/p>\n

\u201cThe Belle \u00c9poque era was a time when French culture and arts flourished \u2013 and no landmark is more iconic to that period than the Moulin Rouge,\u201d Yon, who specializes in French cultural and social history, said in the release. \u201cThis secret room inside the cabaret\u2019s famous windmill has been designed to take you on an authentic journey back in time to experience the French capital of arts and pleasures during a moment in history.\u201d<\/p>\n

Features include sexy boudoir stuffed with art nouveau design; a dressing area that includes vintage costumes, fragrant perfumes and letters from admirers; and a private rooftop terrace adorned with an ornate pagoda and garden.<\/p>\n
