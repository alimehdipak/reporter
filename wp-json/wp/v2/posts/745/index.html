No, this is not something from a Harry Potter movie.<\/p>\n

A group of British nerds claim to have created a mind-blowing\u00a0invisibility\u00a0shield to \u201csurprise friends\u201d and \u201cavoid enemies\u201d.<\/p>\n

The shield is essentially a flat panel that magically hides whoever ducks behind it.<\/p>\n

Even more impressive is the fact the device doesn\u2019t need any power.<\/p>\n

It cleverly works by deflecting light around people using a \u201cprecision engineered lens array,\u201d creators Invisibility Shield Co say.<\/p>\n

But it\u2019s not completely perfect \u2014 you don\u2019t get a clear picture of the scene behind the subject, instead it creates more of a blur.<\/p>\n

So far, they\u2019ve made 25 fully functional invisibility shields.<\/p>\n

But now the team are crowdfunding on\u00a0Kickstarter\u00a0to turn their invention from \u201cscience fiction into reality\u201d.<\/p>\n

Their goal of raising $6,600 has already been passed, with more than $10,100 pledged so far.<\/p>\n

Those who offer $395 can get hold of a full-size shield measuring 37.4in x 25.5in, and should receive it by the end of the year.<\/p>\n

The shield can almost completely replicate the background, making it only a little blurry. Invisibility Shield Co. via Kickstarter Chart shows the science behind the \u201cinvisibility shield.\u201dInvisibility Shield Co. via Kickstarter Women hide partially behind an \u201cinvisibility shield\u201d on the beach. Invisibility Shield Co. via Kickstarter <\/p>\n

A smaller piece ideal for hiding everyday items is available from $65.<\/p>\n

Inventors have also said that the clothes you wear can impact the shield\u2019s effectiveness.<\/p>\n

\u201cThe shield performs at its absolute best when the clothing you are wearing is the same color and slightly lighter or equal to the brightness of the background scenery,\u201d they explain.<\/p>\n

\u201cThe shield still works very well with dark and differently colored clothing against a light background, but this combination makes the face of the shield slightly darker overall relative to the background, than it would be if the holder were wearing light clothing.\u201d<\/p>\n
