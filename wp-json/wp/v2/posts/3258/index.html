The film \u2014 starring Kristen Stewart, L\u00e9a Seydoux and Viggo Mortensen \u2014 is filled with scenes of child autopsies, bloody intestines, body mutations and people orgasming while licking open wounds.\u00a0 <\/p>\n

The majority of the exits reportedly occurred within the first five minutes of the film but a specifically grotesque scene of Seydoux licking an open wound sent others out the door further along in the film. Both Variety and the Daily Mail reported walkouts, but Entertainment Weekly claimed there were none.<\/p>\n

New York Times journalist Kyle Buchanan tweeted from the theater that he counted 15 people who walked out of the cinema during the screening due to \u201cnotably gross plot developments.\u201d <\/p>\n

Despite being too much for some, the movie directed by David Cronenberg received a\u00a0seven-minute standing ovation from the remaining audience members at the end. <\/p>\n
