Nighttime driving<\/p>\n

Even where lower speed limits are not established, you must drive at a reduced speed at night. In darkness, your ability to see hazards on the roadway ahead is significantly hindered. Unless otherwise stated, motorists should reduce their speed by 5 to 15 mph when driving in the dark.<\/p>\n

On a road with one lane in each direction, going 10-mph is legal, but can impede the flow of traffic. If it\u2019s safe to do so, drive closer to the max speed limit.<\/p>\n
