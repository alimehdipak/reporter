\u201cAbbott Elementary\u201d creator Quinta Brunson and ABC are being sued for copyright infringement for the Emmy-nominated series. <\/p>\n

According to court documents obtained by The Post, Christine Davis\u00a0filed the suit, claiming that the hit show is a \u201cveritable knock-off\u201d of her show called\u00a0\u201cThis School Year.\u201d<\/p>\n

Filed July 12, Davis alleges that she wrote her script \u2014 based in a New York City public school \u2014 in 2018 and registered with the copyright office in early 2020. Brunson\u2019s creation premiered in 2021. <\/p>\n

Davis also claims that the show mirrors the \u201clook and feel of the inner-city school, the mockumentary style, unique plot synopsis, set design and unique characters.\u201d <\/p>\n

Additionally, she says many of the characters are \u201cnearly identical\u201d and claims the first few episodes have similar plotlines. <\/p>\n

The suit alleges that both shows \u201clook and feel of the inner-city school, the mockumentary style, unique plot synopsis, set design and unique characters.\u201d ABC <\/p>\n

In Davis\u2019 project, the school\u2019s principal hires filmmakers to make a documentary following how everything is going according to plan. But the teachers and student body have their own perceptions of the school.<\/p>\n

The main character of \u201cThis School Year,\u201d Ms. David, is a \u201cyoung, idealistic teacher hoping to get tenure but also trying to convince everyone that the school needs to be reformed.\u201d<\/p>\n

\u201cAbbott Elementary\u201d creator Brunson stars as the lead character, Janine Teagues, who teaches at an underfunded public school in Philadelphia. Surrounded by enthusiastic and caring colleagues, their principal can be a little tone deaf. <\/p>\n

Davis also claims that both series have similar plotlines in the first few episodes. ABC <\/p>\n

\u201cWithout [Davis\u2019] permission, license, authority, or consent, [Brunson & ABC] knowingly and illegally used [Davis\u2019] works to create the\u00a0\u2018Abbot[t] Elementary\u2019\u00a0television show,\u201d according to the suit.<\/p>\n

Davis claims that she had three meetings with Sullivan Wright and Cherisse Parks at Blue Parks Productions in Los Angeles in mid-June and July, and they then reportedly showed \u201cThe School Year\u201d script to Hulu. \u201cAbbott Elementary\u201d started filming a few months later.<\/p>\n

Neither producer is connected to the ABC series.\u00a0<\/p>\n
