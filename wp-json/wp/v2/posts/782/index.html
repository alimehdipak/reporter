<\/p>\n

Federal prosecutors came out firing in the fraud trial of former Theranos executive Ramesh \u201cSunny\u201d Balwani on Tuesday \u2013 alleging he and ex-lover Elizabeth Holmes worked closely together in a scheme to mislead investors about the blood-testing startup\u2019s business.<\/p>\n

Federal prosecutor Robert Leach argued that Balwani and Holmes \u201cwere partners in everything, including their crimes\u201d as both legal teams delivered their opening statements in the long-awaited trial. <\/p>\n

Balwani, Theranos\u2019 former chief operating officer, is charged with 12 counts of fraud in connect to his alleged misdeeds while working for the doomed medical tech startup.<\/p>\n

\u201cThe defendant and Holmes knew the rosy falsehoods that they were telling investors were contrary to the reality within Theranos,\u201d Leach said, according to The Mercury News.<\/p>\n

Balwani, 56, faces trial roughly two months after Holmes was found guilty of\u00a0three counts of wire fraud and one count of conspiracy to commit fraud. Prosecutors portrayed Balwani as an active participant who defrauded investors and placed patients at risk through falsified information.<\/p>\n

The defense argued that Elizabeth Holmes, not Sunny Balwani, was in control of Theranos.REUTERS <\/p>\n

Leach argued Balwani had oversight over Theranos\u2019 laboratory and pushed claims that \u201cskewed the medical decisions patients were making and put them at risk.\u201d<\/p>\n

Theranos once achieved a $9 billion valuation\u00a0while claiming it developed a proprietary device that could perform dozens of tests with just a few drops of a patients\u2019 blood. But the startup imploded after a series of damning news reports revealed that its devices produced inaccurate results and could not perform the functions that Holmes claimed.<\/p>\n

The stunning downfall sparked a raft of media attention, including a Hulu series called \u201cThe Dropout\u201d detailing Holmes\u2019 life \u2013 and relationship with Balwani \u2013 as well as an upcoming film starring actress Jennifer Lawrence.<\/p>\n

Former Theranos executive Sunny Balwani faces a dozen counts of fraud.Getty Images <\/p>\n

Holmes\u2019 defense team laid some of the blame on Balwani during her trial, arguing he was abusive and manipulative toward the Theranos founder during their relationship.<\/p>\n

Balwani\u2019s defense lawyer Steve Cazares took aim that his client\u2019s portrayal in the media, arguing in his opening statement that \u201cthe headlines and the sensational stories about Theranos and Elizabeth Holmes have no place in this trial.\u201d<\/p>\n

Holmes tearfully recounted the dark side of her relationship with Balwani during her own trial. During her testimony, she alleged that Balwani controlled nearly every aspect of her life during their relationship \u2013 including what she ate and how she spoke.<\/p>\n

Holmes also claimed that Balwani was sexually abusive toward her during their relationship \u2013 allegations that he has denied.<\/p>\n

\u201cHe would force me to have sex with him when I didn\u2019t want to because he would say that he wanted me to know he still loved me,\u201d Holmes said on the stand.<\/p>\n

Cazares noted that Balwani joined Theranos in 2009 \u2013 long after Holmes founded the startup \u2013 and was a key early investor who never sold his shares. The defense attorney telegraphed his defense for claims of fraud, arguing that figures provided to investors were meant to be revenue forecasts rather than firm projections.<\/p>\n

Disgraced Theranos founder Elizabeth Holmes faces sentencing later this year.Getty Images <\/p>\n

The defense also argued that Holmes, not Balwani, was the key driver and source of information on Theranos.<\/p>\n

\u201cSunny Balwani did not start Theranos, he did not control Theranos,\u201d Cazares said. \u201cElizabeth Holmes, not Sunny, founded Theranos and built Theranos.\u201d<\/p>\n

Balwani faces up to 20 years in prison for each count of fraud, but he is not expected to receive a maximum sentence if found guilty. The trial is expected to last about two months.<\/p>\n
