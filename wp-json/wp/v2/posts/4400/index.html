Prince George put on a spirited display during the Wimbledon men\u2019s singles finals Sunday as he sat and watched between parents Prince William and Kate Middleton.<\/p>\n

The 8-year-old, and oldest child of the royal couple, was animated but also appeared uncomfortable at times as temperatures reached the high 80s during the match between Novak Djokovic and Nick Kyrgios.<\/p>\n

He could be seen rubbing his brow and eyes on occasion while sitting in a blue suit and tie. His parents leaned in to talk to George occasionally as they watched the action at Centre Court.<\/p>\n

The youngster was also seen flashing smiles during the match in which Djokovic topped Kyrgios in four sets to take his fourth consecutive Wimbledon tourney.<\/p>\n

It was George\u2019s first ever Wimbledon final in the royal box, according to multiple reports. Younger siblings Charlotte, 7, and Louis, 4, were not in attendance.<\/p>\n

George has an affinity for the sport, once training with all-time great Roger Federer, according to a report.<\/p>\n
