Jada Pinkett Smith\u2019s \u201cRed Table Talk\u201d has returned for its fifth season \u2014 and it referenced Will Smith\u2019s Oscars slap right off the bat. <\/p>\n

\u201cConsidering all that has happened in the last few weeks, the Smith family has been focused on deep healing,\u201d a title card stated at the beginning of the episode, which was released on Facebook Watch on Wednesday. <\/p>\n

\u201cSome of the discoveries around our healing will be shared at the table when the time calls,\u201d the message further read. \u201cUntil then the table will continue offering itself to powerful, inspiring and healing testimonies like that of our incredibly impressive first guest.\u201d<\/p>\n

The episode then cut to a pre-taped segment featuring Pinkett Smith, her daughter, Willow Smith, and her mother, Adrienne Banfield Norris.<\/p>\n

The episode was purportedly taped before the Oscars debacle and included special guest Janelle Mon\u00e1e. <\/p>\n

The show made no further reference to Smith\u2019s assault on actor Chris Rock, which took place at the Oscars on March 27. <\/p>\n

\u201cKeep my wife\u2019s name out of your f\u2013king mouth!\u201d Smith then shouted \u2014 twice \u2014 after returning to his seat.<\/p>\n

A visibly stunned Rock continued with his presentation before leaving the stage. Smith won the Best Actor Oscar a short time later for his role in \u201cKing Richard.\u201d<\/p>\n
