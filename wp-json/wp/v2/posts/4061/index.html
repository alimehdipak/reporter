As the first night of major performances began at Glastonbury Festival, some of the biggest female acts in music addressed the Supreme Court\u2019s overturning of Roe v. Wade, which eliminated the federal right to abortions in the U.S. <\/p>\n

Popstar Billie Eilish, the youngest headliner in Glastonbury history, introduced her song \u201cYour Power\u201d as she took the stage with her brother and fellow artist Finneas.<\/p>\n

\u201cToday is a really dark day for women in the U.S,\u201d Eilish told the audience at Worthy Farm. \u201cI\u2019m just gonna say that because I can\u2019t bear to think about it any longer in this moment. This song is dedicated to that I guess\u2026\u201d<\/p>\n

The \u201cOcean Eyes\u201d singer debuted her song \u2018\u2019TV\u2019\u2019 earlier this month which criticizes the internet\u2019s obsession with celebrity drama and ignorance of reproductive rights. <\/p>\n

\u201cThe internet\u2019s gone wild watching movie stars on trial \/ While they\u2019re overturning Roe v. Wade,\u201d read a lyric in the song referencing the defamation trial between Johnny Depp and Amber Heard.<\/p>\n

Indie-rock artist Phoebe Bridgers, who took the stage earlier, was also among the artists at Glastonbury condemning the Supreme Court\u2019s decision.\u00a0The \u201cMotion Sickness\u201d singer wore an all-black suit on stage as she spoke out about the court\u2019s ruling.<\/p>\n

Phoebe Bridgers performed hits such as \u201cScott Street\u201d and \u201cI Know the End\u201d during day three of Glastonbury Festival on June 24, 2022. Getty Images <\/p>\n

\u201cIn all honesty, [the festival] is like super surreal and fun. But I\u2019m having like the shittiest day ever,\u201d Bridgers admitted to the packed crowd.<\/p>\n

\u201cAre there any Americans here? Who wants to say f\u2014 the Supreme Court on three?\u201d <\/p>\n

The audience roared back as the Grammy-nominated artist led the chant: \u201cOne, two three\u2026 F\u2013 the Supreme Court! F\u2013 that shit. F\u2013 America\u2026 F\u2013 you.\u201d<\/p>\n

\u201cAll these old, irrelevant motherf\u2014kers trying to tell us what to do with our bodies,\u201d Bridgers continued as she strummed her guitar.<\/p>\n

Bridgers previously spoke out about abortion rights leading up to Friday\u2019s events. She revealed to fans on Twitter that she had an abortion last October and \u201ceveryone deserves that access\u201d following the leak of the Supreme Court\u2019s drafts.<\/p>\n
