    RingCentral: Who do we recommend it for?<\/h2>\n

    RingCentral is a reliable cloud-based phone system with effective tools for calls, messages and video conferences. The company boasts an uptime of 99.999%, which equates to only 78 seconds of interruption per quarter.<\/p>\n

    RingCentral’s core product is RingCentral MVP, which ranges in price from $19.99 to $49.99 per user per month if billed annually. RingCentral supports hundreds of meeting participants, hundreds of software integration and advanced features for monitoring calls in real time, whispering to your employees as they assist customers, and entering into conversations as needed.<\/p>\n

    We recommend RingCentral for businesses of any size looking for a powerful and collaborative phone system. Here are some of RingCentral’s distinguishing features:<\/p>\n

      \n
    • The ability to host audio conferences with up to 1,000 participants<\/li>\n
    • Great mobile app for iOS and Android devices<\/li>\n
    • Your own implementation specialist\u00a0<\/li>\n
    • Pre-configured IP desk phones (optional)<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

      Dialpad: Who do we recommend it for?<\/h2>\n

      Dialpad is a cloud-hosted communications platform designed for team and customer interactions across phone, chat and video. The company’s all-in-one business communications solution is available through three plans that range in price and features from $15 to $25 per user per month if billed annually.\u00a0<\/p>\n

      Dialpad’s phone system offers extensive calling and routing features. Its artificial intelligence (AI) technology can transcribe meetings in real time, automate post-call summaries, detect customer sentiment, log action items, and create to-do lists. All of Dialpad’s intelligent voice tools are designed to help companies make the most of every conversation and customer interaction. The company has contact center options with inbound and outbound support in addition to its outbound sales dialer offering.<\/p>\n

      We recommend Dialpad for small businesses that embrace new technology and place a strong emphasis on efficient systems. Here are some of Dialpad’s most attractive features:<\/p>\n

        \n
      • Very fast setup<\/li>\n
      • The ability to take and flip calls with any device<\/li>\n
      • Powerful voice analytics and automation<\/li>\n
      • Automatic data backup and redundancy<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

        Plans<\/h3>\n
          \n
        • RingCentral: RingCentral MVP offers four plans \u2013 Essentials, Standard, Premium and Ultimate \u2013 that start at $19.99 per user per month. However, businesses with more than 20 user accounts must start with the Standard plan. According to RingCentral, its Premium plan offers the best value.\n<\/li>\n
        • Dialpad: Dialpad Business Communications is available in three plans \u2013 Standard, Pro and Enterprise \u2013 that start at $15 per user per month.           Winner: RingCentral offers more variety and customization in its subscription plans, and it does not impose minimum seat requirements for its top offering.<\/p>\n

          Pricing<\/h3>\n
            \n
          • RingCentral: RingCentral MVP plans range in price from $19.99 to $49.99 per monthly user when billed annually. Monthly options are also available for an upcharge of 33%. Enterprise-level clients that purchase more than 100 licenses can receive an additional discount. RingCentral accepts debit cards and credit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express and Discover.<\/li>\n
          • Dialpad: Dialpad’s two business communications plans are priced at $15 and $25 per user per month when billed annually. It does not list the price of its Enterprise plan, which requires a minimum of 100 licenses. Dialpad offers discounted rates for annual subscribers and accepts all major credit cards.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

            Winner: Dialpad’s starting price and midlevel plans are both less expensive than similar offerings from RingCentral.<\/p>\n

            Features<\/h3>\n
              \n
            • RingCentral: RingCentral MVP has every basic VoIP calling feature you’d expect, including local numbers, call screening, extensions, call forwarding, call flip and visual voicemail. It also provides advanced features from the analytics portal, such as quality-of-service reports, live reports and meeting dashboards. You can hold unlimited video conference calls with up to 200 people or audio conferencing with up to 1,000 participants.<\/li>\n
            • Dialpad: Dialpad offers great basic features for custom call routing, toll-free numbers, HD calls, device switching, custom IVR and visual voicemail. Its advanced features include automatic post-call notes, call transcripts and speech coaching. With Dialpad’s voice intelligence software, you can automatically track keywords that come up, measure customer sentiment, and automate post-call summaries to better engage in conversations as they take place.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

                              Does Dialpad have a free version?<\/h3>\n

                              Dialpad offers free video meetings with 10 participants for up to 45 minutes per meeting. While the free version is limited compared to the $15-per-month Business option, it still offers features such as call recording, screen sharing, HD audio and chat. The free version also integrates with Salesforce, Slack, Intercom, Evernote, HubSpot, LinkedIn, Twitter, Google Calendar and Outlook Calendar.<\/p>\n

                              Does RingCentral have a free version?<\/h3>\n

Meeting participants can enjoy HD video and voice, virtual backdrops, closed captioning, and screen sharing.<\/p>\n
