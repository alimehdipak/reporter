There are many types of dentists, each with their own specific qualifications and skills. Some dentists specialize in a certain area of dentistry, such as oral surgery or restorative dentistry. Other dentists may focus on prevention and public health issues related to dental care.<\/span><\/p>\n

To ensure you receive the proper care, read about the different types of dentists and the services they offer below.<\/span><\/p>\n

Common Types of Dentistry<\/b><\/h2>\n

General Dentistry<\/b><\/h3>\n

A general dentistry practice provides dental care for people of all ages. <\/span>Patients typically visit a general dentistry office for routine checkups, oral surgery, and other treatments. General dentists often treat patients with conditions such as tooth decay, gum disease, and other oral health problems. <\/span><\/p>\n

In addition to treating patients’ teeth, general dentists may also provide dental services such as orthodontics and implants.<\/span><\/p>\n

Family Dentistry<\/b><\/h3>\n

Family dentists are trained to provide preventive, restorative, and cosmetic services. They are also trained to diagnose and treat gum disease, tooth decay, and other conditions.<\/span><\/p>\n

Cosmetic Dentistry<\/b><\/h3>\n

Cosmetic dentistry procedures include whitening teeth, bonding, veneers, crowns, dental implants<\/a>, and other procedures that can be done to improve a patient\u2019s smile. <\/span><\/p>\n

Some people may have a bad smile, and they can make improvements with cosmetic dentistry. There are different types of cosmetic dentistry procedures. Each procedure has its own benefits and drawbacks.<\/span><\/p>\n

6 Types of Dental Specialists<\/b><\/h2>\n

Periodontists<\/b><\/h3>\n

Periodontists, who are dental professionals whose specialty is treating periodontal disease, are in high demand. There are many reasons for this: people are living longer and having more chronic diseases, which can lead to tooth decay and gum disease; the increasing popularity of preventive dental care; and the increased awareness of the importance of good oral hygiene.<\/p>\n

Periodontists treat a wide range of diseases and conditions throughout the mouth, including gingivitis (inflammation of the gums), tooth loss, receding gums, bad breath, and leaking teeth. They use a variety of treatments to help their patients achieve optimal oral health. Periodontists may also provide guidance on regular dental care to prevent periodontal disease in the future.<\/p>\n

Prosthodontist<\/b><\/h3>\n

Prosthodontists are dentists who specialize in the restoration and modification of teeth. They can perform a wide range of procedures, from simple tooth cleaning to more complex restorations such as crowns<\/a> and bridges. <\/span><\/p>\n

Prosthodontists generally have a higher degree than regular dentists, and many also have additional training in specialty areas such as periodontics (the treatment of gingivitis and other oral health problems). As with any dental procedure, prosthodontists work with patients to create a personalized treatment plan that takes into account their individual needs.<\/span><\/p>\n

Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeons<\/b><\/h3>\n

Oral and maxillofacial surgeons are medical professionals who specialize in the diagnosis, treatment, and surgery of disorders of the oral cavity, jaw, and facial structures.<\/p>\n

These specialists are responsible for ensuring that patients have healthy mouths and faces, by performing a wide variety of procedures including oral surgeries, dental treatments, and reconstructive surgeries.<\/p>\n

Endodontists<\/b><\/h3>\n

Endodontists are special dental professionals who work with teeth and their surrounding gum tissue. They remove dental plaque and other harmful material from inside the teeth. <\/span><\/p>\n

If endodontists are unable to clean your teeth properly, you may be at risk for tooth decay and other oral health problems.<\/span><\/p>\n

Orthodontics<\/b><\/h3>\n

Orthodontics is a type of dental care that helps correct problems with the bite, alignment, and function of teeth. It is typically used to correct problems like crooked teeth, overcrowding, and overbites. orthodontists use various methods to help fix these issues. <\/span><\/p>\n

These may include braces, retainers, and Invisalign. Orthodontics can be an effective way to improve your smile and quality of life.<\/span><\/p>\n

Pediatric Dentists<\/b><\/h3>\n
