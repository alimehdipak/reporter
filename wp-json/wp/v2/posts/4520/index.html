Dr. Frank Bozekla, an emergency vet from Illinois, frequently shares pet parenting advice and other tales from the clinic for his more than 138,700 followers on TikTok. And though work as a first responder to animals can be stressful, it can also be silly \u2014 as he explained in a recent post about the \u201cweird pet names to yell in a busy animal hospital waiting room.\u201d<\/p>\n

In a skit that simulated his original reactions to the real-life names, Bozelka stood and peered around the room off-camera and asked for \u201cSergeant Sniffers.\u201d<\/p>\n
