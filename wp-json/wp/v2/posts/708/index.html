\u201cAmerican Idol\u201d contestant\u00a0Haley Slaton nabbed much more than a golden ticket on the singing competition.<\/p>\n

The singer, 23, auditioned for judges\u00a0Lionel Richie,\u00a0Katy Perry and\u00a0Luke Bryan during Sunday evening\u2019s episode, singing Miley Cyrus\u2019 2009 hit \u201cThe Climb.\u201d<\/p>\n

But it turns out Slaton had met her now-husband, Jordan Myles, during the first round of auditions \u2014 and even performed her solo for the panel of A-listers while she was five months pregnant, a feat she told host Ryan Seacrest was \u201cnot an obstacle\u201d for her.<\/p>\n

The couple \u201cgot married pretty quick\u201d on Christmas Eve, Slaton said in an interview with The Gazette in Cedar Rapids, Iowa, and she gave birth to a son, Jaelyn Amir Myles, in January.<\/p>\n

\u201cWe just knew. We started right off the bat talking to each other, and it ended up being an everyday thing,\u201d she said. \u201cI was already pregnant, and he stepped up to be a father, and I couldn\u2019t thank him enough for that,\u201d she continued.<\/p>\n

\u201cWe\u2019re very in love, we\u2019re very happy that we met, and I feel like another reason why I was on the show was to meet him,\u201d Slaton gushed about Myles, whose own audition for the ABC show has yet to air. \u201cThat was a great experience \u2014 to meet someone that I finally could spend the rest of my life with.\u201d<\/p>\n

But the audition process gave her \u2014 and hopefully her child \u2014 even more than that.<\/p>\n

\u201cJust being a mom shouldn\u2019t stop you from following your dreams,\u201d she continued during her conversation with Seacrest. \u201cI just want to do this for my son and give him a really good life. And I really want to show him to never stop chasing his dreams in the future.<\/p>\n

\u201cNever give up on your dreams. Mommy went and chased hers,\u201d she concluded.<\/p>\n

In Sunday\u2019s episode featuring Slaton, Perry and the others weren\u2019t too convinced about the Iowa native\u2019s choice of the Cyrus song, which was featured in 2009\u2019s \u201cHannah Montana: The Movie.\u201d<\/p>\n

\u201cWe\u2019re not sure,\u201d the \u201cI Kissed a Girl\u201d crooner said before asking\u00a0Slaton which song she would like to sing second. Slaton offered up Adele\u2019s \u201cOne and Only\u201d \u2014 which led Perry to implore the mom-to-be to sing her heart out and \u201cfight for it.\u201d <\/p>\n

She apparently did: Slaton\u2019s follow-up performance earned her a spot in the next round of the reality series.<\/p>\n

But that hasn\u2019t been the only happy moment for her or Myles, who shared a sweet family photo on Instagram last week of the trio snuggling up in bed. On Sunday, March 20, he also shared behind-the-scenes snapshots of Slaton in all her pregnant glory during her televised audition. <\/p>\n

\u201cProud husband \ud83e\udd70,\u201d he lovingly captioned the series of pics. <\/p>\n

Other contestants competing in this season of \u201cAmerican Idol\u201d include Kenedi Anderson, Emily Fath, Christian Guardino and Donavan Diaz.<\/p>\n
