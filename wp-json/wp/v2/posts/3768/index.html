It apparently couldn\u2019t keep up with the Kardashian.<\/p>\n

The historic dress once worn by Marilyn Monroe that Kim Kardashian showed off at the 2022 Met Gala appears to be damaged from its night out, according to an observer. <\/p>\n

The Marilyn Monroe Collection Instagram account posted new photos Monday showing the bedazzled dress, which Monroe wore to sing \u201cHappy Birthday Mr. President\u201d in 1962 to then-President John F. Kennedy. But the dress appeared to be \u201cmissing\u201d a few crystals, claimed the account, which added that others seemed to be \u201changing by a thread.\u201d <\/p>\n

Kardashian, 41, vowed to not make any alterations to the dress \u2014 declared a \u201cnational treasure\u201d to The Post by one noted Monroe expert \u2014 and altered herself<\/em> instead. She now infamously admitted that she lost 16 pounds in three weeks in order to squeeze into the dress.<\/p>\n

She was finally able to fit it over her famous derriere but was unable to zip it \u2014 hence why she draped herself with a white fur shawl while on the carpet. <\/p>\n

\u201cI tried it on and it didn\u2019t fit me,\u201d Kardashian\u00a0recalled on the red carpet\u00a0as she retold the tale of her first fitting. \u201cIt was such a challenge. It was like a role, I was determined to fit in it.\u201d<\/p>\n

The Post has reached out to representatives for Kardashian as well as Ripley\u2019s, where the dress is currently on display, for comment.<\/p>\n

In 2016, Ripley\u2019s Believe It or Not! Museum in Orlando bought the dress for $4.8 million. After taxes and fees, the total price was valued at over $5 million. Ripley\u2019s told The Post that the piece is the \u201cworld\u2019s most expensive dress\u201d ever sold at an auction. The dazzling gown is now valued at over $10 million.<\/p>\n

The reality star-turned-businesswoman only wore Monroe\u2019s artifact for a matter of minutes. She slipped into the gown in a dressing room near the Met Gala carpet and\u00a0changed into a replica gown\u00a0as soon as she made it up the steps.<\/p>\n

Kim Kardashian admitted that Monroe\u2019s dress did not fit when she first tired it on. Sipa USA via AP <\/p>\n

Some experts and collectors previously chastised Kardashian for wearing the 60-year-old garment, telling The Post that it is \u201cextremely fragile\u201d and that \u201canything could have happened to it.\u201d <\/p>\n

An expert also suggested to The Post that \u201csweat, perfume and makeup\u201d could have gotten onto the dress and caused \u201cuntold damage and staining,\u201d although Kardashian reportedly went makeup-free to the event. <\/p>\n
