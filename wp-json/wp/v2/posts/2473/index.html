1. LeBron James is one of the most well-known players in the NBA today. He has been named MVP several times, won 3 championships, and led his team to multiple Finals appearances. In addition to his dominance on the court, he has also worked hard at building his brand off of it and has had great success with endorsements and business ventures. LeBron is an incredible athlete who continues to impress us all with his skills on the basketball court.<\/p>\n

2. Kevin Durant is another top player in the league who has had a successful career thus far. He was drafted into the league straight from college and quickly became a starter for his team. He is known for his talent and has won multiple scoring titles as well as an NBA championship. Like LeBron, Durant also has a strong brand off the court and continues to be one of the top players in the league.<\/p>\n

3. Kawhi Leonard is a young player who has quickly shown that he belongs among the top players in the league today. In just a few years, he has had some great accomplishments including winning Finals MVP, Defensive Player of the Year, and 2 championships with his team. He shows incredible potential and it will be exciting to see what else he can achieve over his career.<\/p>\n

4. Steph Curry is another talented player who stands out from the rest due to his amazing shooting skills. His ability to hit 3-pointers from long range is unprecedented and has helped him win multiple MVP awards. In addition to his shooting, Curry is also a great playmaker and passer which makes him one of the most complete players in the league today.<\/p>\n

5. Russell Westbrook is an explosive player who is known for his high energy level on the court. He is a great scorer and rebounder and has won multiple MVP awards throughout his career. Westbrook also has a strong fashion sense off the court and is often seen rocking the latest trends.<\/p>\n

6. James Harden is another top player in the league who is known for his scoring ability. He has won multiple scoring titles and was named MVP in 2018. Harden is also a good playmaker and passer and often finds ways to get his teammates involved in the game.<\/p>\n

7. Anthony Davis is a young player who has already shown that he is one of the best in the league. He is an incredible athlete with a unique skill set that includes shot-blocking and rebounding. Davis has won multiple MVP awards and is poised for more success in the future.<\/p>\n

8. Damian Lillard is a great point guard who is known for his scoring ability. He has won multiple MVP awards and led his team to the playoffs numerous times. Lillard is also a good playmaker and often finds ways to get his teammates involved in the game.<\/p>\n

9. Kristaps Porzingis is a tall player who stands out due to his size and shooting ability. He is a great rebounder and shot-blocker, which makes him one of the top players in the league today. In addition to his skills on the court, he also has good fashion sense off of it and often rocks some unique outfits.<\/p>\n
