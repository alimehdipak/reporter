It was a proposal fit for the Gilded Age!<\/p>\n

Laurie Cumbo, commissioner of New York City\u2019s Department of Cultural Affairs, got proposed to by her partner \u2014 former NY State Assembly candidate Bobby Digi Olisa \u2014 at one of the most fashionable events of the year. <\/p>\n

The pair gave Kim Kardashian\u2019s skin-tight Marilyn Monroe gown a run for its scene-stealing money as they made their way down the infamous Metropolitan Museum of Art steps at Monday evening\u2019s 2022 Met Gala.<\/p>\n

Cumbo and Olisa, both 47, were at the bash when the latter got down on one knee and presented his love with an unmistakable robin\u2019s egg-colored Tiffany\u2019s ring box.<\/p>\n

Cumbo and Olisa \u2014\u00a0executive director and founder of Staten Island\u2019s the Canvas institute \u2014 share a 5-year-old son and have been together for almost eight years.<\/p>\n

\u201cI guess my fianc\u00e9 just asked me to marry him and this is amazing, unexpected, wonderful. This is the best day ever!\u201d Cumbo gushed to NBC after the surprise, which even hit the pages of fashion bible Vogue.<\/p>\n

She also told the outlet that she was previously an intern for Met Gala decades before \u2014 when she was 15 years old. \u201cNow I\u2019m coming back as the Commissioner of the New York City Department of Cultural Affairs and it\u2019s an honor to be here tonight,\u201d she added.<\/p>\n

Cumbo was in for a big surprise when her partner of eight years proposed to her on Monday evening. AFP via Getty Images \t \t <\/p>\n

Cumbo was appointed by Mayor Eric Adams in March 2022. Adams, 61, also attended the star-studded Gilded Age-themed party, rocking a custom-made suit that pleaded \u201cEnd Gun Violence.\u201d<\/p>\n

She also stopped by to talk to Entertainment Tonight about the proposal\u00a0at the museum and noted she wasn\u2019t expecting it.<\/p>\n

\u201cI didn\u2019t know it was going to happen tonight. We\u2019ve been talking about it,\u201d she said, also stating that she and her man have been through several hurdles as a couple so \u201cthis is such an honor and this is such a blessing.\u201d \u00a0<\/p>\n

\u201cI didn\u2019t know it was going to happen tonight. We\u2019ve been talking about it,\u201d she said.AFP via Getty Images <\/p>\n

Olisa later divulged to the Associated Press that he had been wanting to pop the question for quite some time. <\/p>\n

\u201cYou know, it\u2019s always like trying to wait for the right time,\u201d he said. \u201cSo when is the right time considering everything that\u2019s going on worldwide? So I was just like, \u2018Today we\u2019re going to make it happen.\u2019 \u201c<\/p>\n

Cumbo was definitely impressed by Olisa\u2019s grand romantic gesture: \u201cYou wait for this moment your whole life almost and for it to be right here, it was everything that I thought it would be,\u201d she said.<\/p>\n
