Kevin Spacey is losing another paycheck.<\/p>\n

The Oscar winner, 62, will no longer star in \u201c1242: Gateway to the West,\u201d a film about Mongolian warrior Genghis Khan, according to a Variety report.<\/p>\n

The news follows Spacey pleading not guilty to sexual assault and other charges Thursday. Producer Bill Chamberlain told the publication that the project will be replacing him due to the charges.<\/p>\n

The historical drama was shopped around at Cannes with Spacey attached to act.<\/p>\n
