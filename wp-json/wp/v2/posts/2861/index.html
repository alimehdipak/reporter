Definition of mock-up<\/p>\n

1 : a full-sized structural model built to scale chiefly for study, testing, or display. 2 : a working sample (as of a magazine) for reviewing format, layout, or content.<\/p>\n

What is difference between mockup and prototype?<\/h3>\n
