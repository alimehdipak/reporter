This home is timeless on TV, but being reduced to rubble in the material world.\u00a0<\/p>\n

Demolition has begun at the shooting location of the Australian sitcom \u201cKath & Kim.\u201d While the two-story house is located in the made-up suburb of Fountain Lakes on the comedy series \u2014 which ran from 2002 to 2007 \u2014 it was actually located at 4 Lagoon Place, Patterson Lakes in southeast Melbourne, the Guardian reported.\u00a0<\/p>\n

Construction workers have already started bulldozing the storied property, where the characters Kath Day-Knight and her daughter, Kim, lived out four seasons of shenanigans for a rapt audience down under, where it reached a \u201cFriends\u201d-level caliber of fame. The company Dig Dig Demolition confirmed to Guardian Australia that the four-bedroom is actively in the process of being knocked down.\u00a0<\/p>\n

\u201cWe\u2019re in the iconic \u2018Kath & Kim\u2019 house, it\u2019s nice, it\u2019s different, it\u2019s unusual, this is the living room, where the foxy ladies would sit and watch TV,\u201d says reporter Brendan Crew in a walk-through of the partially disassembled home posted to social media on Monday. \u201cSo many TV memories. All being demolished to make way for a new house.\u201d\u00a0<\/p>\n

American audiences didn\u2019t take to a US adaption of the sitcom, which was a smash hit in Australia.Facebook\/ Kath and Kim A local reporter recently walked through the partially demolished home.Twitter\/ @10NewsFirstMelb <\/p>\n

The property sold for $1.48 million in November 2016, almost a decade after the show finished airing, according to the Guardian.\u00a0<\/p>\n

News of the home\u2019s demolition came the same week rumors began swirling the series would be get a reboot some two decades since its original run wrapped.\u00a0<\/p>\n

\u201cIt was unimaginable neighbors could keep TV\u2019s biggest secret under wraps much longer,\u201d an insider told the magazine Woman\u2019s Day, adding that an official announcement will be made before the end of the month, the Daily Mail reported.\u00a0<\/p>\n

Show actress Magda Szubanski, however, has said as recently as last May that she doesn\u2019t agree with the idea or rebooting the beloved boob tube hit.<\/p>\n
