<\/p>\n

Would you use an iPhone feature that allows you to hear what your friends say about you when you walk away from the table?<\/p>\n

One woman did \u2014 and says it\u2019s a \u201cwonderful little hack.\u201d<\/p>\n

TikTok user Michelle Guo @woahguo shared her experience activating the hidden feature.<\/p>\n

\u201cThinking about the time I turned on \u2018live listen\u2019 on my iPhone & left my phone on the table & walked away with my AirPods in so that I could listen to what everyone said without me being in the room,\u201d she wrote in a video.<\/p>\n

Live Listen was introduced in 2014 as a way for iPhone users with hearing aids to hear others more easily in noisy environments.<\/p>\n

The setting allows an iPhone or iPad to act as a microphone, sending sound to the hearing aids.<\/p>\n

Originally, it only worked with iPhone-compatible hearing aids but has since expanded to AirPods.<\/p>\n

That\u2019s how Guo was able to use it in a room with her friends.<\/p>\n

Guo learned of the setting from the Apple website, she told the Daily Dot.<\/p>\n

TikTok user Michelle Guo shared her experience activating the feature.TikTok <\/p>\n

She said she tested it out with her friends, which led to the TikTok video.<\/p>\n

Guo told the outlet she posted about the setting to spread awareness.<\/p>\n

The video has garnered nearly 725,000 views.<\/p>\n

Hundreds of people have commented asking Guo how she activated the hidden setting.<\/p>\n

She responded to many by writing: \u201cSettings > Control Center > add Hearing > connect AirPods > turn on Live Listen > place phone down & walk away & listen.\u201d<\/p>\n

Guo also posted a follow-up comment explaining her specific reasoning for using the setting.<\/p>\n

\u201cSTORYTIME: The only time I did this was when I tested it out with my friends after we read about it online \u2026 USE RESPONSIBLY!!!!,\u201d Guo wrote.<\/p>\n

Some users were shocked to learn about the setting, while others shared feeling nervous about what they\u2019d learn from using the setting while with friends.<\/p>\n

\u201cI\u2019m scared of what they will say,\u201d one user wrote.<\/p>\n

\u201cI did smth similar in elementary school, found out my friends were talking abt me and dropped them,\u201d said another.<\/p>\n

Guo\u2019s video has racked up more than 720,000 views.Getty Images\/iStockphoto <\/p>\n

\u201cI did it once and I will not do it again,\u201d another user wrote.<\/p>\n
