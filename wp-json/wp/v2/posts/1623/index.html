A big victory resulted in a small (but significant) move for Alexander Volkanovski.<\/p>\n

The UFC men’s featherweight champion had his way with Chan Sung Jung on Saturday in their UFC 273 title fight, outstriking the South Korean by 152-51 on his way to a one-sided fourth-round TKO. The win, Volkanovski’s 21st in a row and his third title defense, boosted him up one spot into the top three of the latest ESPN pound-for-pound rankings.<\/p>\n

The winner of the UFC 273 co-main event, men’s bantamweight champ Aljamain Sterling, moved into the top-10 for the first time, and the former champ he defeated, Petr Yan, dropped out.<\/p>\n

The women’s rankings remain unchanged, but with several top-10 women now scheduled for their next bouts, we soon might see some movement.<\/p>\n
