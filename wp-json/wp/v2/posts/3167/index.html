Describing how she\u2019d been \u201cslut shamed,\u201d Amelia explained: \u201cA male flight attendant just told me to cover up and my clothing was too revealing.<\/p>\n

\u201cI can\u2019t wear this and it\u2019s inappropriate to wear on JetStar. NEVER heard of this.\u201d<\/p>\n

Sharing a photo of herself in her seat, she joked: \u201cBabe, crop tops are my BRANDDDDD xoxoxo.\u201d<\/p>\n

Following the incident, Amelia then tried to look up their policy online and could only find a rule about \u201coffensive clothing\u201d.<\/p>\n
