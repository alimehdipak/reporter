Facebook users are being locked out of their accounts for failing to respond to a message some mistook for spam.<\/p>\n

People who realized the warning is actually real have since tried to follow the social network\u2019s instructions \u2014 but an issue is apparently preventing them from regaining access.<\/p>\n

A notice was sent to selected accounts deemed at particular risk from hackers, telling them to enable an enhanced security feature called Facebook Protect.<\/p>\n

Many were given a March 17 deadline to set it up themselves or face having their account locked until it\u2019s done.<\/p>\n

It\u2019s possible some thought the message was a scam, so ignored it.<\/p>\n

The unusual email address used, security@facebookmail.com, probably didn\u2019t help matters.<\/p>\n

After the deadline passed, users were told they had to switch on Facebook Protect to get in.<\/p>\n

However, those who went ahead with it claim the setup system is broken \u2014 meaning they are still locked out.<\/p>\n

Facebook users lost access to their accounts and many were unable to fix the issue.AFP via Getty Images <\/p>\n

Apparently, the authentication tools \u2014 such as a text message to prove it\u2019s you \u2014 are not working.<\/p>\n

\u201cI got locked out from Facebook indefinitely today because I didn\u2019t respond to emails from FB (that looked like a scam) about its new Facebook Protect system, which I was required to enable by today,\u201d one user\u00a0said.<\/p>\n

\u201cSo far, the text and security key options don\u2019t work, many report.\u201d<\/p>\n

Another complained on\u00a0Twitter, saying: \u201cThis Facebook Protect thing is so annoying because it\u2019s not letting me turn it on and I need Facebook for work so I\u2019m really hoping Facebook fixes the stupid code.\u201d<\/p>\n

Facebook Protect is a special monitoring program for people at heightened risk of being targeted by hackers.<\/p>\n

This includes those working in human rights, journalists and government officials.<\/p>\n

Facebook owner Meta has been approached by the Sun for comment.<\/p>\n
