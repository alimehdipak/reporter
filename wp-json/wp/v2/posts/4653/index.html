A British teen left with horrific burns on more than half of her body has become an unexpected beauty influencer on TikTok, inspiring her vast, new following.<\/p>\n

Aleema Ali, 18, has clocked up more than 780,000 followers on the social media app, six years after she caught fire while walking through her family\u2019s kitchen with flammable head-lice treatment in her hair. <\/p>\n

Ali \u2014 who was 12 years old at the time \u2014 was left in a coma for five weeks following the fire. Doctors feared the youngster would die due to the severity of her burns, but she miraculously managed to pull through. <\/p>\n

\u201cThe only thing I was thinking was, \u2018I\u2019m going to die,\u2019 \u201d Ali recalled in a new interview with Caters. \u201cHow can somebody make it out after such a big accident?\u201d <\/p>\n

Ali suffered burns to her face, leaving her with little self-confidence. Then the teen discovered social media beauty tutorials, with cosmetic tips and tricks helping her to feel more beautiful. <\/p>\n

She subsequently decided to start her own TikTok beauty account as a creative outlet and has unexpectedly become a source of makeup wisdom for hundreds of thousands of fawning fans.<\/p>\n

While walking through the kitchen, where her mom was making dinner, Ali\u2019s hair came into close proximity of a cooker, which turned the tween into a \u201chuman fireball.\u201d<\/p>\n

After her nine-month hospital stint, and with her appearance drastically altered, the high school student became interested in makeup as a means of helping her feel more beautiful. Over the years, she has become a cosmetics connoisseur, and she is now sharing that wisdom on TikTok. <\/p>\n
