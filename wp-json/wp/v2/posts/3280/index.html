Counter culture<\/h2>\n

Unsubscribed has just launched its first flagship, at 1190 Madison Ave. <\/p>\n

With its ethos of \u201cslow fashion,\u201d American Eagle Outfitters\u2019 new eco-friendly offshoot, Unsubscribed, has quickly built a robust list of fans \u2014 including Hilaria Baldwin. Launched online less than a year ago, the elevated basics brand has now opened the doors of its first flagship at 1190 Madison Ave. Alongside stylishly sustainable offerings, you\u2019ll find lounge-inspired spaces that encourage shoppers to relax and indulge in a little socially responsible retail therapy.\u00a0<\/p>\n

Island style<\/h2>\n

\u201cNew York\u201d T-shirt, $30 at Mango, 711 Fifth Ave. <\/p>\n

Cross the threshold of Mango\u2019s new boutique at 711 Fifth Ave. and you\u2019ll find yourself transported straight to the Mediterranean. A light-filled atrium gives way to three floors (and nearly 23,000 square feet) of the Barcelona-based brand\u2019s signature colorful styles. Refresh your summer wardrobe with straw totes and prints that pop, or recycle past-season pieces at the eco-conscious store\u2019s \u201cCommitted Boxes,\u201d where cast-off garments and footwear are collected to be reimagined anew.\u00a0<\/p>\n

In bloom<\/h2>\n

Jonathan Cohen\u2019s new Madison Avenue pop-up.Evgeny Popov <\/p>\n
