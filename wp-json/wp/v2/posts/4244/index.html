These pampered pooches certainly aren\u2019t living the ruff life.<\/p>\n

Style mavens hoping to add a dash of couture to the lives of their spoiled brats now can turn their fur babies into fashionistas with Gucci\u2019s pet collection \u2014 if not bowled over by the barking mad prices.<\/p>\n

The 2022 Gucci Pet Collection offers a $7,500 made-to-order pet bed for the fleabag who prefers to lounge in a French-inspired chaise, a $460 poop bag holder and $500 leash. <\/p>\n

Other items include $220 embellished AirTag holders, $895 bowl covers, $4,050 pet carriers with the iconic Gucci house monogram and a \u201ctravel bowl set\u201d with a hard-sided case emblazoned with the \u201cGG\u201d logo and priced at $4,250. <\/p>\n

Studded, bedazzled and adorned with plumage, there\u2019s also a whole line of apparel from T-shirts to hats for animal fanatics to mix and match.<\/p>\n

This corgi seems perfectly comfortable in his $7,500 made-to-order bed.Gucci \t \t <\/p>\n

The Italian luxury house headed by Alessandro Michele released a whimsical ad campaign this week with a wide range of domesticated cutie-patootie pups \u2014 basset hounds, corgis and dachshunds make cameos \u2014 rocking the new line, offered in a variety of materials including plain leather, canvas, web stripe and herbarium print. <\/p>\n

Not to be outdone by the canines, some fancy felines even sport their Gucci best in the video, captured by Max Siedentopf.<\/p>\n

In fact, Siedentopf was so smitten with one of the pups in the campaign that he gave it a fur-ever home.<\/p>\n

\u201cVery happy to share on my birthday the new campaign for Gucci\u2019s first pet collection where I got to meet little Lucio who changed my life forever,\u201d the artist wrote on Instagram, with photos showing his new cuddle buddy dachshund.<\/p>\n<\/p>\n

\u201cContinuing the narrative of surprise and delight that defines the Gucci Lifestyle selection, the Gucci Pet Collection infuses the everyday with a magical aura,\u201d the products\u2019 landing page description reads. \u201cUnveiled in a colorful, retro-inspired campaign, the pieces allow each animal\u2019s personality to shine as an ode to the Creative Director\u2019s vision of individuality.\u201d<\/p>\n

This King Charles Spaniel seems thirsty for her bowl, which retails for $4,250.Gucci Tr\u00e8s chic! This Weimaraner is rocking a $500 spiked collar.Gucci <\/p>\n

Along with the craftmanship expected with Gucci\u2019s high-end luxury goods offerings, the pet collection is \u201ccrafted from animal-free raw materials that are primarily from sustainable, renewable and bio-based sources\u201d \u2014 so you know Fido won\u2019t feel sheepish about his eco-conscious duds.<\/p>\n

The foray into the pet market is a first for Gucci \u2014 but not a first in fashion.
