The 65 best bodysuits of 2022 that are worth your coin

Posted on 2022


New York Post may be compensated and/or receive an affiliate commission if you buy through our links.

For every layering and styling need, the classic bodysuit never ceases to amaze. Everything just looks more put together when you pair this convenient wardrobe essential with a pair of jeans, paperbag shorts or wide-leg pants.

Who would have thought erasing the need to tuck — and retuck — would be the modern-day game-changer?

Not to mention, there’s virtually a style fit for everyone, with nearly every floral, puff-sleeved and full-on sequined top coming in a bodysuit version.

To further drive home the “we-love-bodysuits!” train, it’s crucial to have a styling vision. Picture a simple black bodysuit with your best high-waisted shorts, or Spanx’s cult-favorite faux leather leggings for a going-out look — or an off-the-shoulder pastel floral bodysuit with cropped shorts for a mod picnic.

Whatever the occasion, there’s a bodysuit for it.

To give you the tip creme da la creme, we curated the 65 best options to shop below. From classic whites and blacks to lace, shapewear and more, you’ll surely have your shopping fix without going in stores.

Additionally, click to visit some of our favorite destinations for bodysuits: Amazon, Abercrombie, Anthropologie, Lulus, Revolve.
