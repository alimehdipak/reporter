48 best luxury gifts for women: spoil her this Mother’s Day

Posted on 2022


New York Post may be compensated and/or receive an affiliate commission if you buy through our links.

While there are certainly thousands of budget-friendly gifts on the market, one of the best parts about Mother’s Day is getting the chance to spoil your Mom with a splurge item they might never buy themselves.

Whether you’re shopping for mom, grandmother, aunt, best friend or others, we’ve rounded up over 45 different gift options that any mother-figure in your life is bound to enjoy on her big day — and perhaps you’ll reap the benefits, too!

Our list includes a present for every type of mother — whether she’d love a luxurious handbag from Yves Saint Laurent, a virtual cooking class with an International chef, or an Apple iWatch.

Ahead find a wide variety of gifts for her from brands like Burberry, Casper, Chanel, David Yurman, Facebook and more.

Nap Loungwear

If she’s a fan of loungewear (who isn’t?) then you can’t go wrong with this ultra-comfy cashmere rib-knit cardigan. It comes in dozens of colors!

Janessa Leoné

This Janessa Leonè hat will not only protect your mom’s face from the sun but she’ll look fabulous too.

Venus Et Fleuremma holley

If your mom adores flowers, gift them the best of the best: an arrangement from Venus Et Fleur. The brand is known for their gorgeous florals that last for 365 days or longer.

Casper

Weighted blankets have become popular over the years for their anxiety-reducing effects. Research has shown that sleeping under weight brings a better sleep overall — leaving you feeling rested and revitalized.

Others have said that weighted blankets have even helped decrease their anxiety and stress. This one created by Casper is extra special since it’s made of nothing but biodegradable tree fabric that is naturally cooling, perfect for hot sleepers.

Lunya

A high-end silk robe is another great gift. This one from Lunya is made of 100% silk, oversized, can be machine washed (but make sure to air dry) and comes in 10 different colors including white, blue, green, black and more.

Saks Fifth Avenue

This fashionable Gucci watch will definitely put a smile on her face, no matter what occasion you’re shopping for.

If the mom you’re shopping for takes her skincare routine seriously, we recommend gifting her a Foreo Luna 3 tool. The device lifts away dirt and oil within one minute and gives a targeted firming facial massage that leaves your skin looking younger and smoother.

CarawayCaraway

If she loves spending time in the kitchen and cooking all of her favorite recipes, consider gifting her a new set of cookware from Caraway. This set includes four non-stick pans that come in five different colors. The best part? The set is currently $100 off! Check out our standalone review on the brand for more information.

Bloomingdales

What’s better than a sleek, new water bottle? A water bottle that cleans itself! The Larq self-cleaning water bottle may be on the pricier side, but it’s worth it if it means one less item to clean in the sink. It uses UV-C LED light to eliminate up to 99.9% of bio-contaminants from your water and bottle. It’s available in both 17-ounce and 25-ounce sizes, and comes in two different colors.

AIRE Ancient Baths

Give the true gift of luxury this year: a relaxing day at the spa. AIRE Ancient Baths has locations all over the world including New York, Chicago, Copenhagen, Barcelona, London and more.

Gorjana

This diamond link necklace from Gorjana is the perfect gift for mom. It would look great layered with her other favorite necklaces or on its own in the spotlight.

Meta Quest

The Facebook Oculus Quest 2 goggles allow users to access VR (virtual reality) communities, play games and connect with their real-life friends through Facebook.

Saks Fifth Avenue

You can’t go wrong with a classic David Yurman cable bracelet. They come in a variety of different sizes and colors but we especially like the smaller size since it can easily be stacked with other pieces.

MasterClassMasterclass

Some prefer experiences, not more things. The MasterClass all-access pass is great for the man in your life who’s always eager to learn new things. They offer classes in a variety of different areas, including cooking, music, writing, sports, communication and more.

Facebook

The Portal Go by Facebook is a video calling device that allows you to connect with your friends and family through video chat (even if they don’t have a Portal themselves). It’s available in both white and black and has a 10-inch screen.

Saks Fifth Avenue

Any fashionista would appreciate this beautiful Chloé Canvas Tote for a day out on the town in the spring or summer — or even a winter trip to Miami.

Amazon

If your gal hasn’t already cut the cord, consider buying them a pair of the new Apple AirPods Pro. They’re noise-canceling, can be customized to the size of their ear, connect easily to Bluetooth and have up to 4.5 hours of listening time.

The Chef & The Dish

Have her cook around the world without even stepping foot outside of her house! The Chef & The Dish is a company that offers virtual (luxury) cooking classes with International chefs around the globe.

Away

There’s always an excuse to upgrade her luggage. Not only is the large-size suitcase from Away Luggage super-spacious and convenient, but the fancy aluminum exterior is extra unique as well.

It’s durable enough to handle the hustle and bustle of baggage handlers and its 360-degree spinner wheels make it super easy to glide along any platform. For more information on the brand, check out our standalone review.

Amazon

The new Apple Series 7 iWatch is a great gift for the mom who is always trying to make her health a priority. It can track fitness, sleep, blood oxygen and so much more.

Ulta Beauty

When in doubt, the luxurious mama bear is bound to love a high-end perfume. Chanel Chance is extremely well-loved and has a delicate floral-fruity fragrance that will add a dash of femininity wherever she goes. It’s available in a 1.7- and 3.4-ounce bottle.

Amazon

Help your special lady keep her teeth in tiptop shape in the new year with this Philips Sonicare DiamondClean Toothbrush that has over 3,100 positive reviews on Amazon and an impressive 4.6-star rating.

Saks Fifth Avenue

Everyone should have a least one fancy watch in their collection. This beautiful Michele watch features diamonds, a mother-of-pearl face and a stainless steel band.

Brooklinen

Make sure she’s extra comfy in bed this winter with this luxurious sheet set from Brooklinen. It comes with one fitted sheet, one flat sheet and two pillowcases. You can choose between 13 different shades and colors and the sheets are available in twin, twin XL, full, queen, king and California king.

Saks Fifth Avenue

If mom appreciates fashion, then she definitely won’t be disappointed when she opens these Stuart Weitzman black leather boots. They’re made with leather and stretchy gabardine on the back plus a small, chunky block heel to give a bit more height.

AmazonAmazon

The Sony Noise-Canceling Headphones are a great option if they like more of an over-the-ear option. They’re an Amazon No. 1 bestseller and have over 27,000 positive reviews. These Sony headphones offer up to 30 hours of battery life and have a touch sensor to control pausing, playing and skipping various tracks.

Williams Sonoma

Spoil her with a fancy Breville Expresso coffee machine this year. This model can make almost every warm coffee beverage under the sun including cappuccinos, lattes, Americanos, mochas, espressos and macchiatos. It also comes with a milk frother and an internal grinder.

Anthropologie

If she’s obsessed with candles, she’s definitely had her eye on Anthropologie’s jumbo candle.

Saks Fifth Avenue

She’ll totally love and appreciate this YSL black leather clutch for her next date night.

Amazon

An Apple TV HD is a great gift if they’re a big movie buff or enjoy spending their spare time binge-watching the latest shows. While there are many streaming devices on the market (for lower price points) the Apple TV has the most apps available of any streaming platform, including Netflix, Disney+, Hulu and more. Plus, if you’re an iPhone user, it enables AirPlay, which means you can wirelessly mirror your screen and share your content on the TV.

Net-A-Porter

Add a pop of color to her space with a fun and bright Assouline coffee table book.

Saks Fifth Avenue

If she appreciates a glass of champagne now and then, consider gifting her this set of gorgeous flutes by Baccarat.

Tempo

If she’s really into fitness and can’t go to the gym as of late, have her bring a personal trainer right to her living room with Tempo. The at-home studio experience gives expert coaching on a variety of different exercises, offers dozens of classes and even comes with weights.

Tory Burch

These Tory Burch leather gloves will make an awesome addition to her winter wardrobe this year.

Saks Fifth Avenue

Any gal would be lucky to receive this trendy YSL hobo bag for Mother’s Day 2022.

Net-A-Porter

Add a little sass to her pajama collection with this fun option from SLEEPER that features feather trim.

GoldBelly

Calling all foodies! You can’t go wrong with this classy charcuterie kit from GoldBelly if she loves to eat yummy treats. It comes with a variety of different meats and spreads.

Amazon

If your mom loves to cook, she likely already owns one of the popular Le Creuset Cast Iron Dutch Ovens but if not, we highly recommend you gift her one for Mother’s Day. They’re great for creating stews, soups, sauces and more. The best part? Amazon is currently slashing the price of this 7.25-quart option by 20 percent.

lululemon

Consider gifting her the cult-favorite lululemon Define jacket for the mom who loves to workout or wants a light jacket to run around town in. Available in multiple colors and sizes.

Rothy’s

Pretty much everything at Rothy’s is Mother’s Day approved. This tote bag comes in a variety of colors and will take her from the office to the beach in no time.

Baublebar

Harry & David

If you’re unable to deliver your Mother’s Day gift yourself, your Mom will surely love a fancy gift basket like this one that features chocolate, fruit, and most importantly, wine.

Amazon

If your mom loves to read and doesn’t mind using an e-reader (which is way more convenient, if you ask us!) she’ll totally adore receiving the brand new Amazon Paperwhite for Mother’s Day.

Our Place

Click & Grow

iRobot Roomba 694 Robot VacuumAmazon
