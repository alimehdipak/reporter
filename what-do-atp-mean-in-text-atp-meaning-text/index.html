ATP Meaning Text – As the primary source of energy for cellular processes, the ATP molecule is often called the “energy currency of the cell”. It consists of three phosphate groups, adenosine, and ribose. A high-energy bond is formed between the third phosphate group and the ribose sugar by substrate-level phosphorylation, a process that requires energy input.
